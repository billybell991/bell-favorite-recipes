## Ingredients

- 5-6 boneless skinless chicken thighs patted dry
- 1 teaspoongarlic powder
- 1 teaspoonsmoked paprika
- ½ teaspoonoregano
- ½ teaspoonsalt
- ¼ teaspoonblack pepper

## Instructions

1. Pat chicken thighs dry with a paper towel, then transfer to a bowl.
2. Combine all the seasonings together in a bowl, then pour them into the bowl with the chicken and use your hands to coat the chicken with the spices.
3. Preheat your air fryer to 380° (mine took 3 minutes to preheat). Then, place chicken smooth side down into the air fryer, spray with nonstick spray/olive oil spray, and cook at 380° for 12 minutes. NOTE: every air fryer is different, so cook times may vary.
4. Once the air fryer beeps, use kitchen tongs to flip the chicken over, and cook for an additional 6-8 minutes on the second side, or until chicken is cooked through.  (Billy's note:  I only put them in for 4 additional mins instead of 6-8.  I think they'd have been too dry with that, so gauge them when you flip them.)
