## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. Boneless Skinless Chicken Thighs (approximately 4 thighs)
- 2 large Eggs
- 2/3 cup All Purpose Flour
- 2/3 cup Cornstarch
- 1 cup Panko Breadcrumbs
- 1 teaspoon Paprika
- 1 teaspoon Salt
- 1/2 teaspoon Pepper
- 1/2 tablespoon Garlic Powder
- 1/2 tablespoon Onion Powder

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, mix flour, cornstarch, and spices together.
2. In a separate small bowl, whisk eggs.
3. In a third bowl large enough to dip a whole chicken thigh, pour in the Panko bread crumbs.
4. Coat a piece of chicken in flour mixture, then dredge in the egg wash, and finish coating it with the panko crumbs. Place on a clean solid surface, such as a plate or cutting board.
5. Repeat for each piece of chicken.
6. Pre-heat the air fryer to 350 degrees F. (1-2 minutes depending on your air fryer model).
7. Spray your air fryer basked and chicken thighs with olive oil spray. The main reason for nonstick cooking spray is it will help the chicken thighs crisp up more and keep them from sticking to the basket.
8. Working in batches, place the thighs in the air fryer basket in a single layer.
9. Air fry the chicken on 350F for 12 minutes. Use tongs to flip chicken and air fry for another 3-5 minutes to make sure all sides of the chicken are nice and crisp. Check the chicken with a meat thermometer to make sure the internal temperature reaches 165F.
