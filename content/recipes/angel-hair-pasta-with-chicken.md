## Ingredients

- 2 tbsp olive oil, divided
- 2 boneless chicken breasts, cubed
- 1 carrot, sliced diagonally
- 1 cup broccoli florets
- 2 cloves of garlic, minced
- 12 oz angel hair pasta
- 2/3 cup chicken broth
- 1/4 cup parmesan cheese
- 1 tsp dried basil

## Instructions

Heat 1 tbsp of oil in a medium sized skillet over medium heat.  Add chicken and cook until cooked through, about 5 minutes. Remove from skillet and drain on paper towel.

Heat remaining oil in same skillet. Begin heating water for pasta. Add carrots to skillet, stir while cooking for 4 minutes.  Add broccoli and garlic to skillet, stir while cooking for 2 minutes longer.

Cook pasta according to package directions. While pasta is cooking, add chicken broth, basil and parmesan to skillet.  Stir to combine. Return chicken to skillet. Reduce heat and simmer for 4 minutes.

Drain pasta and place in a large serving bowl. Top with chicken and vegetable mixture. Serve immediately.
