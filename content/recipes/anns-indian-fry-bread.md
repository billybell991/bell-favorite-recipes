## Ingredients

- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 heaping Tbsp baking powder
- ½ tsp table salt
- water to mix and oil or lard to fry in

## Instructions

METHOD:  In a large bowl combine all dry ingredients. Then slowly add cold water as needed. The texture is a thick texture, thicker than pancake batter. Mix well.

Flour the area where you will be rolling out the dough. Drop the whole dough chunk onto flour, and freely sprinkle more flour on this dough. Begin to knead as though making bread, making sure dough is not sticking to your hands. If that happens, just ad a little more flour.

Now roll out dough till about ½” thick, and cut into 2 . 2” squares. Tear holes in each piece – this allows for even heat when frying. Prepare your frying pan with oil – oil should be at least ½” deep. Temperature should be med/hot – deep frying temperature. Drop each piece one by one till evenly distributed in oil.  Use thongs to turn bread over when cooking and fry till each side is golden brown. TIP: When taking bread out of hot pan, stand wach bread up to allow for any drippings. Do this in a pan with paper towel at bottom of pan. ENJOY!
