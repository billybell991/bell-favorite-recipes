Preheat oven to 350 degrees.

Mix together:

## Ingredients

- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 1 cup rolled oats
- 1 cup lightly-packed brown sugar
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon

## Instructions

Cut in ½ cup butter or margarine.  Set aside.

Wash, peel and slice apples to make 4 cups.

Mix about ¼ of the crumb mixture with the apples and spread in a buttered 8” x 12” baking dish. Cover with remaining crumbs and pat down slightly.

Bake in the preheated oven for 35 to 40 minutes or until apples are tender.

Serve hot or cold, with cream.

Yield: 6 to 8 servings.

VARIATION: QUICK APPLE CRISP Prepare as directed above, replacing sliced apples with a 20-oz can of apple pie filling. Bake in a preheated 350 degree oven for 25 to 30 minutes or until golden brown.
