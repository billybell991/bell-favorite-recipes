Submitted by Heather Bell

Bride

- 32 Ritz crackers, crushed
- 1 ½ cups brown sugar
- ¼ cup flour
- ¼ tsp cinnamon
- ½ cup butter
- 4 cups chopped apples

Mix all ingredients together except for the apples. Place half of the apples on the bottom of a baking dish. Add half of Ritz mixture on top of apples. Place remaining apples and then remaining Ritz mixture on top.

Bake at 375 degrees for 30 minutes.
