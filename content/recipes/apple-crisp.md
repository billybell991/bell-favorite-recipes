## Ingredients

INGREDIENTS

- 5 medium sized apples, peeled and chopped into chunks
- 2 tspcinnamon
- 1/2 tspnutmeg
- 1/2 cup water
- 1 tbspmaple syrup
- 4 tbsp butter
- 3/4 cup old fashioned rolled oats
- 1/4 cup flour
- 1/4 cupbrown sugar
- 1/2 tsp salt

DIRECTIONS

## Instructions

1. Place apples on the bottom of yourInstant Pot. Sprinkle with cinnamon and nutmeg. Top with water and maple syrup.
2. Melt the butter. In asmall bowl, mix together melted butter, oats, flour, brown sugar and salt. Drop by the spoonful on top of the apples.
3. Secure the lid on the instant pot. Use the manual setting, and cook on high pressure for 8 minutes.
4. Use a natural release. Let sit for a few minutes, the sauce will thicken.
5. Serve warm and maybe top with vanilla ice cream.

Servings: 3-4
