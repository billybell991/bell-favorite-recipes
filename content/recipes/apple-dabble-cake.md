- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup vegetable oil
- 3 eggs
- 2 tsp vanilla
- 3 cups flour
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 2 tsp cinnamon
- 4 cups raw apples, chopped

Mix ingredients by hand in order given.  Pour into 13x9 inch pan.  Bake at 350 degrees Fahrenheit for 30 to 40 minutes.

Combine 1 cup of brown sugar, 1/4 cup milk, 1/4 cup butter.  Boil for 4 minutes.  Pour over baked cake and return to oven for a few minutes.  This is scrumptious!
