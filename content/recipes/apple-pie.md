Submitted by Yolande and Dave Worthington

Aunt and Uncle of the Groom

- 4 VERY large Granny Smith apples (6 cups)
- 2/3 to 1 cup white sugar, depending on tartness of apples
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1/8 tsp salt
- 1 tbsp cornstarch

Prepare the “Never Fail Pastry” on page 92. Use 2 portions.

Mix sugar, cinnamon, salt and cornstarch together. Peel and thinly slice the apples. Toll out one portion of the pastry and line the bottom of a 9 inch pie pan. Place alternate layers of apples and sugar mixture in the pie pan. Roll out the second portion of pastry, cut vents and place top crust in position. Seal edge and flute.

Bake in a pre-heated, 400 degree oven for 40-50 minutes or until pastry is golden brown and apples are tender.
