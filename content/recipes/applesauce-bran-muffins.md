## Ingredients

- 1 cup All-Bran (Cereal)
- ¼ cup milk
- 1 cup applesauce
- 1/3 cup oil
- 1 egg
- ½ cup raisins or ½ cup carob chips(optional)
- ¾ cup all-purpose flour
- ¾ cup whole wheat flour
- 3 tsp baking powder
- ½ tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1/3 cup Brown Sugar Twin

## Instructions

Stir All-Bran, milk, applesauce, oil and egg together.

Stir together the dry ingredients.

Add bran mixture to dry ingredients, stirring just to moisten.

Bake at 375 degrees for 15 – 20 minutes.
