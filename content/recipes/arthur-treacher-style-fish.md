## Ingredients

- 3 lbs fish fillet
- Dairy buttermilk (enough to cover)
- 1 lemon, sliced
- 1 pint (8 oz) oil for frying
- Dry pancake mix (for dredging)
- 2 cups pancake mix
- 2-1/2 cups club soda

## Instructions

Soak fish fillets in buttermilk. Place lemon slices over them, cover, and refrigerate 2–3 hours to remove "fishy" taste.

Heat oil in a heavy 2-1/2 quart saucepan.

Drain off milk and cut fillets in halves (triangle shape). Dredge in dry pancake mix.

In another bowl, combine 2 cups pancake mix and 2-1/2 cups club soda to the consistency of buttermilk.

Dip floured fillets into batter, let excess drip off.

Deep fry 4 minutes each side. Keep warm in a 250°F oven without stacking until all are fried.
