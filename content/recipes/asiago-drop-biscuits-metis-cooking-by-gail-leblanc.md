## Ingredients

- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 cup whole wheat flour
- 2 Tbsp sugar (can use Splenda)
- 1 Tbsp baking powder
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1/3 cup margarine
- 1/3 cup grated Asiago cheese
- 2 Tbsp chopped herbs (rosemary, thyme, sage,
- oregano – these can be fresh or dried herbs)

## Instructions

Combine dry ingredients. Cut in margarine. Stir in cheese and herbs. Pour buttermilk over flour mixture and stir only enough to form rough batter (don’t over mix)

Drop batter by tablespoonful on greased baking sheet.

Bake at 400 deg. F. for 12 to 15 minutes (don’t over cook – these do not necessarily have to brown.
