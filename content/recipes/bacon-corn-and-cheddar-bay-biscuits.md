## Ingredients

- 1 cup flour
- 7/8 cup milk
- 4 tsp baking powder
- 2 Tbsp milted shortening OR melted bacon fat
- ½ tsp salt
- 4 Tbsp brown sugar
- ½ cup corn kernels
- 1 cup corn meal
- 6 bacon slices
- 1 egg

## Instructions

Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Line an 8x8x2-inch pan with bacon slices.

Sift flour, baking powder, salt and brown sugar. Add corn meal and stir to blend.

Beat egg, add milk, melted fat and corn kernels and stir to blend.

Pour liquid ingredients over dry ingredients and stir just enough to blend.

Pour batter into pan over bacon slices.

Bake in preheated oven from 25 to 30 minutes or till light brown on top.

To serve, unmould same as an upside-down cake.

Serve hot.
