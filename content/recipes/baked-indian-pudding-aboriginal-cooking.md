## Ingredients

- 5 cups milk
- 2 level Tablespoon butter
- 6 eggs
- 1 tsp nutmeg
- 1 tsp ginger
- 1 cup Indian meal (Cornmeal)
- 1 cup powdered sugar (Fruit)

## Instructions

Scald 4 cups milk. Mix Indian Meal with 1 cup cold milk and stir into hot milk. Add butter. Continue to stir until mixture comes to a boil. Remove from heat.

Beat eggs until fluffy with the sugar and add nutmeg and ginger, then stir the whole mixture together. Pour into a buttered casserole or deep 12 x 8” baking dish.

Bake at 400 F. until nicely browned, approximately 30 minutes. Serve hot with Wine Sauce.

WINE SAUCE:  Beat 1 cup fruit sugar and ½ cup soft butter to a cream. Flavour with ¼ to ½ tsp nutmeg and 2 to 4 Tbsp wine or brandy. Chill and Serve.
