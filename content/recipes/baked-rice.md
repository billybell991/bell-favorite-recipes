## Ingredients

- 2 cups of Uncle Ben’s converted rice
- 4 cups water
- ½ cup soya sauce
- ½ cup oil
- 1 pkg onion soup mix
- 1 can drained mushrooms
- 1 can drained peas

## Instructions

Mix all ingredients in roast pan. Place in oven for one hour at 350 degrees.
