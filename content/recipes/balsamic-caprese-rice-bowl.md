## Ingredients

Phase 1: For the Steamer

- 2 cups Long-grain rice (Basmati or Jasmine, rinsed)
- 3 cups Broth (Chicken or Vegetable) or water
- 6 cloves Garlic, smashed
- 2 tbsp Butter or Olive oil
- 2 pinches of Sea salt

Phase 2: The Fresh Components

- 3 Roma tomatoes, diced
- 1 cup Fresh basil, sliced into ribbons
- 1 cup Shredded cheese (Mozzarella or Italian blend)
- 4 tbsp Balsamic vinegar (or 1 tbsp Balsamic Glaze)

Instructions

## Instructions

1. Steam: Place all Phase 1 ingredients into the rice steamer. Start the normal cook cycle. The garlic will soften and mellow as the rice cooks.
2. Prep: While the rice is steaming, dice the tomatoes and toss them with a tiny pinch of salt in a small bowl. Slice the basil ribbons and set aside.
3. The Garlic Mash: Once the steamer finishes, find the softened garlic cloves. Mash them into a paste with a fork and fold them back into the rice.
4. Season: Drizzle the balsamic vinegar over the hot rice and fluff thoroughly with a fork until the color is consistent.
5. Melt: Sprinkle the shredded cheese over the rice. Close the steamer lid for 1–2 minutes (with the power off) to allow the residual steam to melt the cheese.
6. Garnish: Fold in the salted tomatoes and fresh basil ribbons. Serve immediately.
