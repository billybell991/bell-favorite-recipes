## Ingredients

- 1 3/4 cups flour
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 1/4 tsp baking soda
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/3 cup shortening
- 2/3 cup white sugar
- 2 eggs, beaten
- 1 cup mashed bananas

## Instructions

Blend flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt together. In another bowl, cream shortening and sugar together. Add beaten eggs and blend. Add dry ingredients alternately with bananas.

Beat after each addition until well blended. Pour into a loaf pan and let stand for 20 minutes. Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.  Poke it in the middle and if it's still doughy-raw, then kick 'er back in for another 5 mins.
