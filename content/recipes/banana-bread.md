## Ingredients

- 3 bananas, mashed
- 1 cup white sugar
- 1 egg, beaten
- 1-1/2 cup flour
- 1 tsp each baking soda, baking powder
- 1/8 tsp salt
- 2 tsp melted butter or oil
- 1 tsp vanilla

## Instructions

Mash bananas and mix with white sugar.  Add beaten egg.  Sift together flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt.  Add to first mixture.  Beat well.  Add melted butter and vanilla.  Bake in a greased loaf pan at 350 degrees for 35 to 45 minutes.  Test to make sure it is baked right through.
