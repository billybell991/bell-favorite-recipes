Cousins of the Groom

Cake:

- 2 ½ cups flour
- 1 ½ tsp baking soda
- 1 ½ tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 ½ cup white sugar
- ¼ tsp cinnamon
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup mashed bananas
- 1 cup buttermilkor1 cup sourmilk (add 2 tbsp vinegar to your regular cup of milk)

Throw all the ingredients into a big bowl and mix. Pour into a 3 L pan that has been greased, and bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes or until a knife inserted in the middle comes out clean and dry.

Caramel Icing:

## Ingredients

- 2 cups whipping cream
- 2 cups brown sugar

## Instructions

Put these two ingredients in a large enough saucepan and bring to a boil. Start your stove at #8 until it boils, stir constantly, then turn your stove down until it reaches a simmering boil and let it boil down, stirring occasionally. This usually takes about 30 minutes. Remove from stove and let it sit on the counter until it cools down a bit, roughly 10-15 minutes.
