Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Blend or sift together:

- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- ¼ cup sugar
- 4 tsp baking powder
- ¼ tsp salt
- Chopped walnuts (optional)

Make a well in center of the dry ingredients.

Combine and add:

- 1 beaten egg
- 2/3 cup milk
- 1 cup well-mashed bananas
- 2 Tbsp vegetable oil

Stir only until combined. (The batter should be lumpy.) Fill greased muffin cups 2/3 full.

Bake in preheated oven for approx. 20 minutes.

Yield:  12 medium-sized muffins – for approx. 20 minutes.

42 very small muffins – for 10 minutes.

Note: I (Mom) iced mine with cream cheese frosting.
