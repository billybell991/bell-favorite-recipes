## Ingredients

- 1 cup flour
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 2 tsp brown sugar
- 1 pinch of salt
- Water, as needed

## Instructions

1. Mix ingredients into a bowl until mixed and a sticky dough forms. Can add a soupcon more flour to make it less sticky. But this is important, and I cannot stress this enough - DO NOT KNEAD. This is not bread. Do not overmix or spend too much time slapping this dough ball around, or the bannock will end up harder than Henry Cavill’s pectorals.
2. Flatten out dough onto floured surface until its about a half-inch thick or so, then cut up into pieces. Don’t use rolling pin, use your hands like a peasant. Cut into whatever size pieces you think you’ll want. If you use larger pieces, you  might need to make holes in the middle to allow it to properly cook through.
3. Deep fry in some oil until golden brown for best results. Can also be baked in the oven, or wrapped around a bannock stick and cooked over a fire. Feel free to throw in extra bits to your liking, like throw in some blueberries if you want more desserty bannock, or jalapenos if you are feeling spicy.
