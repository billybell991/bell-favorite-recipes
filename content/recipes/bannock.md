## Ingredients

- 3 cups flour (730 ml)
- 3 level Tbsp baking powder
- 1 Tbsp sugar
- 1 cup cold water
- 1 Tbsp (heaping) lard
- ¾ tsp salt

## Instructions

Mix together the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar in large bowl. Add the lard, cut into small pieces.
Mix and make a well in the middle. Add water and mix again. If the dough is too sticky, add a little more flour. Pat and form into a cake.
Deep fry in enough lard or oil so the mixture floats OR bake over coals on a stick like  hot dogs. Cook about 20 minutes. Tastes great alone or buttered, with molasses, honey or corn syrup.

NOTE: I haven’t tried this recipe yet. It was given to me by a good friend who neglected to tell me how much salt to put in.  I took a guess. You might have to adjust it to your taste.
