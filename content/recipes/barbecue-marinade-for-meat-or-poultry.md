## Ingredients

- ½ cup prepared barbecue sauce
- ¼ cup vegetable oil
- ¼ cup Realemon lemon juice
- 1 Tbsp packed brown sugar

## Instructions

In small bow, combine barbecue sauce, oil, lemon juice and sugar. Pour over meat or poultry. Cover and refrigerate six hours or overnight, turning occasionally. Remove meat from marine; grill or broil as desired, basting frequently with marinade. Makes about 1 cup.

BEER MARINADE – For beef or pork

1 large onion, finely chopped	2 cloves garlic, crushed, chopped
½ cup vegetable oil, divided	½ cup Realemon lemon juice
1 cup beer	2 Tbsp packed brown sugar
2 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce

In medium frypan, cook onion and garlic in ¼ cup oil until tender. Remove from heat. Add beer, lemon juice, remaining oil, sugar and Worcestershire sauce. Pour over meat. Cover and refrigerate six hours or overnight, stirring occasionally. Remove meat from marinade.Grill or broil as desired, basting frequently with marinade.
