## Ingredients

- ½ cup Canola oil
- ½ cup chili sauce
- 1 cup vinegar
- 1 Tbsp chopped onion
- 1 clove garlic
- 1 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
- ½ tsp chili powder (optional)
- ¼ cup brown sugar

## Instructions

Measure all ingredients into bowl. Mix until thoroughly blended.

YIELD: 2 cups

NOTE: Use Sauce for Barbecued Frankfurters, Chicken, Fish, Pork Chops or Ribs.
