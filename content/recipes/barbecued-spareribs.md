- 4 lbs spareribs
- ¼ cup vinegar
- 1 cup sliced onions
- ¼ cup brown sugar
- 1 cup ketchup
- 2 tsp dry mustard
- 1 cup water
- 1 tsp paprika
- 2 tsp salt
- 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce

Cut ribs into serving pieces.  Brown them in frying pan.  Combine the remaining ingredients and pour over ribs.  Cover.  Bake in moderate oven at 350 degrees for 1-3/4 hrs.  Spoon sauce over the ribs two or three times during baking.  Then uncover and cook for 15 minutes.
