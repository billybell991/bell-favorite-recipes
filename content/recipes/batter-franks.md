- 1 egg
- ½ cup milk
- 1 cup Bisquick
- 2 tbsp yellow corn meal
- ¼ tsp paprika
- ½ tsp dry mustard
- 1/8 tsp cayenne pepper
- 1 lb (12) frankfurters

Heat deep fat to 375 degrees.  Blend egg and milk.  Stir in dry ingredients.  Dip frankfurters into batter.  Fry until brown – 2 to 3 minutes

NOTE:  Do not use previously frozen wieners as batter will not stick to them.

NEAT TRICK:  Push wooden skewers into end of Batter Franks for eating with fingers.
