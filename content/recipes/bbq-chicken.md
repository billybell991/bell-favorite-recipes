- 2 lb chicken breasts
- 2 tbsp shortening
- 1 can tomato soup
- 1/2 cup chopped onion
- 1/2 cup chopped celery
- 2 tbsp brown sugar
- 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- 2 tsp dry mustard

Brown chicken in shortening.  Stir in other ingredients.  Cover and simmer for 45 minutes until chicken is tender.  Serve on a bed of cooked rice.
