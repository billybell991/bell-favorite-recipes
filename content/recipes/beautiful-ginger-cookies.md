## Ingredients

- ½ cup softened butter
- ½ cup packed dark brown sugar
- ¼ cup molasses
- 1 medium or large egg (do not use extra large or jumbo)
- 2-1/4 cups flour
- ½ tsp cinnamon
- 2 tsp ginger
- 2 dashes cloves
- 1 dash cayenne pepper

## Instructions

Thoroughly cream the butter by hand. Add brown sugar and molasses, then the egg. In a separate bowl mix together all dry ingredients. Stir them into the butter mixture. Knead the dough briefly. Chill the dough, then form the cookies as directed in the “General Instructions” and bake at 375 degrees for 10 – 12 minutes, or until the edges brown.

Yield: 5 to 8 cookies depending on size of mold.
