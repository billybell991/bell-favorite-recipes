Submitted by Becky and Darren Shackleton

Friends of the Bride and Groom

- 1 large package of wings (about 25)
- ¾ cup of soya sauce
- ½ cup ketchup
- ½ cup water
- ½ cup brown sugar
- 2 tsp flour

Take a large glass casserole dish and mix soya sauce, ketchup, water and brown sugar.  Add the flour and mix well.  Add the wings in the dish and place in oven for 40 minutes at 400 degrees.
