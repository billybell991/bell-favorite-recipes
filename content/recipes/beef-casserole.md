- 2 lb stewing beef
- 1 pkg dry onion soup mix
- 1 10-oz tin cream of mushroom soup
- 1 cup red table wine

Combine ingredients in casserole. Cook slowly for 2 – 3 hours in a 250 degree oven. Serve on a bed of egg noodles.

YIELD: Six to eight servings.
