Groom

## Ingredients

- 3 cups self-rising flour
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1 six-pack of beer
- 1 tbsp butter, melted

## Instructions

Preheat the oven to 325 degrees.

In a large bowl, blend the flour and sugar. Quickly mix in one can of beer. Place the mixture in a 9x5 inch loaf pan. Wet your fingers and smooth the top.

Bake for 1 hour or until golden brown. Drink the other 5 beer while you’re waiting.

Brush the top of the bread with melted butter.
