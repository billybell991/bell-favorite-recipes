## Ingredients

- Boil, peel and dice beets to make 16 cups.
- ½ cup flour
- 1 Tbsp dry mustard
- 2-1/2 cups white vinegar
- 2 cups white sugar
- 1 cup water
- 1 Tbsp salt

## Instructions

Mix until well blended and bring to a boil.  Boil until sauce is thickened. Add diced beets.
Pour hot mixture into hot sealer jars. Seal and let cool. Vacuum seal is accomplished when jar lids “pop” inward.
