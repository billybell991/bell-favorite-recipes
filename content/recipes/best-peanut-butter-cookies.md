## Ingredients

- 1 cup soft butter
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup peanut butter (Smooth or chunky)
- 2-1/2 cups flour
- 1 cup white sugar
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1 cup brown sugar
- ½ tsp salt

## Instructions

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Use a wooden spoon or electric mixer to cream the butter in a large bowl. Add peanut butter and mix until well blended. Add white and brown sugars, a little at a time, mixing until they are blended in. Break an egg into a small bowl and stir. Add to the peanut butter mixture and mix well. Do the same with second egg. Sift flour, baking powder and salt over mixture; stir until well blended. Form into 1 to 1 inch balls. Place on greased cookie sheet. Flatten with a fork. Bake for 10 minutes. Makes 36 large cookies.
