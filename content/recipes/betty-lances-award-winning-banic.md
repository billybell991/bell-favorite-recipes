## Ingredients

- 2-1/2  to 3 cups flour
- Pinch of salt
- 1-1/2 Tbsp baking powder
- 2 Tbsp margarine – melted
- 1-1/2 cups milk (enough milk to moisten – like dumplings)

## Instructions

Bake at 350 degrees for 35 to 40 minutes until golden brown.

Use a cast iron frying pan or an 8 x 8 cake pan.
