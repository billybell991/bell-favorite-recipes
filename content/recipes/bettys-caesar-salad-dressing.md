## Ingredients

- ¾ cup salad oil
- ¼ cup lemon juice
- 1 egg
- 2 garlic cloves
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions

Place these ingredients in a blender and blend thoroughly.  Chill.

Iceberg lettuce or Romaine lettuce	Croutons
Bacon, cooked crispy and crumbled

Tear lettuce in bite-sized pieces into salad bowl.  Add dressing, bacon bits and Caesar salad mix to taste.  Top with croutons.
