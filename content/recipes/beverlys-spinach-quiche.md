## Ingredients

- 1 box chopped spinach or broccoli
- l/2 chopped onion
- l/2 cup bell pepper
- l cup cheese
- ¾ cup Bisquick
- 3 eggs
- l/2 tsp salt
- l/4 tsp pepper

## Instructions

Spray Pam in pan.  Put first 4 ingredients (spinach, onion, bell pepper, cheese) in bottom of pie plate.  Mix rest with mixer to pour over other ingredients.  Bake at 400 degrees for 35 minutes.
