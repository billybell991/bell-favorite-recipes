## Ingredients

- 16 ounces of ground beef
- 6 cups Romaine lettuce
- 1/4 cup red onion, diced
- 2 dill pickle spears, chopped
- grate a bit of cheddar cheese and toss it in there too
- Thousand Island Dressing
- Salt and Pepper to taste (I didn't use this)

## Instructions

1. Brown ground beef until cooked through.
2. Chop Romaine lettuce into individual bowls.
3. Chop red onion, pickles, and tomatoes and set aside.
4. Pout Thousand Island Dressing on the lettuce as you desire.
5. Mix the lettuce and dressing until evenly coated.
6. Add ground beef and chopped vegetables to the lettuce and dressing and mix until evenly coated.
7. Salt & Pepper to taste.

Billy's Notes:  I tweaked this a bit compared to the recipe linked below.  Big Macs don't have tomatoes and I grated some cheese on mine as well.
