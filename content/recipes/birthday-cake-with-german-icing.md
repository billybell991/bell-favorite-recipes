Aunt and Uncle of the Bride

Cake:

- 2 cups flour
- 1 1/4 cup sugar
- dash of salt
- 3 tsp baking powder
- 2 eggs
- 3/4 cup oil
- 1 tsp vanilla

Mix first four ingredients together, then add remaining ingredients. Bake at 350 degrees for 30-45 minutes

German Icing:

## Ingredients

- 1 egg, beaten
- 2/3 cup brown sugar
- 1/2  cup can milk
- 2 tbsp butter
- 1/4  tsp salt

## Instructions

Boil until it starts to thicken and add 1 ½ cup of coconut and ½ cup of nuts.
