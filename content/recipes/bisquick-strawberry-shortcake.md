## Ingredients

- 2-1/3 cups Bisquick
- 3 Tbsp melted butter
- ½ cup milk
- 1 quart strawberries, sliced and sweetened
- 3 Tbsp sugar

## Instructions

Heat oven to 425 F. Stir all ingredients except strawberries until soft fough forms.

Drop by 6 spoonfuls onto ungreased baking sheet.

Bake 10 – 12 minutes. Split; fill and top with strawberries and whipped cream, if desired.
