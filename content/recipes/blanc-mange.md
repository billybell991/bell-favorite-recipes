## Ingredients

- 1/3 cup sugar
- 3 Tbsp cornstarch
- ¼ tsp salt
- 2-1/4 cup milk

## Instructions

Mix sugar, starch and salt in top of double boiler. Add milk, mix and cook until thickened.
