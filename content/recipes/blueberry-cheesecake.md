Cousins of the Bride

Crust:

- ½ cup butter
- 2 cups graham crumbs
- ¼ cup sugar

Topping:

- 2 pkgs Dream Whip
- 1 pkg cream cheese
- ½ cup sugar

Filling:

## Ingredients

- ¾ cup sugar
- 1 tsp salt
- 3 tbsp cornstarch
- 1 tbsp butter
- ¼ cup water
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 4 cups fresh/frozen blueberries

Note:  Above filling can be substituted for 2 cans of blueberry pie filling.

Crust:Melt butter. Stir in sugar and graham crumbs. Press into 9x13 inch pan.

Filling:Combine sugar, salt and cornstarch into a pot. Add water. Add 2 cups of blueberries, stirring constantly over medium heat until mixture comes to a boil and is clear and thickened. Remove from heat. Add remaining blueberries, lemon juice and butter and stir. Let cool. Pour over top of

graham crust.

Topping:Prepare Dream Whip according to package directions. In separate bowl, blend cream cheese and sugar. Add to Dream Whip. Spread over top of filling, starting with the edges and working your way to the middle.

Sprinkle with graham crumbs and blueberries to garnish. Refrigerate for approximately 30 minutes before serving.
