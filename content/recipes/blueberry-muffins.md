## Ingredients

- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1/2 cup granulated sugar
- 1 tbsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 cup fresh or frozen blueberries
- 1 cup milk
- 1/4 cup vegetable oil or melted butter
- 1 large egg
- 1 tsp vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a 12-cup muffin tin with paper liners.
2. In a bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
3. In a separate bowl, whisk milk, oil, egg, and vanilla until combined.
4. Stir wet ingredients into dry just until combined, then gently fold in blueberries.
5. Divide batter evenly between muffin cups and bake 18–22 minutes, or until golden and a toothpick comes out clean.
6. Cool slightly before serving.
