## Ingredients

- 3 cups blueberries
- 1 cup water
- ½ cup sugar
- 3 Tbsp lemon juice
- Pinch of cinnamon
- Pinch of cloves
- ½ tsp coriander
- 1 cup yogurt or cream

## Instructions

In a blender, blend blueberries until pureed. Place in cooking pot and add remaining ingredients. Bring to a boil, then lower heat and simmer 5 minutes (covered). Remove from heat and cool. Once cooled, add one cup of yogurt or one cup of cream.
