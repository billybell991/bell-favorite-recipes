Aunt of the Bride

- 2 cups raisins
- 1 cup water

Boil together for 5 minutes.

- 4 cups flour
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 cups white sugar
- 1 cup shortening
- 3 eggs
- 1 tsp nutmeg
- 1 tsp allspice
- ½ cup chopped nuts
- 2 tsp cinnamon

Mix dry ingredients. Add beaten eggs with the raisins and water. Drop by the tablespoon onto a cookie sheet.

Bake at 350 degrees for 10 to 15 minutes.
