## Ingredients

- 1 package of garlic and fine herbs Boursin cheese
- 2 cups uncooked pasta (bowties work well)
- 4 cloves of garlic finely chopped
- 1/2 cup of cubed cheddar cheese
- 2 cups of chopped fresh broccoli
- 2 tablespoons of olive oil
- 2 cups of stock (vegetable or chicken)
- 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika
- 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
- 1 teaspoon of salt
- 3/4 shredded cheese (any kind)
- 1/2 cup of heavy cream (can add extra cheese instead to thicken)

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees Fahrenheit
2. Add Boursin to middle of baking dish, surround with uncooked pasta, broccoli, and cheese cubes
3. Add olive oil, spices, and stock over top of everything.
4. Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes covered.
5. Remove after 30 minutes. Add shredded cheese and cream, stir and place back in oven uncovered for 10 minutes or until pasta is tender.

*If too runny, use the cornstarch and water method to thicken the sauce*
