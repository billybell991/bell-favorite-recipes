## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 1 cup bran
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp soda
- ½ tsp cream of tartar
- 1 Tbsp melted shortening
- 1 cup sour milk

## Instructions

Mix dry ingredients, add the melted shortening, then sour milk. Pour into a greased baking pan and bake in a moderate oven for about 40 minutes.
