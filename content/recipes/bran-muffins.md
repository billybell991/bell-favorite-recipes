## Ingredients

- 1 cup All-Bran
- ½ cup sugar
- ½ cup raisins
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 1 cup milk
- 1 cup flour
- ½ cup butter
- Pinch salt
- 1 egg

## Instructions

Mix all together and put into muffin tin. Bake at 400 F. for 15 to 20 minutes.
