## Ingredients

- 24 cukes, sliced, let stand in salt 2 hrs.
- Rinse in cold water, drain.
- 12 onions, sliced
- 4 cups vinegar
- 2-1/2 cups white sugar
- 1 tsp ginger
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 1 tsp mustard seed
- 1 tsp celery seed

## Instructions

Put cukes and onions in vinegar solution and just bring to a boil. Take off heat immediately and pack cukes & onions in jars.

Bring solution to a boil again and pour into jars. Seal
