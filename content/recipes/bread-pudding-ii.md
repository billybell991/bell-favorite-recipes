## Ingredients

- 6 slices day-old bread
- 2 tablespoons butter, melted
- 1/2 cup raisins (optional)
- 4 eggs, beaten
- 2 cups milk
- 3/4 cup white sugar
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Billy's Note:I didn't have raisins once, so I subbed in 2 apples cut up in little pieces.  I drizzled caramel sauce on it to make a caramel apple bread pudding.  Worked great!

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Break bread into small pieces into an 8 inch square baking pan. Drizzle melted butter or margarine over bread. If desired, sprinkle with raisins.
3. In a medium mixing bowl, combine eggs, milk, sugar, cinnamon, and vanilla. Beat until well mixed. Pour over bread, and lightly push down with a fork until bread is covered and soaking up the egg mixture.
4. Billy's Note:I let it soak up all that eggy milkness for around 15 mins here before putting it in the oven.
5. Bake in the preheated oven for 45 minutes, or until the top springs back when lightly tapped.
