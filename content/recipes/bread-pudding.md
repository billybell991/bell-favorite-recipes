## Ingredients

- 4 cups bread crumbs
- ½ cup white sugar
- ¼ tsp salt
- 1 tsp cinnamon or nutmeg
- ½ cup seeded raisins
- 2 eggs
- 2 cups milk
- ¼ cup of butter

## Instructions

Scald milk and add butter.  Beat eggs slightly and add to milk with sugar, salt, spice and raisins.  Pour over bread crumbs and mix well.  Pour into a greased deep casserole and bake in a 350 degree oven for 45 minutes.  Serve with cream or lemon sauce.
