## Ingredients

- ½ cup raisins
- ½ cup shelled sunflower seeds
- 1 medium red onion, chopped
- ½ lb crisp, crumbled bacon
- 3 heads broccoli florets

## Instructions

Combine the above ingredients in a large bowl.

Dressing:
½ cup Miracle Whip
2 tsp rice vinegar
1/3 cup white sugar (or Splenda)

Combine and add to broccoli mixture. Stir well and chill.
