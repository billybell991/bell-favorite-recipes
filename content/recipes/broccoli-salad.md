Sister and Brother-in-law of the Bride

- 1/2 cup raisins
- 1/2 cup shelled sunflower seeds
- 1 medium red onion, chopped
- 1/2 lb crisp, crumbled bacon
- 3 heads broccoli florets

Combine the above ingredients in a large bowl.

Dressing:

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup Miracle Whip
- 2 tsp rice vinegar
- 1/3 cup white sugar

## Instructions

Combine and add to broccoli mixture. Stir well and chill.
