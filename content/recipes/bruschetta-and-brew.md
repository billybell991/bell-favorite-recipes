Friends of the Groom

- ¼ cup white wine vinegar
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp fresh, chopped parsley
- 1 tsp each of sugar and dried basil
- ½ tsp dried dillweed
- ¼ tsp each of salt and pepper
- dash of hot pepper sauce
- 1 clove of garlic, minced
- 2 tomatoes, chopped
- 1 cucumber, chopped
- 1 french baguette (or bread)
- feta cheese for topping

In a bowl, combine all ingredients (except cheese), stir, set aside.

Cut bread into ½ inch slices, place on baking sheet and broil, turning once until toasted. Spoon mixture over toast, top with cheese, broil for one minute.

Crack open your finest bottle of Stella Artois and enjoy!
