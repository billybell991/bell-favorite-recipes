## Ingredients

- 1 chicken breast
- 1 brick of cream cheese, softened
- 1/2 cup of ranch dressing
- 1/2 cup (or more) of Franks Red Hot
- 1 cup cheddar cheese, shredded
- Salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Season the chicken breast with salt, pepper, and chili flakes.  Bake in the oven or a pan.
2. Shred the chicken and add the Frank's Red Hot to it.  Mix it up.  (This can be left in the fridge overnight or can go to the next step right away).
3. In a separate bowl, combine the cream cheese and ranch dressing.  Mix until mostly smooth.
4. Add in the chicken/hot sauce mixture with a 1/2 cup of the cheddar cheese and mix till combined.
5. Put the dip in a baking pan (8x8 works well) and cover with the remaining cheese.
6. Bake at 350 for about 20 mins or until it starts bubbling.
