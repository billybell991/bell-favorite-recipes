Buffalo Wild Wing has to be one of the best chicken wing joints going.  Here are a handful of their cooking secrets and sauce recipes.

How to fry your wings:

- Heat oil or shortening in your fryer to 350°F.
- Fry wings for 10-12 minutes, or until golden brown.
- Remove wings and dry on a rack or paper towels briefly.
- Place in plastic container with a sealable lid.   Add 1/4 to 1/3 cup of your preferred sauce (see below) to the container.
- Close lid and shake well to coat wings.

Buffalo Wild Wing Sauce Recipes:

NOTE: For ALL of these sauce recipes, do the following:

- Combine all ingredients in a blender.  Simmer in a saucepan for 20-30 minutes.

Medium Wing Sauce:

Hot Wing Sauce:
