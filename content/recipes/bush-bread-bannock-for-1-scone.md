## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- ½ tsp salt
- 2 tsp baking powder
- ¼ cup margarine

## Instructions

Mix all the above ingredients together to form a cornmeal-like mixture.

Put this mixture in plastic bag for transporting over portages.

When needed, mix with water in a bowl, then dump it in frying pan and cook till brown. (Not too fast.)
