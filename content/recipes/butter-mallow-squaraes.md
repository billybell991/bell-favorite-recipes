## Instructions

BASE:
1/3 cup butter	1 cup lightly packed brown sugar
1 egg	1 tsp vanilla
¾ cup all-purpose flour	1 tsp baking powder
¼ tsp salt	½ cup chopped pecans (I used walnuts)
FILLING:
2-1/2 cups miniature marshmallows
TOPPING:
¼ cup butter	½ cup lightly packed brown sugar
2 Tbsp milk	1 cup sifted icing sugar
32 pecan halves (optional)
BASE: Melt butter in large saucepan. Stir in brown sugar, egg and vanilla. Blend together flour, baking powder and salt. Stir dry ingredients into butter mixture along with pecans or walnuts. Spread batter in greased 8” square pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 20 – 30 minutes. Immediately scatter KRAFT marshmallows over entire surface of hot cake. Press gently to flatten.
TOPPING: Melt butter in saucepan. Add brown sugar and simmer over low heat, stirring constantly for 2 minutes. Add milk, stirring until mixture just return to boil. Remove from heat. Gradually add icing sugar, beating with a wooden spoon after each addition until smooth. If necessary, thin icing with an additional few drops of milk. Spread quickly over top of marshmallows. Decorate with pecans, if desired. Cool before cutting into squares. Makes 20 squares.
NOTE: I found squares are easier to cut and remove from pan if they are still a bit warm when I cut them.
