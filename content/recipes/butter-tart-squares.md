## Ingredients

- Base:
- 1-1/4 cup flour
- ¼ cup brown sugar
- ½ cup butter
- Filling:
- 1/3 cup butter
- 1 egg
- 2 tbsp cream
- 1/2 tbsp flour
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1/2 cup raisins (or walnuts)
- 1 cup brown sugar

## Instructions

Grease pan lightly, especially around the edges to make it easier to remove edges pieces.
Mix base ingredients together and press firmly in a 9-inch square pan.  Mix filling together.  Pour over base and bake at 350 degrees until done. (25 to 30 minutes)

NOTE:  I found it easier to cream butter or margarine with brown sugar first in the Filling. It made for a smoother filling.
