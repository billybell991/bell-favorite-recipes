## Ingredients

- 1/3 cup soft butter
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 2 tbsp milk
- 1 egg
- 1 tsp vanilla
- raisins or walnuts (optional)

## Instructions

Cream butter and brown sugar.  Add milk, vanilla and egg, and beat well, preferably with an electric beater.  Half fill pastry-lined tart tins and bake in a 350 degree oven for 30 minutes.
