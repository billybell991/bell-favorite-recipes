## Ingredients

- 1 cup Crisco (or ½ cup Crisco + ½ cup white margarine)
- 1 kg (2.20 lbs) icing sugar
- ½ cup water (I use a wee bit more – in icing I use to ice the cake – it’s easier to spread)
- 1 tsp almond flavoring (I use 2 – 3 tsp of almond or coconut flavor)

## Instructions

This icing is the easiest recipe for doing cakes.  It has a creamy texture and is great to practice making flowers.  And for the borders – just add a few drops of  water.

Cream shortening, gradually add icing sugar.  Cream again.  Add flavoring while still creaming.  Add water slowly also while you are creaming until you reach the desired texture.  (IMPORTANT THAT YOU DO NOT CREAM YOUR ICING AT TOO HIGH A SPEED FOR IT CAUSES AIR BUBBLES IN YOUR ICING – THEREBY MAKING IT DIFFICULT TO HAVE YOUR ICING SMOOTH ON THE CAKE.)
