## Ingredients

- 1 9”baked pie shell
- 2-1/2 cups milk
- 1 cup dark brown sugar
- 3 egg yolks, slightly beaten
- 3-1/2 tbsp cornstarch
- 1 tsp vanilla
- ½ tsp salt
- 3 tbsp butter

## Instructions

Combine sugar, cornstarch and salt in top of a double boiler.  Stir in cold milk.  Cook over boiling water until thickened – stirring constantly.  Cover and cook 15 minutes longer.

Stir a little of the hot mixture into slightly beaten egg yolks.  Add to remaining mixture in double boiler and cook for 2 minutes over hot, not boiling, water, stirring constantly. Add the butter and vanilla and let cool.  Pour into the baked pie shell.

Top with meringue made from the 3 egg whites or Dream Whip or whipped cream.
