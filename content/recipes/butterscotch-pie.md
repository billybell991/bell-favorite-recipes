## Ingredients

- 3 tbsp butter
- 1-1/2 cups of milk
- 1-1/2 cups brown sugar
- 6 tbsp flour
- ¾ cup boiling water
- pinch of salt
- 4 egg yolks

## Instructions

Melt butter, add sugar and allow to brown.  Gradually add boiling water.  In medium size bowl, beat egg yolks; add a small amount of the measured milk and the flour.  Beat until smooth.  Then add remainder of milk and salt.  Pour this slowly into sugar mixture and cook until thick, stirring constantly.

MERINGUE;
4 egg whites	1 tsp lemon juice
¼ tsp salt	6 tbsp white sugar
¼ tsp vanilla

Beat egg whites until stiff.  Add remaining ingredients slowly as you beat.  Spread on pies and put in oven to brown.
