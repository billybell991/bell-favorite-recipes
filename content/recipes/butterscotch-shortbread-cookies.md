## Ingredients

- ½ lb butter
- ½ cup brown sugar
- 2 cups flour

## Instructions

Mix the ingredients together; roll out ½”thick.

Cut out with metal cookie cutters.

Bake 325 F. or 20 minutes on an ungreased cookie sheet.

NOTE FROM COLETTE PEPLINSKIE: I use a pastry cutter, then knead into a ball
