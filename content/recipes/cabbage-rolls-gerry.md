## Ingredients

- 5 lbs of lean hamburg
- 1 1/2 cans of tomato juice (48 oz)
- salt pork (small) chopped
- 3 heads of cabbage
- 3 cups long grain rice, cooked
- onions, salt and pepper

## Instructions

1. Boil cabbage, peel leaves off, and core.  Keep centre for top of cabbage rolls.
2. Fry hamburg, onions, salt, and pepper in the oven at 350 degrees for a 1/2 hour untill cooked.
3. Cut salt pork, while partially frozen, into small pieces.  Fry on low about 20 mins.
4. Cook rice separately.  (Guess it's up to you to figure out how!)
5. Mix rice, hamburg, and salt pork together gently.
6. Line a roast pan with foil.
7. Don't overstuff the cabbage leaves (about 1 to 1 1/2 tbsp of filling in each)
8. Place cabbage rolls in the pan.  Put a little bit of tomato juice over the first later.  Layer 2 more times.
9. Place leftover cabbage around outside of other rolls (so they won't burn).
10. Pour tomato juice over all.  Can put a few leaves on top of other rolls.
11. Go around the edges with a knife to make sure there's enough juice.
12. Add tomato juice during cooking if needed.
13. Cover with foil and put cover on the roast pan.
14. Cook 1 hour at 350 degrees, then 2 hours at 300 degrees.
