## Ingredients

- 2 egg yolks
- ½ clove garlic
- ½ tsp salt
- ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
- 2/3 cup olive oil

## Instructions

Combine all ingredients in a blender.  Mix well.  Serve with romaine lettuce, croutons and bacon.  Makes 1-1/2 cup dressing.
