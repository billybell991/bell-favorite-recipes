## Ingredients

- ½ tsp salt
- ½ tsp ground pepper
- ½ tsp dry mustard
- 1 cup (1/4 lb) grated Romano
- ½ tsp crushed oregano
- or Parmesan cheese or a blend
- ½ tsp M.S.G. (optional)
- or Parmesan and Romano

## Instructions

Mix all ingredients thoroughly.  Store in a covered jar at room temperature.  Use as the seasoning in Caesar Salad.  It may also be sprinkled over other tossed salads and used as a topping for casseroles and creamed soups.
