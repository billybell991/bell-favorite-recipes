## Ingredients

- Preparation: 10 minutes
- Cook: 22 minutes
- 2 Tbsp margarine or butter
- 1 Tbsp all-purpose Flour
- 2 cups milk
- 1 pouch Loney’s Onion Soup mix
- ½ cup parmesan cheese, grated
- 1 tsp Dijon mustard
- ½ tsp black pepper
- Pinch garlic powder
- 4 cups peeled potatoes, sliced

## Instructions

In large, deep microwaveable casserole, melt margarine or butter at high. Blend in flour, microwave at High 30 seconds. Whisk in milk and Soup Mix; microwave at High 4 to 6 Minutes, stirring twice, until mixture thickens slightly. Stir in cheese, mustard, pepper, garlic powder and potatoes. Microwave at High, covered, 12 to 16 minutes or until potatoes are tender. Stir twice during cooking.

For a crisp golden topping, sprinkle these microwave-cooked potatoes with grated Parmesan cheese and place until broiler a few minutes.  Garnish with fresh parsley.

Makes 4 servings.
