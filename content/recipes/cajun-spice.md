## Ingredients

- 1 tbsp sweet paprika
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp onion powder
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp cayenne pepper
- ½ tsp white pepper
- 1 tsp black pepper
- ½ tsp dried thyme
- ½ tsp dried oregano

## Instructions

Spread melted butter on cold fish that has been wiped dry.  Sprinkle spices on fish.  Cook fish in ungreased very hot iron frying pan.  NOTE:  Fish should be sliced fairly thin so that it will cook right through without burning on the outside.  Fish done this way cooks very rapidly.

FOR LARGER QUANTITY:
½ cup sweet paprika	¼ cup salt
¼ cup onion powder	¼ cup garlic powder
¼ cup cayenne powder	1/8 cup white pepper
¼ cup black pepper	1/8 cup dried thyme
1/8 cup dried oregano
Makes 2-1/8 cups.
NOTE:  I omitted the salt when I made it.
