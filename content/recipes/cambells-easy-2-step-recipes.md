## Campbell's Easy 2-Step Recipes

CAMPBELL’S 2-STEP MUSHROOM PORK CHOPS

Step 1     Brown 4 pork chops in 1 Tbsp hot oil, in skillet.

Step 2     Add 1 can Condensed Cream of Mushroom Soup mixed with ½ cup water. If desired, add ½ tsp garlic powder. Cover and simmer until pork chops are cooked through.

Serves 4.

CAMPBELL’S 2-STEP LEMON BROCCOLI CHICKEN

Step 1     Brown 4 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves in 1 Tbsp hot oil, in skillet.

Step 2      Add 1 can Condensed Cream of Broccoli Soup mixed with ½ cup water and 1 Tbsp lemon juice. Cover and simmer until chicken is cooked through. Garnish with lemon slices.

Serves 4.

TIP: Bone-in chicken may be substituted. Add about 5-10 minutes to cook time.

CAMPBELL’S 2-STEP HONEY MUSTARD CHICKEN

Step 1     Brown 4 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves in l Tbsp hot oil in skillet.

Step 2     Add 1 can Condensed Cream of Chicken Soup mixed with ½ cup water, 3 Tbsp Dijon-style mustard and 1 Tbsp honey. Cover and simmer until chicken is cooked through.

Serves 4.

CAMPBELL’S EASY 2-STEP CHICKEN

Step 1:  Brown 4 boneless chicken breasts

Step 2.  Add 1 can Cream of Mushroom Soup and ½ cup water. Cover and simmer until done.

Prep/Cook time: 20 minutes.

Serves 4

Also delicious with Cream of Chicken, Cream of Celery or Tomato with Roasted Garlic and Herbs soup

CAMPBELL’S EASY 2-STEP BEEFY TACO JOES

Step 1:  Brown 1 lb ground beef

Step 2:  Add 1 can Tomato Soup, 1 cup Salsa. Heat through. Top with Cheese.

Serve on Rolls.

Prep/Cook time: 15 minutes. Makes 8 sandwiches.

Also delicious served on a corn muffin or rice.

CAMPBELL’S EASY 2-STEP CREAMY CHICKEN & PASTA

Step 1:  Brown 1 lb cut-up boneless chicken breasts.

Step 2:  Add  1 pkg Frozen Vegetable Pasta Blend, 1 can Cream of Chicken Soup and

½ cup water. Simmer until done.

Prep/Cook Time: 20 minutes.

Serves 4.

Also delicious with Cream of Mushroom or Cream of Celery Soups.
