## Ingredients

- 4 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves
- 1 Tbsp oil
- 1 can Condensed Cream of Chicken Soup
- 1/2 cup water
- 3 Tbsp Dijon-style mustard
- 1 Tbsp honey

## Instructions

Brown 4 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves in 1 Tbsp hot oil in skillet.

Add 1 can Condensed Cream of Chicken Soup mixed with 1/2 cup water, 3 Tbsp Dijon-style mustard and 1 Tbsp honey. Cover and simmer until chicken is cooked through.

Serves 4.
