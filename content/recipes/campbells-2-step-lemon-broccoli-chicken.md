## Ingredients

- 4 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves
- 1 Tbsp oil
- 1 can Condensed Cream of Broccoli Soup
- 1/2 cup water
- 1 Tbsp lemon juice
- Lemon slices for garnish

## Instructions

Brown 4 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves in 1 Tbsp hot oil in skillet.

Add 1 can Condensed Cream of Broccoli Soup mixed with 1/2 cup water and 1 Tbsp lemon juice. Cover and simmer until chicken is cooked through. Garnish with lemon slices.

Serves 4.
