## Ingredients

- 4 pork chops
- 1 Tbsp oil
- 1 can Condensed Cream of Mushroom Soup
- 1/2 cup water
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder (optional)

## Instructions

Brown 4 pork chops in 1 Tbsp hot oil in skillet.

Add 1 can Condensed Cream of Mushroom Soup mixed with 1/2 cup water. If desired, add 1/2 tsp garlic powder. Cover and simmer until pork chops are cooked through.

Serves 4.
