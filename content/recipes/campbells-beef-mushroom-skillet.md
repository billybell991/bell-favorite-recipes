## Ingredients

- 2 Tbsp vegetable oil
- 1 lb boneless beef sirloin or inside roundsteak, cut in strips
- 2 cups sliced mushrooms
- 1 medium onion, sliced
- 1 can Condensed Cream of Mushroom Soup
- ½ cup water
- 2 Tbsp Dijon-style mustard
- 4 cups hot cooked rice

## Instructions

Heat half the oil in large skillet over medium high heat. Add beef and brown until cooked through. Set beef aside. Reduce heat to medium.

Add remaining oil. Add mushrooms and onion and stir fry until tender.

Stir in mixture of soup, water and mustard. Heat to a boil. Return beef to skillet and heat through. Serve with rice. If desired, garnish with fresh parsley.

Prep/cook time: 25 minutes.  Serves 4.
