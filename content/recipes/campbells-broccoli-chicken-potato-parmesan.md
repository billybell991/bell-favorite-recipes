## Ingredients

- 2 Tbsp vegetable oil
- 8 mini red-skinned potatoes, thinly sliced
- 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasthalves, cut in strips
- 1 can Cream of Broccoli Soup
- ½ cup milk
- ¼ tsp garlic powder
- 2 cups fresh or frozen broccoli florets
- 1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

Heat oil at medium-high in large skillet. Add potato slices and stir fry until tender crisp.

Add chicken and continue to stir fry until chicken is browned on all sides. Reduce heat to medium.

Stir in mixture of soup, milk and garlic powder. Arrange broccoli on top; heat to a boil. Simmer, covered, over low heat until chicken is cooked through and broccoli is tender – about 5 minutes. Stir in Parmesan cheese.

Prep/cook time: 30 minutes.  Serves 4.
