## Instructions

EASY HERBED RICE – Just substitute Campbell’s Chicken Broth for the water when cooking rice. During the simmering time, add your favourite fresh or dried herbs, such as oregano, thyme or parsley.

EASY MUSHROOM RICE – Cook your rice with Campbell’s Chicken or Beef Broth instead of water. Stir fry fresh sliced mushrooms in a saucepan. Add to simmering rice at the end of cook time.

EASY SAVOURY RICE PILAF – Simply cook rice in Campbell’s Chicken or Beef Broth, instead of water. During the last 10 minutes of the simmering time, add fresh or frozen diced vegetables.
