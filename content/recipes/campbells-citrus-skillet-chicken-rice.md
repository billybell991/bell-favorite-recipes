## Ingredients

- 1 Tbsp vegetable oil
- 4 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves,(about 1 lb/500 gr.)
- 1-1/4 cups Campbell’s Ready to UseBroth or 1 can Condensed ChickenBroth.
- 1 medium onion, chopped
- ¾ cup orange juice
- 1 cup uncooked regular long grain rice
- 1-1/2 cups fresh (cut-up) or frozen greenbeans
- 1 tsp grated orange peel
- 3 Tbsp chopped fresh parsley or l Tbsp dried parsley flakes

## Instructions

Heat oil at medium-high in large skillet. Add chicken and cook until browned on both sides. Set chicken aside.

Stir in broth, orange juice and onion. Heat to a boil. Stir in rice beans and orange peel. Reduce heat to low. Cover and cook – about 10 minutes. Return chicken to skillet.

Simmer covered, until chicken is cooked through and rice has absorbed most of liquid – about 10 minutes. Remove chicken to platter. Stir parsley into rice mixture. If desired, garnish with orange peel.

Prep/cook time: 30 minutes.  Serves 4.
