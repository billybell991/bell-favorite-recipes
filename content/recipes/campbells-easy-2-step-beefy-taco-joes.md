## Ingredients

- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 can Tomato Soup
- 1 cup salsa
- Cheese for topping
- Rolls for serving

## Instructions

Brown 1 lb ground beef.

Add 1 can Tomato Soup and 1 cup salsa. Heat through. Top with cheese.

Serve on rolls. Makes 8 sandwiches.
