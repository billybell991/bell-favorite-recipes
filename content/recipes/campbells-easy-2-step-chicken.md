## Ingredients

- 4 boneless chicken breasts
- 1 can Cream of Mushroom Soup
- 1/2 cup water

## Instructions

Brown 4 boneless chicken breasts.

Add 1 can Cream of Mushroom Soup and 1/2 cup water. Cover and simmer until done.

Serves 4.
