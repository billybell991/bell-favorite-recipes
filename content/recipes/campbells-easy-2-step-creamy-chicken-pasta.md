## Ingredients

- 1 lb boneless chicken breasts, cut up
- 1 pkg frozen vegetable pasta blend
- 1 can Cream of Chicken Soup
- 1/2 cup water

## Instructions

Brown 1 lb cut-up boneless chicken breasts.

Add 1 pkg frozen vegetable pasta blend, 1 can Cream of Chicken Soup and 1/2 cup water. Simmer until done.

Serves 4.
