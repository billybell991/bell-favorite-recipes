## Ingredients

- 1 can Cheddar Cheese Soup
- ½ soup can milk
- dash ground black pepper
- 4 cups hot cooked elbow macaroni, (about 2 cups uncooked)
- 1 medium tomato, chopped
- 1 green onion, sliced
- 3 Tbsp dry bread crumbs
- 2 Tbsp margarine or butter, melted

## Instructions

Combine soup, milk and pepper in 1-1/2 quart baking dish.

Stir in macaroni, tomato and green onion. Mix bread crumbs with margarine and sprinkle over macaroni mixture.

Bake at 400 F. for 20 minutes or until heated through.

Prep/cook time: 30 minutes. Serves 4.
