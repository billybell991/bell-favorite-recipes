## Ingredients

- 2 Tbsp vegetable oil
- 1 lb boneless beef sirloin or inside roundsteak, cut in strips
- 1  medium green pepper,cut in strips
- 1 medium onion, sliced
- 1 can Condensed Tomato Soup
- ¼ cup orange juice
- 2 Tbsp soya sauce
- 1 Tbsp vinegar
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 4 cups hot cooked rice

## Instructions

Heat half the oil at medium-high in large skillet. Add beef and stir fry until cooked through. Reduce heat to medium. Set beef aside.

Add remaining oil. Add pepper and onion and cook until tender-crisp.

Stir in mixture of soup, orange juice, soya sauce, vinegar and garlic powder. Return beef to skillet. Heat through, stirring occasionally. Serve over rice.

Prep/cook time: 30 minutes.     Serves 4.
