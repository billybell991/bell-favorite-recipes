## Ingredients

- 1 Tbsp vegetable oil
- ¾ lb ground beef, cooked and drained
- ¼ cup diced onions
- ¼ cup diced celery
- 1 can Tomato w/Basil & Oregano Soup
- ¾ cup water
- 5 cups hot cooked spaghetti
- ¼ cup grated Romano or Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

Heat oil at medium-high in large skillet and stir fry onions and celery until tender.

Stir in mixture or soup, water and cooked ground beef. Bring to boil; reduce heat to low.

Simmer, covered, for 10 minutes. Serve over spaghetti or toss together. Sprinkle with cheese.

Prep/cook time: 30 minutes.  Serves 4
