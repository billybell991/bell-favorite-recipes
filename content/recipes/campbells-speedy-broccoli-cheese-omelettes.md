## Ingredients

- 4 tsp vegetable oil
- 1 can Broccoli & Cheese Soup
- 8 eggs
- 2 cups cooked, chopped broccoli
- dash ground black pepper
- grated Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

Mix eggs gradually into soup with whisk or fork. Stir in half the broccoli and pepper.

Heat 1 tsp oil in non-stick 10” skillet or omelette pan, over medium heat

Add ¾ cup egg mixture. As it begins to set, lift edges allowing uncooked egg mixture to flow to bottom. Cook until set but still moist. Top one half with ¼ cup broccoli.

Fold omelette in half using spatula and slide onto plate. Sprinkle with cheese. Repeat for each of 4 omelettes. Serve with crusty bread.

Prep/cook time: 15 minutes.   Serves 4.
