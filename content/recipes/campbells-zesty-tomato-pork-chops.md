## Ingredients

- 1 Tbsp vegetable oil
- 4 pork chops (about 1 lb/500 gr.)
- 1 large red or green pepper,cut in strips
- 1 can Condensed Tomato with Basil &Oregano Soup
- ¼ cup water
- 1 Tbsp vinegar
- 1 Tbsp packed brown sugar
- 1 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
- 3 cups hot cooked mini potatoes

## Instructions

Heat oil at medium-high in large skillet. Add pork chops and cook until browned on both sides. Set pork chops aside. Add pepper strips and stir fry – about 3 minutes. Drain fat. Reduce heat to medium.

Stir in mixture of soup, water, vinegar, Worcestershire sauce and brown sugar. Heat to a boil. Return pork chops to skillet.

Simmer cover, at medium-low heat until pork is cooked through – about 10 minutes. Serve with potatoes.

Prep/cook time: 30 minutes.   Serves 4
