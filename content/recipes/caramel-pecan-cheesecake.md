## Ingredients

- 1 ready-to-use Graham pie crust
- 20 caramel squares
- 3 tbsp milk
- ½ cup chopped pecans
- 2 pkgs cream cheese, softened
- ½ cup sugar
- 2 eggs

## Instructions

Microwave caramels and milk until melted (about 2 min; stir after 1 min). Stir in pecans and mix well. Pour into pie crust and chill for 10 minutes.

Beat (medium speed) softened cream cheese, sugar and eggs until smooth. Pour into chilled pie crust.

Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes or until set. Cool to room temperature. Chill for at least 3 hours. Decorate with sundae caramel sauce and pecans.
