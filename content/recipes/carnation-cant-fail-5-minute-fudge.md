## Ingredients

- 2/3 cup (Small can) undiluted Carnation evaporated milk
- 1-2/3 cup sugar
- 1-1/2 cup (6 medium) diced marshmallows
- ½ tsp salt
- 1-1/2 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
- 1 tsp vanilla
- ½ cup chopped nuts

## Instructions

Mix Carnation, sugar and salt together in sauce pan over low heat.  Heat to boiling; cook 5 minute, stirring constantly.  Remove from heat.  Add marshmallows, chocolate chips, vanilla and nuts.  Stir 1 to 2 minutes or until marshmallows melt.  Pour into buttered pan (9” square).  Cool.  Then cut into squares.  Indeed it is wonderful to know the secret of making fudge that is smooth and creamy – perfect every time.
