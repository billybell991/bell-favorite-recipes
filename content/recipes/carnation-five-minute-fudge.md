## Ingredients

- 1-2/3 cup granulated sugar
- 2/3 cup evaporated milk
- 2 tbsp butter
- ½ tsp salt
- 2 cups miniature marshmallows
- 1-1/2 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
- ½ cup chopped walnuts
- 1 tsp vanilla

## Instructions

In medium saucepan, combine sugar, evaporated milk, butter and salt.  Stir over medium heat until mixture comes to a full boil.  Boil 5 minutes, stirring constantly. Remove from heat  Stir in marshmallows, chocolate chips, nuts and vanilla. Stir vigorously until marshmallows melt and blend. Pour into foil-lined 8” square pan. Chill until set. Cut into 1-inch squares to serve. Store in refrigerator in sealed container. Makes 64 squares.
Variations: Omit chopped walnuts and substitute one of the following:
1 cup chopped toasted hazelnuts
1 cup chopped toasted almonds, plus 1 tsp almond extract
1 cup chopped well-drained maraschino cherries plus ½ cup chopped nuts
¾ cup finely chopped candied ginger
