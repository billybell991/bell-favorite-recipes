## Ingredients

- ½ cup melted butter
- 1 6-oz pkg chocolate chips
- 1 6-oz pkg butterscotch chips

## Instructions

Melt above ingredients in a saucepan.

ADD:
1 cup peanut butter and cool

Add to cooled mixture the following:
1 cup coconut	1 pkg colored mini marshmallows
½ cup finely chopped nuts

Mix together and pack in a buttered pan and keep in fridge.
