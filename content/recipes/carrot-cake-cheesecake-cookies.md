Cream Cheese Filling:

- 1 block of cream cheese
- 1/2 cup powdered sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Cookie Dough:

- 1 cup butter or margarine
- 3/4 cup white sugar
- 1/3 cup brown sugar
- 1 egg
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 4 large carrots
- 3 cups all purpose flour
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 tbsp cinnamon
- 1 tsp nutmeg

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
2. Mix room temp cream cheese, powdered sugar, and vanilla together in a bowl
3. Scoop 1 tablespoon balls of the mixture onto parchment paper and chill in freezer while preparing cookie dough
4. Beat butter, white sugar, and brown sugar together for about 1-2 minutes. Once combined, mix in egg and vanilla
5. Shred, pat dry, and then mix carrots in.
6. Fold in flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, and nutmeg.
7. Scoop 1/3 cup of dough, flatten in palm of hand and place a cream cheese ball in the middle. Roll the dough around the cream cheese ball.
8. Roll cookie dough ball in white sugar (optional)
9. Place 6 to a tray with lots of room for expansion
10. Cook 10 minutes, check them, 3 more minutes, check them, 3 more minutes
11. Allow to cool ON TRAY for 10 minutes
