## Ingredients

- 2 medium-sized carrots, peeled & grated
- ½ tsp salt
- ½ cup raisins
- ¾ tsp cinnamon
- ½ cup healthy margarine
- ¼ tsp each ground ginger, nutmeg, allspice
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- ¾ cup dark brown sugar
- 1 cup whole wheat flour
- 1 egg
- 2-1/2 tsp baking powder
- ½ cup buttermilk
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp vanilla

## Instructions

1.      Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Coat 12 muffin cups with a little margarine. Measure 1 cup grated carrot into a bowl. Add raisins. Melt ½ cup margarine in microwave or saucepan. Let cool to room temperature.

2.      Place flours, then baking powder, soda, salt and spices into large bowl. Add sugar and stir with a fork until evenly blended. Make a well in center. Place egg, buttermilk and vanilla in another bowl. Whisk with a fork. Whisk in cooled margarine. Pour into well. Stir just until dry ingredients are moistened. Add carrots and raisins. Stir until evenly distributed.

3.      Immediately spoon into muffin cups. Do not press down.

4.      Bake in center of oven until a skewer inserted in center of muffin comes out almost clean, 17 to 20 minutes. Cool on a rack. Makes 12 muffins.
