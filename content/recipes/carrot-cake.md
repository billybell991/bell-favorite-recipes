- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup oil
- 2 cups flour
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 tsp cinnamon
- 4 eggs, beaten
- 3 cups grated carrots
- 1 cup finely chopped walnuts (optional)

Mix all together.  Do not overbeat.  Bake for 1 hour at 350 F.  Check after 35 minutes.  Do not overbake.

Use a greased angelfood cake pan or a 9 x 13 pan.
