## Ingredients

- 6 carrots, sliced (Boil 10 min.)
- 1 medium onion
- ¼ cup margarine
- 1/8 cup flour
- 1 cup milk
- 1/6 tsp dry mustard
- 1/8 tsp celery salt
- 1/8 tsp pepper
- ¾ tsp salt
- grated cheese

## Instructions

Cook onions in margarine in microwave for 5 min.  Add flour,  spices and mix.  Add milk.  Cook milk till thick 5 – 10 minutes.  Layer carrots, onion-milk mixture, then cheese.  Top with cracker crumbs, then cheese.
Bake for 30 minutes at 350 degrees F.
