## Ingredients

- 6 carrots, sliced (boil for about 10 minutes)
- 1 medium onion
- 1/4 cup margarine
- 1/8 cup flour
- 1 cup milk
- grated cheese (as much as you like)
- 1 cup cracker crumbs
- 3/4 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp dry mustard
- 1/8 tsp celery salt
- 1/8 tsp pepper

## Instructions

Cook onions in margarine in microwave for 5 minutes. Add flour and spices and mix. Add milk. Cook in milk until thick, about 5-10 minutes in microwave.

Layer cooked carrots, onion/milk mixture, then cheese, usually about 2 layers of each. Top with cheese and cracker crumbs.

Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes. Can be doubled.
