## Instructions

Combine in a mixing bowl
2 cups shredded carrots
½ cup diced celery
½ cup raisins

Mix together
1/3 cup mayonnaise or salad dressing
1 Tbps vinegar

Toss lightly with two forks and turn into lettuce-lined serving bowl.

Yield: 4 to 6 servings.
