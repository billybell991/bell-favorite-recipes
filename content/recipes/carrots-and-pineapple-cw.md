## Ingredients

- 2 cups baby carrots
- 1 20-oz tin pineapple chunks
- 4 tsp cornstarch
- ½ tsp ground cinnamon
- ½ cup packed brown sugar
- 1 tbsp butter or margarine

## Instructions

In a saucepan, bring 1” of water to a boil; place carrots in a steamer basket over water.  Cover and steam for 8 – 10 minutes or until crisp-tender.  Drain pineapple, reserving juice; set pineapple aside.

In a saucepan, combine cornstarch and cinnamon.  Add the brown sugar, butter and reserved juice.  Bring to a boil, cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened.  Stir in the carrots and pineapple.  Heat through.
YIELD: 4 servings
