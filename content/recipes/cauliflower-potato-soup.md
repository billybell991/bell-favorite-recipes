## Ingredients

- 1 Tbsp vegetable oil
- 1 tsp crushed garlic
- 1 cup chopped onions
- 1 medium cauliflower (separated into florets)
- 4 cups chicken stock (broth)
- 2 small potatoes, peeled and chopped
- ¼ cup shredded cheddar cheese
- 2 Tbsp chopped fresh chives
- In large non-stick saucepan, heat oil, brown the garlic and onions until softened (approx. 5 minutes). Add cauliflower, stock and potatoes. Bring to a boil, cover and reduce heat and simmer for 25 minutes or until tender.
- Transfer to food processor and blend until creamy and smooth. Return to saucepan and thin with more stock of desired.
- Serve with a sprinkle of cheese and chives.
