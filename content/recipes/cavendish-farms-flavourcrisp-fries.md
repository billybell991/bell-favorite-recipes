## Ingredients

- INGREDIENTS:  2 oz Mozzarella cheese, shredded
- 1 oz onion, diced
- 1 oz red pepper, diced
- 1 oz green pepper, diced
- DIPPING SAUCE:
- ¼ cup mayonnaise
- ¼ cup sour cream
- 1 finely chopped garlic clove
- 1 Tbsp chopped fresh dill

## Instructions

Combine ingredients for dipping sauce and place in serving dish.
Cook Cavendish Farms FlavourCrisp Crispy Coated or Spicy fries according to package directions.
Combine the cheese, onions and peppers. Sprinkle over fries.
Broil or bake at 450 degrees until the cheese is melted.

Serves 1.
