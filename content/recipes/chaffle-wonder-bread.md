## Ingredients

- 12 Tbsp of egg white (this is equal to 4 Large eggs)
- 4 heaping tsp of Mayonnaise
- 4 Pinches of salt
- 4 Tbsp of cream cheese (softened - can just microwave for a few seconds)
- 2 tsp of baking powder
- 1 tsp of sweetener (Stevia)
- 8 Tbsps of almond flour
- 2 tsp of psyllium husk
- 2 tsp of water (if necessary - should be like a pancake batter consistency - I didn't need to when I made this)

NOTE: I quadrupled this because the original recipe only makes 2 of them.  The recipe measurements above will make 8 of them.

## Instructions

1. Mix all ingredients together in a bowl and whisk it until it becomes a nice pancake-ish textured batter.
2. Heat up your chaffle maker and, when ready, pour ¼ cup of the batter onto the bottom plate of it.
3. To prevent overflowing, let that bottom part cook for 30-60 seconds before closing it.
4. Let them cool for 5-10 mins and they harden and get less pancake-like.

Billy's Notes:  This is honestly a game changer, if you're doing low carb or keto!  Really bready and really good.  Toasts decently and is just a great substitute for bread when you can't have bread.  I figured around 0.25g of carbs in each of these.

Ideas for using this bread:

- grilled cheese
- BLT
- egg salad sandwiches
- sloppy joes
- toasted tomato sandwiches
- egg mcmuffins
