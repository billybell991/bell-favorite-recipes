## Ingredients

- ¼ cup butter or margarine
- 1-1/4 tsp seasoned salt
- 4-1/2 tsp Wooster sauce
- 8 cups Chex cereals
- 1 cup peanuts – of course!
- 1 cup small pretzels

## Instructions

Melt and mix seasonings in a large saucepan.  Dump in remaining ingredients and mix thoroughly.  250 degree oven for 1 hour stirring well every 20 minutes.
