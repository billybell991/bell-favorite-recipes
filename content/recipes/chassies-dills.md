## Ingredients

- 13 cups water
- 5 cups vinegar
- 1 cup pickling salt
- 1 tsp alum

## Instructions

Bring to a boil.  Put plenty of dill in jars and 2 garlic buds. Pour hot mixture over cukes to ½” from top of jar. Seal tightly, cool and store.
