## Ingredients

- 1-1/2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 Tbsp baking powder
- 1 Tbsp white sugar
- ¼ tsp salt
- 1 cup shredded old cheddar cheese
- 1 pkg onion soup mix
- 1 cup milk
- 1 egg
- ¼ cup oil

## Instructions

Stir together dry ingredients and stir in the cheese

Combine soup mix and milk. Let stand 5 min.

Beat egg, milk mixture and oil and add to dry ingredients mixing lightly just to combine.

Spoon into well-greased muffin cups and bake 375 degrees for 15- 20 minutes.

Serve hot!
