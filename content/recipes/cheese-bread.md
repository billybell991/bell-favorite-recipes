## Ingredients

- ½ cup chopped onion
- 1 Tbsp butter
- 1 egg
- ½ cup milk
- 1-1/2 cup Bisquick
- ½ cup grated cheese

## Instructions

Saute onion in butter. Mix egg in milk. Add Bisquick and grated cheese, then onion. Stir only until mixed well. Pour into buttered 1-1/2 quart pan. Top with more cheese or poppy seed if desired. Bake at 400 F. for 20 – 25 minutes.

Good warmed over; best when served warm.
