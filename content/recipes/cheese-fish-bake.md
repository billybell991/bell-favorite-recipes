- 1 lb fish fillets (Cod)
- 1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
- 1 can cream of mushroom soup
- Dash of paprika
- Salt and Pepper

Arrange fish in greased shallow baking dish and stir soup over fish. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and cheese. Bake at 375 degrees for 45 minutes.
