This recipe came from Pat Johnston, who is Yolande's Friend

- 1 cup Butter or Margarine
- 1 Pkg (1/2 lb.) Imperial Cheese (Red)
- Dash Cayenne Pepper
- Pinch of salt
- ½ tsp Worcestershire Sauce
- 1-1/2 cups Flour
- 4 cups Rice Krispies

Combine all ingredients by hand or with a pastry blender. Form into small balls. Place on cookie sheet and flatten with fork. Bake 15 – 20 minutes at 350 degrees.
