## Ingredients

- 3 cups frozen vegetable blend with broccoli, carrots, cauliflower
- 1 pkg Kraft Dinner Macaroni & Cheese
- ¾ cup skim milk
- ¼ cup Kraft Calorie-Wise Zesty Italian Dressing
- 2 tins (170 gr each) Clover Leaf Chunk White Tuna, drained
- 1 cup Kraft Double
- Cheddar Shredded Cheese Light, divided

## Instructions

PREHEAT oven to 375 F. Place vegetables in colander in sink. Cook Macaroni as directed on

package. Pour cooked Macaroni over frozen vegetables in colander to drain and quickly thaw

vegetables.

RETURN macaroni and vegetables to same saucepan. Add Cheese Sauce Mix, milk and

dressing; mix well. Add in tuna and ½ cup of the cheese; mix well. Spoon unto a 2-L round

casserole dish; cover with foil.

BAKE 35 minutes or until heated through. Uncover; top with remaining ½ cup cheese. Bake an

additional 3 to 4 minutes, or until cheese is melted.

Makes 4 servings, 1-3/4 cups (425 ml) each. Prep: 10 min. – Total 50 min.
