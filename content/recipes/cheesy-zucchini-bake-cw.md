## Ingredients

- 4-1/2 cups sliced zucchini
- 2 – 3 tbsp oil       salt & pepper to taste
- 1 large onion, chopped
- 2 tbsp minced garlic
- 1 10-3/4 oz tin tomato puree
- 1 6-oz tin tomato paste
- 3 tbsp sugar
- 1 tsp Italian seasoning
- 1 tsp dried basil
- 2 2-1/4 oz tins of drained sliced ripe olives
- 3 cups (12 oz) shredded mozzarella cheese      6 eggs, lightly beaten
- 1-1/2 cups grated Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

In a large skillet, sauté zucchini in oil until tender. Sprinkle with salt and pepper; stir; transfer to an ungreased 13 x 9 x 2 baking dish.
In same skillet, sauté onion until crisp-tender. Add garlic; sauté 3 minutes longer. Stir in tomato puree, tomato paste, sugar, Italian seasoning and basil. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat. Simmer, uncovered, for 10-15 minutes or until slightly thickened. Stir in olives. Pour over zucchini. Sprinkle with mozzarella.
Combine the eggs and Parmesan cheese; pour over zucchini. Bake, uncovered, at 375 degrees for 25-30 minutes or until a knife inserted near he center comes out clean. Let the dishstand for 15 minutes before serving.
YIELD: 12 to 16 servings.
