- 1/2 to 1 cup icing sugar
- 250 gr. Pkg cream cheese
- 1 envelope Dream Whip or 1 cup whipping cream
- 1 tin cherry pie filling
- 1 graham crumb crust

Bake crumb crust at 350 degrees for 10 minutes.  Cream cheese and sugar till nice and soft.  Make Dream Whip and combine with cheese.  Cover graham crust.  Put cherries on top.  Leave in refrigerator for 24 hours.
