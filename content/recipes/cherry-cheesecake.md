Friends of the Bride’s Family

- 2 cups graham crumbs
- ½ cup melted butter
- 2 tbsp brown sugar
- 1-8 oz pkg of Philadelphia cream cheese
- 1 large pkg of Dream Whip
- 1 cup white sugar
- 1 can cherry pie filling

Mix graham crumbs, melted butter and brown sugar together. Press into an 8x12 inch pan.

Mix Dream Whip according to directions on package. Add cream cheese and white sugar to whipped Dream Whip. Spread over graham crumbs. Top with cherry pie filling.

Refrigerate for 2 hours or more before serving.
