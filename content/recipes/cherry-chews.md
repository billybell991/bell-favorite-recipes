Aunt and Uncle of the Bride

Bottom:

- l cup sifted flour
- 1 cup rolled oats
- 1 cup brown sugar
- ¼  tsp salt
- ½ cup butter

Mix flour, oats, brown sugar, soda and salt. Add butter and mix until crumbly, first with a fork, then with fingers. Place mixture into bottom of a greased 9x13 inch pan. Bake for 10 minutes.

Filling:

- 2 eggs
- 1 cup brown sugar
- ½ tsp almond extract
- 2 tbsp flour
- 1 tsp baking powder
- ½ tsp salt
- 1 cup coconut
- ½ cup pecan halves
- 1 cup well drained maraschino cherries, halved

Beat eggs. Stir in brown sugar, almond flavoring, flour, baking powder, salt, coconut, and maraschino cherries and stir to blend. Pour over first mixture and spread evenly. Sprinkle with pecans. Return to oven and bake 25 minutes until lightly browned. Ice with Cherry Almond icing. Cut into squares or bars to serve. Makes 2 dozen 1½x2 inch bars.

Cherry Almond Icing:

## Ingredients

- 3 tbsp soft butter (or more)
- 2 cups sifted icing sugar
- 2 tbsp maraschino cherry juice
- ¼  tsp almond extract

## Instructions

Blend butter and sugar. Add cherry juice and almond extract, using enough juice to make icing  easy to spread. Stir until smooth.
