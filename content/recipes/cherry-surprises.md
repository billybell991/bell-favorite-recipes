## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- ½ cup coconut
- 1-1/2 cup icing sugar
- 1 tsp milk
- 1 tsp vanilla Pinch of salt
- Cherries

## Instructions

Cream butter, mix all ingredients and make into balls around cherries. Roll in graham wafers.

NOTE: If using maraschino cherries, drain them well and dry them or icing will stick to them.
