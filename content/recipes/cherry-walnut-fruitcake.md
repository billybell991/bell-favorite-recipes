This fruitcake is an easy- to-make classic holiday offering. If you like, make it ahead, wrap and freeze for entertaining and gift-giving.

## Ingredients

- 2-1/2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 2 eggs, lightly beaten
- 1 jar (750 ml) mincemeat
- 1 tin (300 ml) sweetened condensed milk
- 2 cups chopped mixed glace fruit
- 1 cup chopped walnuts
- Whole glace cherries

## Instructions

Grease two 9 x 5 loaf pans – set aside.

In bowl, stir together flour and baking soda. In separate large bowl, combine eggs, mincemeat, condensed milk, chopped fruit and walnuts. Add flour mixture; mix well. Divide batter between prepared pans.

Bake in 300 F oven for 80 to 85 minutes or until cake tester inserted in center comes out clean. Let cool in pans on racks for 15 minutes. Remove from pans, let cool completely. Garnish with glace cherries.

Makes 2 cakes – 16 to 20 servings.
