## Ingredients

- 1-1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
- 1 cup chocolate chips
- 1 cup butterscotch chips
- 1 cup chopped walnuts
- 11-oz sweetened condensed milk

## Instructions

Measure all ingredients.  Stir together well.  Grease a 9 x 9 pan.  Smooth with back of spoon.  Bake for 30 – 35 minutes in a 350 degree oven.  Cool for 4 – 5 minutes.  Cut into 36 squares

NOTE:  I tried this in a well-greased pan and it was a real struggle to get out of the pan. The next time I will grease the pan, line it with tin foil and grease this very generously and keep my fingers crossed that it will come out easy.
