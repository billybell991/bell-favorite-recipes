## Ingredients

- 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
- ¼ cup parmesan cheese
- 2 cups tomato sauce (or leftover spaghetti sauce)
- ½ - 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

## Instructions

Put ½ cup of sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.  Place the chicken on top of the sauce and pour remaining sauce over chicken.

Sprinkle parmesan cheese over the sauce and chicken and place in a preheated 350 degree oven. Bake for about 1 ½ hours or until chicken is tender when poked with a fork.

In the last 15 minutes, sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top and let it melt. Serve with egg noodles or even spaghetti. Serves 4.
