## Ingredients

- 1 tin cream of chicken soup
- 1 cup cubed cooked chicken
- ½ cup milk or water
- Mashed potatoes

## Instructions

Place ingredients in a pan. Then cover with mashed potatoes.  Heat in oven at 325 degrees for 30 minutes.
