## Ingredients

Chicken:

- 3 large chicken breasts
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1/4 tsp chili powder
- 1/4 tsp garlic powder
- 1/4 tsp cumin
- 1/4 tsp salt

Filling:

- 1 block cream cheese
- 3 tbsp sour cream
- 1/3 cup salso
- 2 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp chili powder
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 and a 1/2 cups of shredded cheese
- 16-18 small flour tortillas

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
2. Mix together chili powder, garlic powder, cumin, and salt. Drizzle chicken with olive oil and add seasoning mix to both sides of each chicken breast
3. Cook the chicken for 20-25 minutes or until fully cooked (internal temp of 165 Fahrenheit). Shred once cooked using 2 forks.
4. Turn the oven up to 425 degrees Fahrenheit
5. Mix cream cheese, sour cream, salsa, cumin, chili powder, garlic powder, salt, cheeses, and shredded chicken together in a bowl.
6. Add a few tablespoons to each tortilla and roll them up.
7. Place on a parchment lined baking tray or in the airfryer until golden brown (about 10-15 minutes).
