Submitted by Christine Shank

Cousin of the Bride

## Ingredients

- 1 boiling fowl (5 ½ - 6 lbs)
- a few stalks of celery, chopped
- 3 small onions, chopped
- 1 quart milk
- ¼ lb butter
- 4 ozs hard flour
- 2 cups chicken stock
- 1 lb spaghetti
- 1 lb mushrooms
- ½ cup parmesan cheese
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

Boil fowl. Take meat from bones and put stalks of celery, onions and salt and pepper to taste into chicken stock. Simmer.

Make roux of butter and flour in saucepan. Bring milk to boiling point in double boiler. Thicken with roux. Add 2 cups strained chicken stock.

In separate pot, boil spaghetti in salt water until cooked. Wash in cold water. In separate pan, sauté washed and sliced mushrooms.

Grease good-sized casserole dish with butter and fill as follows in layers: one of spaghetti, one of mushrooms, one of chicken meat, one of strained celery/onions, one of white sauce. Repeat until casserole is filled, ending with white sauce with a topping of parmesan cheese (ordinarily, 2 layers of everything, ½ and ½).

Bake in 375-400 degree oven until top is golden brown. Can be made ahead and frozen.
