- 3-1/2 cup water
- 1 cup sliced carrot
- 1 Tbsp chicken-flavored bouillon granules
- 1 (8-oz)can mushroom stems and pieces,
- 1 (14-1/2 oz) can no-salt added whole drained tomatoes, undrained and chopped
- 1 cup diced zucchini
- ¼ cup dried minced onion
- 1 cup cooked chicken breast (skinned before cooking and cooked without salt or fat)
- 1 tsp dried basil
- 1 tsp paprika
- ¾ tsp minced garlic
- 2 Tbsp dry red wine

Combine first 7 ingredients in a Dutch oven. Bring to a boil; cover, reduce heat, and simmer 10 minutes. Add carrot; cover and simmer 10 minutes. Add mushrooms and remaining ingredients; simmer, uncovered, 8 minutes. Yield: 7 (1 cup) servings.

Diabetic Exchange per Serving: 1 very lean meat, 2 vegetable.

Nutritional Analysis per Serving: Calories 83, Carbohydrate 10.5 g, Protein 7.5 g, Fat 1.6 g, Cholesterol 16 mg, Fiber 1.2 g, Sodium 492 mg
