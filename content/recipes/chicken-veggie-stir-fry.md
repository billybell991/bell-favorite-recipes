## Instructions

Prep. Time 15 min.   -  Cooking Time – 20 min.

2 Tbsp cornstarch	1 lb skinless boneless chicken, cut into
1 tin condensed chicken broth	     strips
1 Tbsp soya sauce	1 onion, sliced
¼ tsp ground ginger	5 cups cut-up veggies (broccoli, carrots
2 Tbsp vegetable oil	   mushroom, celery & green onion)

Stir together cornstarch, broth, soya sauce and Ginger – set aside.
In large skillet, heat half of the oil. Stir fry chicken with onion till browned. Remove from skillet; season lightly with salt and pepper and set aside.
In same skillet, heat remaining oil. Stir fry veggies until tender crisp.
Add reserved cornstarch mix. Cook until mixture boils and thickens, stirring constantly. Return chicken to skillet. Heat thru. Serve over rice, if desired.

Makes 4 servings.
