- 1 lb hamburg
- 1 cup uncooked rice
- 1-1/2 tsp salt
- ¼ tsp pepper
- 1 tsp chili powder
- 2 minced onions
- 1 28-oz tin canned tomatoes
- 1-1/4 cup water
- 1 tin mushroom (optional)

Mix well and bake at 350 degrees F. for 2 hours, covered.
