Preparation: 10 minutes Baking time: 30 minutes

## Ingredients

- 2-1/2 lb chicken wings
- ½ cup dry bread crumbs
- ¼ cup flour
- 1 Old El Paso Chili Seasoning Mix (35gr pkg)

## Instructions

Heat oven to 350 degrees. Line a baking sheet with foil, grease lightly. With a sharp knife, remove wing tips, discard. Cut through wings at joint to yield two pieces each. Place bread crumbs, flour and chili seasoning mix in large plastic bag, shake to combine. Shake chicken wings in mix to coat thoroughly.

Place wings on baking sheet and bake 25 to 30 minutes or until cooked through.

Serve with Mexi-Veggie dip if desired.

Makes about 30 wings.
