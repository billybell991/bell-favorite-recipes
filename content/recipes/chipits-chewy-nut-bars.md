## Ingredients

- ½ cup margarine
- 2 cups packed brown sugar
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 2 eggs
- 1 ½ cup all purpose flour
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 cup semi-sweet Chipits
- 1 cup coarsely chopped nuts

## Instructions

Melt margarine in a large pot. Remove from heat. Add sugar and vanilla stirring until well blended. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir together flour, baking powder and salt. Add to the pot and mix well.

Spread in a lightly greased 9x13 inch rectangular baking pan. Sprinkle Chipits and nuts over surface of cookie base. Press down lightly.

Bake at 350 degrees for 35-40 minutes. Cool thoroughly before cutting into bars.
