## Ingredients

- Melt 6-oz pig (1 cup) Chipits chocolate chips and 6-oz pkg (1 cup) Chipits butterscotch chips in a 1-1/2 quart bowl over hot (not boiling) water. Stir in 1 cup chow mein noodles and 1 cup peanuts.

## Instructions

Drop with 2 teaspoons onto wax paper. Chill until firm.  Makes 2 dozen.

For an interesting variation, substitute (for noodles) one cup broken thin pretzel sticks, corn flakes or rice crispies.
