## Ingredients

- Soda crackers
- 1-1/2 cups chocolate chips
- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup chopped almonds
- 1 cup brown sugar

## Instructions

Grease cookie sheet.  Line bottom of sheet with soda crackers.  Boil butter and brown sugar for 5 minutes;  keep stirring.  Pour hot mixture over crackers.  Bake in oven for 5 minutes at 375 degrees.  Pour chocolate chips and almonds on top of hot crackers.  Refrigerate to harden.  Break into pieces.
ENJOY!
