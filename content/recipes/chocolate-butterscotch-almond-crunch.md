## Ingredients

- 1 ¾ cup flour
- ¾ cup white sugar
- ¾ cup brown sugar
- 1 cup margarine or butter
- 2 egg yolks
- ½ tsp vanilla
- 1 pkg milk chocolate chips
- 1 cup slivered almonds

## Instructions

Combine flour and sugars. Mix margarine in until blended. Add egg yolk and vanilla. Mix until smooth.

Press with floured fingers onto a 10x15 inch cookie sheet with sides. Bake at 350 degrees for 13-18 minutes or until light golden.

Cool slightly, about 20 minutes. Spread melted chocolate over top. Sprinkle almonds on top.

Cool until set. Cut into squares or triangles.
