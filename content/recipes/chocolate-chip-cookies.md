## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- ¼ cup white sugar
- ¾ cup brown sugar
- 1 cup margarine or butter
- 2 cups chocolate chips
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1 pkg vanilla or chocolate instant pudding
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 2-1/4 cups flour
- Walnuts (optional)

## Instructions

Mix egg, butter, sugars, vanilla.

Add instant pudding and the rest of the dry ingredients.

Bake for 10 minutes at 375 degree F. oven.
