## Ingredients

- 1 container of chocolate icing
- 1 box of graham crackers
- 1 pkg of instant vanilla pudding
- ½ cup of sugar
- 500 mL whipping cream

## Instructions

Prepare vanilla pudding according to the directions on the package. Set aside.

Whip up whipping cream, slowly adding sugar until peaks form. Fold whipping cream into vanilla pudding.

Line an 8x10 inch baking dish with graham crackers. Pour in half of the pudding mixture. Cover pudding mixture with another layer of graham crackers. Pour second half of pudding mixture over graham crackers. Line mixture with graham crackers.

Let chill in the fridge for 20-30 minutes. Ice top with chocolate icing. It helps to warm up icing in microwave and then pour and spread over graham crackers.

Chill before serving.
