## Ingredients

- 8 oz white chocolate, divided
- 8 oz semi-sweet chocolate chips
- ½ cup each salted peanuts,
- mini marshmallows,
- crispy rice cereal,
- coconut,
- red & green cherries

## Instructions

In a heavy saucepan or top or a double boiler, melt 6 oz white chocolate and all the chocolate chips.  Stir in peanuts, marshmallows and cereal.  Pour onto a greased 10”pizza pan or a 10” circle of cardboard covered with foil.  Spread to even out top.  Sprinkle with coconut.  Top with cherries.  Melt remaining white chocolate and drizzle over pizza.  Chill.

Yield: 16  - 20 servings.
