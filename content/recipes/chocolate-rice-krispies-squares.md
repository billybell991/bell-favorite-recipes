## Ingredients

- 1/3 cup margarine
- l/2 cup cocoa
- 1/3 cup honey
- 3 cups small marshmallows
- 4 cups rice krispies
- l cup peanuts (optional)

## Instructions

Melt margarine in large pan, add honey, cocoa and marshmallows.  Stir till marshmallows are melted.  Remove from heat and add rice krispies and peanuts.  Press into a buttered 9”square pan.
