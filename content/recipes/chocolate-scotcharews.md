## Ingredients

- 1 cup white sugar
- 1 cup peanut butter
- 1 cup corn syrup

## Instructions

Mix and melt together in a large deep pan, then remove from stove.  Add six cups Rice Krispies gradually and mix.  Spread in a buttered 8” x 8”pan.  Cool.

MELT  TOGETHER;:
1 6-oz pkg chocolate chips	1 6-oz pkg butterscotch chips

Spread on top of above mixture and let cool.  Cut in squares.  Kids just love it.
