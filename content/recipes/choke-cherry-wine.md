## Ingredients

- 15 cups of cherries to 1 gallon of hot water
- Acid Blend
- 2 tsp per gallon
- Grape Tannin
- ¼ tsp per gallon
- Pectic Enzyme
- ½ tsp per gallon
- Yeast Nutrient
- ½ tsp per gallon
- Sugar
- 3 lbs per gallon   -  Stir well.
- Wine Yeast
- 1 pkg per 1 to 5 gallons

## Instructions

Cool mixture and add 1 pkg of wine yeast.  Stir daily for two weeks.  After two weeks, extract liquid.
NOTE:  Use same cherries to make a second brew.  (Make half the amount of first batch.)

When brew stops working (fermenting), check for sweetness and if too dry, add more sugar (1 cup or so per 5 gallons).  Brew will start to work again.

When this stops working, check again and repeat same procedure as before.

NOTE:  It is necessary to rack the brew several times in order to clarify it.
