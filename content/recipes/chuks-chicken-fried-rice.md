## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- 2 green peppers
- 2 onions
- 2 cups celery
- mushroom pieces and stems
- 1/2 cup soya sauce
- 2 tins consommé soup
- 4 cups minute rice

## Instructions

Saute diced green peppers, chopped onions, chopped celery, mushroom in butter til onions are

transparent. Add soya sauce and soup. Bring this mixture to a boil and stir in minute rice.

Cover and cook on low heat for 1/2 hour.

Cooked chicken chunks, or ham, or egg can be added when rice is added.
