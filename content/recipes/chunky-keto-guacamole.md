## Ingredients

- 3 Medium roughly chopped avocados
- 1/4 cup finely chopped red onion
- 1/4 cup roughly chopped cilantro
- 3 heaped tablespoons lime juice
- 1/4 cup chopped roma tomatoes
- 1/2 tablespoon finely minced jalapeno
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon pepper

## Instructions

### INSTRUCTIONS

1. Add all ingredients to a large bowl and stir well to combine. Adjust seasoning if desired.
