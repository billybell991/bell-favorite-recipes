Cookie Dough:

- 1/2 cup softened butter
- 1/2 cup vegetable oil
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 2 tsp cinnamon
- 1/2 tsp baking soda
- 1/2 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 2 and 1/2 cups flour

Streusel Topping:

- 1/4 cup softened butter
- 1/4 cup brown sugar
- 1/3-1/2 cup flour (depending on the consistency once mixed)
- Pinch of salt

Icing:

- 1/2 cup powdered sugar
- 1 tbsp milk
- splash of vanilla

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
2. Combine butter, oil, and brown sugar and mix for a few minutes until light and fluffy. Add egg and vanilla and mix again. Add remaining ingredients and mix until dough forms
3. For streusel, combine ingredients and mix. If the mixture is too sticky and not crumbly, add more flour.
4. Scoop 1/4 cup of dough and place on a tray with parchment paper. Make a small indent on the top of the dough ball and add streusel topping into the dent.
5. Bake for 12-15 minutes. While baking make the icing, combine all ingredients and whisk until a runny icing/glaze forms.
6. Add icing once cookies are cooled :)
