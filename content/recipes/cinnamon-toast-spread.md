## Ingredients

- 2 cups margarine
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 2 tbsp cinnamon

## Instructions

Stir in a large bowl.  Spread on hot toast.

NOTES:  This really tastes like the store-bought stuff!  I'd give it a 4.5 out of 5.
