## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cups of ground coconut
- 1/3 cup of shredded coconut
- 1 2/3 cups of powdered sugar
- 2 large egg whites (approx. 1/4 cup)
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1/3 cup neutral oil (such as sunflower or canola)

## Instructions

1. In a bowl, combine the finely ground coconut and powdered sugar.

2. Add the egg whites directly to the mixture. There is no need to whisk them beforehand. Mix until a soft, smooth dough forms.

3. Heat the oil until hot (not smoking).

4. Gradually pour the hot oil into the dough while mixing continuously, allowing the oil to fully absorb into the mixture.

5. Once the dough is uniform, add the salt and lemon juice. Mix briefly until well combined.

6. Fold in the shredded (unground) coconut to improve the texture of the cookies.

7. Let the dough rest at room temperature for 30 minutes. This resting time allows the coconut to absorb moisture and helps the dough firm up slightly, preventing the cookies from spreading too much during baking.

8. Transfer the dough to a piping bag. Pipe small round mounds onto a lined baking tray, leaving some space between them.

9. Bake in a preheated oven at 300°F (150°C) for about 20 minutes, or until the cookies are lightly golden on top.
