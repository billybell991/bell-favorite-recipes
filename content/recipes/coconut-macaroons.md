## Ingredients

- 3 pkg (200 gr ea) flaked coconut
- 1 can (300 ml) Eagle Brand Milk
- 2 tsp vanilla extract
- 1-1/2 tsp almond extract

## Instructions

1. Combine coconut, Eagle Brand, vanilla and almond extracts; mix well.

2. Drop by rounded spoonfuls onto well-greased cookie sheets.

3. Bake 12 at a time on middle rack of preheated 325 F. oven, 10 – 12 minutes or until browned around the edges. Remove immediately to wire racks, cool completely.

Makes about 4 dozen.

For Chocolate Macaroons; Omit almond extract. Add 4 squares (1 oz or 28 grams each) unsweetened chocolate, melted. Proceed as above.

For Chocolate Chip Macaroons; Omit almond extract. Add 1 cup (250 ml) mini chocolate chips. Proceed as above.
