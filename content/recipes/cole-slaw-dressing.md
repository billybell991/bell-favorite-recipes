## Instructions

If you have a microwave and need a nice potato salad or cole slaw dressing in a hurry, here is one.  In a medium glass bowl, with wire whip or rotary beater, thoroughly combine

½ cup water	1/3 cup vinegar
¼ to 1/3 cup sugar	1 egg
1 tbsp flour	½ tsp salt
½ tsp dry mustard

Heat on high 4 – 5 minutes until mixture is thickened, stirring twice; stir in

1 tbsp butter or margarine.
