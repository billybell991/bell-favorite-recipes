## Ingredients

- 4 qts finely shredded cabbage
- 1 tbsp coarse salt (salad salt)
- 1 cup peeled and shredded carrot
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- ½ cup commercial sour cream
- 1 tsp celery salt
- 1 tbsp vinegar
- Salt and pepper
- 3 tsp sugar

## Instructions

In a large colander over a large bowl, layer the shredded cabbage and coarse salt.  Place a plate over the cabbage and weight it down with a heavy object.  Allow cabbage to stand at room temperature for at least 2 hours.  Press out as much moisture as possible and place cabbage and the shredded carrot in a large bowl.  In a small bowl blend the mayonnaise, sour cream, celery salt, sugar and vinegar.  Add the mayonnaise mixture to the cabbage and stir until well combined.  Chill for several hours or overnight.  Taste the coleslaw after it has been chilled and adjust the seasoning if necessary.

Makes 2 – 3 quarts.
