## Ingredients

- 1 7-oz tin crab meat
- 1 pkg. (250 gr) cream cheese
- 1 tsp lemon juice
- Sprinkling of parsley

## Instructions

Mix all ingredients.  Seafood sauce on top for flavor and color (optional)
