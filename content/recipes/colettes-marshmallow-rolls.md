## Ingredients

- 1-1/3 cup small marshmallows
- 3-1/4 cup graham crumbs
- ½ cup red cherries (chopped)
- ½ cup green cherries (chopped)
- ½ cup chopped walnuts
- 1 can Eagle Brand

## Instructions

Mix well and roll into balls or shape into rolls for slicing.
