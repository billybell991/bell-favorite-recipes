## Ingredients

- 1 cup peanut butter (plain or chunky)
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 egg

## Instructions

Mix egg and sugar together. Add peanut butter. Spoon onto greased cookie sheet. Press with a fork. Use small teaspoon and note that they really flatten and spread.

Bake at 350 F. for l0 minutes.
