## Ingredients

- 1 cup sour cream
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 1 pkg frozen chopped spinach
- 1 pkg Knorr vegetable soup mix

## Instructions

Drain spinach well, then mix all ingredients together.  If desired, put dip into pumpernickel bread.
