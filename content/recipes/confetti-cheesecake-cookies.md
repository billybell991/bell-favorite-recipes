## Ingredients

Cream Cheese Filling:

- 1 block of cream cheese
- 1/2 cup powdered sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Cookie Dough:

- 1 cup butter or margarine
- 1 and a 1/2 cups granulated sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 and a 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 2 and 3/4 cups all purpose flour
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/4 cup sprinkles

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
2. Mix room temp cream cheese, powdered sugar, and vanilla together in a bowl
3. Scoop 1 tablespoon balls of the mixture onto parchment paper and chill in freezer while preparing cookie dough
4. Beat butter and sugar together for about 1-2 minutes. Once combined, mix in egg and vanilla
5. Fold in flour, baking powder, and baking soda. Mix in sprinkles.
6. Scoop 1/3 cup of dough, flatten in palm of hand and place a cream cheese ball in the middle. Roll the dough around the cream cheese ball.
7. Roll cookie dough ball in white sugar (optional)
8. Place 6 to a tray with lots of room for expansion
9. Cook 10 minutes, check them, 3 more minutes, check them, 3 more minutes
10. Allow to cool ON TRAY for 10 minutes
