Cousins of the Bride

- 3 ½ lbs of skinless chicken (cut up 2 whole chickens)
- 1 ½ cup rice
- 1-540 mL can black-eyed peas
- 1-400 mL can coconut milk
- 3 tbsp butter
- 3 tbsp soya sauce
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- 1 tsp basil
- 3 cloves of garlic, minced
- 1 large onion, minced
- pinch of black pepper
- salt to taste

Wash chicken and cut into pieces (leave bones in). Place chicken in a large bowl. Blend in onion, garlic, soya sauce, basil, pepper and salt.

Heat oil in a stewing pot and add seasoned chicken. Stir occasionally until all pieces are browned. Stir in dry rice and cook for 3 minutes. Then, add peas, coconut milk and enough water to cover the entire mixture.

Simmer until rice is loose and stir in butter. Serve with your favorite pepper sauce.
