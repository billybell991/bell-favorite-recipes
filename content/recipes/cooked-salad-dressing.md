## Ingredients

- 2 heaping tsp cornstarch
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup white sugar
- 1 cup vinegar and 4 tsp water
- 1 level tsp dry mustard
- 1 tsp salt
- 4 tbsp canned milk

## Instructions

Combine above in double boiler and cook.  After it starts to thicken, boil for 5 minutes.  Chill before using.
