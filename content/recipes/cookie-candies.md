Cousins of the Groom

- 1 cup soft butter
- ¾ cup icing sugar
- 1 tbsp vanilla
- 2 cups flour & ¾ tsp salt sifted together
- ½ cup rolled oats
- ½ cup semi sweet chocolate pieces
- ½ cup milk
- sprinkles of your choice

Mix butter, sugar and vanilla. Cream well. Add flour mixture (sifted) and rolled oats. With hands, make into balls, roll into logs or use cookie cutters. Cook at 325 degrees for 20 minutes.

Melt chocolate and milk. Stir until smooth. Dip cookies in chocolate and add sprinkles to decorate.

Freezes well.
