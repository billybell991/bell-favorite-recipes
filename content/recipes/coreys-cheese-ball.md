## Ingredients

- 1 250 gr pkg cream cheese
- ½ jar Cheez Whiz cheese
- 1 cup shredded cheese
- 1-1/2 tsp onion flakes
- 1-1/2 tsp Worcestershire sauce

## Instructions

Mix well. Refrigerate till firm. Makes 2 balls. Roll in parsley flakes or crushed walnuts.
