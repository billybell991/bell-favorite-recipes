## Ingredients

- 2 cups white sugar
- 1 cup water
- ½ tsp garlic
- 5 Tbsp soya sauce

## Instructions

Wash and place wings in pan. Melt sugar in water, Add garlic and soya sauce. Marinade for 1 hour. Bake uncovered at 350 degrees for 2-1/2 to 3 hours till sauce is thick and bubbly.

Stir every ½ hour.
