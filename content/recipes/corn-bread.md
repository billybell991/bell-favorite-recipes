- 1 cup flour, sifted
- ½ cup yellow corn meal
- 3 tsp baking powder
- 1 cup milk
- ½ tsp salt
- 1 egg, beaten
- ½ cup sugar
- 1 Tbsp melted shortening

Mix and sift flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. Stir in corn meal. Add milk to beaten egg and stir into first mixture. Add shortening and blend. Turn into shallow greased 8-inch pan. Bake at 400 degrees for 20 minutes.
