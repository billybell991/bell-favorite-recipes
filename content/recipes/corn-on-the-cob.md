## Ingredients

On Rack

- 4 Ears Corn on the Cob (or more)
- 1 cup Water

In Pot

- 4 Ears Corn on the Cob (or more)
- 1 cup Water
- 1/2 cup Milk
- 1 TablespoonSugar
- 2 Tablespoons Butter
- 1 teaspoonSea Salt

Instructions

On Rack

## Instructions

1. Add one cup of water to the Pressure Cooker cooking pot and place a trivet on top.  Lay corn on trivet.
2. Lock on lid and close Pressure Valve.  Cook on Steam (High Pressure) for 1 minutes. If frozen, cook for 0-1 minute.  After 5 minutes, do a Quick Release.

In Pot for Butter Flavor

1. Add corn, water, milk, sugar, salt and butter to Pressure Cooker cooking pot.
2. Lock on lid and close Pressure Valve.  Cook on High Pressure for 1 minute. If frozen, cook for 0-1 minute.  Allow a Natural Pressure Release.  The longer the corn sits in the liquid, the more flavor is infused.
