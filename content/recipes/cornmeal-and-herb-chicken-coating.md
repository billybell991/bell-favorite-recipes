## Ingredients

- 1 cup dry bread crumbs
- 1 cup powdered milk
- 1 cup cornmeal
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 3 tbsp salt
- 1 tbsp dried thyme
- 1 tsp pepper
- 1 tsp garlic salt
- 1 tsp celery salt
- 1 tsp onion salt
- 1 tsp cried oregano

## Instructions

Combine all the ingredients.  To use, place 1 cup mixture in a plastic bag.  Shake chicken pieces until coated.  Place on a baking pan and bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.  Store unused mixture in an airtight container.

Yield:  Recipe makes enough coating for three chickens, each 2-1/2 to 3 lbs.
