Cousins of the Groom

- 8 oz light cream cheese, softened
- 2 tbsp grated onion
- 4.2 oz canned crab meat, drained and cartilage removed
- 2 tbsp fine bread crumbs
- 1/4  tsp salt
- 1/16 tsp garlic powder
- 1 lb. pkg wonton wrappers
- cooking oil for deep frying

Mash cream cheese in a bowl. Work in onion, crab meat, bread crumbs, salt and garlic powder.

Place 1 tsp of mixture in center of each wonton wrapper. Dampen edges of wonton with water. Fold one corner at a time to centre, pressing to seal well. Cover with damp towel until ready to cook.

Deep fry in hot 375 degree cooking oil for about 2 minutes until browned. Drain on paper towel. Cool and refrigerate in covered container.

To serve, arrange on ungreased baking sheet. Heat in 450 degree oven for about 4 minutes until hot.

Makes 54 puffs.
