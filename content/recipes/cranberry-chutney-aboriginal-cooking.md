## Ingredients

- 1 16-oz package cranberries
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 cup water
- 1 cup orange juice
- 1 cup raisins
- 1 medium chopped granny smith apple
- 1 Tbsp ground ginger
- ¼ tsp ground cloves
- ½ tsp ground nutmeg

## Instructions

In a 3-quart saucepan, combine cranberries, water, raisins and shopped apple. Bring mixture to a boil. Reduce heat to simmer. Cook, covered for 15 minutes or until cranberries pop.

Stir in sugar, orange juice, ginger, nutmeg and cloves. Cover and simmer for 30 minutes.

Ladle into jars or freezer containers. Refrigerate or freeze until ready to use.
