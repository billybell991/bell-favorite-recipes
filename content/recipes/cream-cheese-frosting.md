## Ingredients

- 1 4-oz pkg cream cheese
- 2 cups icing sugar
- 2 tbsp milk
- ¼ tsp salt

## Instructions

In beater bowl beat cheese until creamy then add sugar and salt and beat until smooth, adding milk slowly until of smooth almost runny spreading consistency.
