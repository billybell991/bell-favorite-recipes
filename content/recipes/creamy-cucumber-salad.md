## Ingredients

- 2 long English cucumbers
- Optional: 1/3 cup of sliced onion
- 1/2 cup of sour cream (or plain Greek yogurt)
- 3 Tablespoons of Mayonnaise
- 1/4 Tsp of fresh dill (chopped)
- 3 Tablespoons of white vinegar
- 1/2 Teaspoon of white sugar
- Salt to Taste

## Instructions

1. Peel the cucumbers and cut in half lengthwise. Scrape out the seeds if desired and slice into ¼″ slices.
2. Combine all dressing ingredients and toss with cucumbers.
3. Refrigerate 1 hour before serving.
