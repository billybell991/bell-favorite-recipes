## Ingredients

- 1 cup ketchup
- ½ cup reduced-fat mayonnaise
- 3 Tbsp cider vinegar
- 3 Tbsp honey
- 2 Tbsp water
- 1 Tbsp olive or canola oil
- 1 tsp lemon juice
- ½ tsp ground mustard
- ¼ tsp salt
- Salad greens & vegetables of your choice

## Instructions

In a blender or food processor, combine the first nine ingredients; cover and process until blended. Serve over salad. Refrigerate leftovers. Yield: 1-3/4 cups

Nutritional Analysis: One serving (2 Tbsp dressing) equals 70 calories, 4 g fat (1 g saturated fat), 3 mg cholesterol, 318 mg sodium, 10 g carbohydrate, trace fiber, trace protein.

Diabetic Exchanges: ½ starch, ½ fat
