## Ingredients

- 1-1/2 cup brown sugar
- 2-1/2 cups Rice Krispies
- 2//3 cup corn syrup
- 1 cup coconut
- 2/3 cup peanut butter
- ½ cup chopped walnuts

## Instructions

Combine brown sugar, corn syrup and peanut butter over low heat.  Remove and add remaining ingredients.  Make small balls and put them on wax paper.  Chill for ½ hour.
