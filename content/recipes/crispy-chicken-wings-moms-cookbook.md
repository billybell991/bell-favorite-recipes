## Ingredients

- 2 lbs chicken wings
- ½ cup melted butter or margarine
- ¼ tsp garlic powder
- 2 tbsp chopped parsley
- 1 cup dry bread crumbs
- ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- ½ tsp salt

## Instructions

Cut chicken wings in two pieces, discarding the tips.  In a small shallow bowl, combine butter and garlic powder.  In another bowl, combine remaining ingredients.  Dip chicken into butter mixture, then into crumb mixture.  Place on greased cookie sheet.  Bake at 350 degrees for 50 – 60 minutes or until done.

Yield:  about 20 appetizers.
