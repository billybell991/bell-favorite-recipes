## Ingredients

- ¼ cup plus 2 Tbsp frozen egg substitute, thawed
- ¼ tsp garlic powder
- ¼ tsp salt
- 1 Tbsp water
- ¼ tsp pepper
- 1 cup crispy rice cereal, crushed
- 1 (3-lb) broiler-fryer, cut up and skinned
- 1/3 cup toasted wheat germ
- ¼ all-purpose flour
- 1 Tbsp instant minced onion Vegetable cooking spray
- ½ tsp salt-free herb and spice blend

## Instructions

Combine egg substitute and water in a shallow dish. Combine cereal and next 6 ingredients in a shallow dish. Place chicken and flour in a large zip-top plastic bag; seal bag, and shake until chicken is well coated. Dip chicken in egg substitute mixture; dredge in cereal mixture. Place chicken on rach of a broiler pan coated with cooking spray. Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees for 1 hour or until chicken is tender.

Yield: 6 servings. Diabetic Exchange per Serving: 3 very lean meat, 1 starch. Nutritional Analysis per Serving: Calories 209, Carbohydrate 14.0 g, Protein 27.3 g, Fat 4.2 g, Cholesterol 76 mg, Fiber 1.3 g, Sodium 253 mg.
