## Instructions

Prep: 10 min.   Total: 35 minutes

2 pkg (300 g each) frozen chopped spinach,	1 tub (250 g) Philadelphia Chives & Onion
thawed and well drained	    Spreadable Cream Cheese
½ cup Draft Rancher’s Choice Dressing	2 eggs, lightly beaten
1-1/2 cups Draft Double Cheddar Shreds, 	1 cup crushed Ritz Crackers, divided
divided

PREHEAT oven to 375 F. Mix spinach, cream cheese spread, dressing, eggs and ¾ cup of the shredded cheese in large bowl. Stir in ½ cup of the crushed crackers.

SPOON spinach mixture evenly into greased 2-L ovenproof casserole dish. Sprinkle with remaining ½ cup crushed crackers and remaining ¾ cup shredded cheese.

BAKE 20 to 5 minutes or until heated through and cheese on top is melted.

Makes 12 servings.
To thaw spinach, place in refrigerator overnight, or unwrap; place in microwaveable bowl and thaw in microwave as directed on package. Be sure to squeeze well after thawing to remove as much water as possible.
