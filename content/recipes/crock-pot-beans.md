Submitted by Tom Alexander

Friend of the Bride and Groom

- 3 cups beans
- 6 cups water
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 onion, chopped
- ½ lb green bacon or regular bacon (I use a chunk of salt pork)
- ½ cup molasses
- 1 cup ketchup
- ¼ cup brown sugar
- 2 tsp dry mustard

Place everything in the crock pot and let cook on low for 15-20 hours or on high for 5-7 hours.  (7 hours seemed to work best)

Also note - Not a bad idea to soak the beans ahead of time if you like them softer.
