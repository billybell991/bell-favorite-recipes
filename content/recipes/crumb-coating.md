## Ingredients

- 2 cups dry sifted toasted breadcrumbs
- oil for brushing
- ½ cup flour
- 2 tsp M.S.G. (Accent)
- 1 tsp salt
- ½ tsp fruit sugar
- ¼ to ½ tsp garlic salt
- ½ tsp flavor salt

## Instructions

To make the crumb coating brush sparingly with oil (or melted butter) the slices from half a standard loaf of bread.  Arrange overlapping a little on broiler pan and dry out until crisp in oven at 200 degrees F.  Buzz in blender until extremely fine.  To the two cups add all remaining crumb-coating ingredients and mix well.
