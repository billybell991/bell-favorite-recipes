## Ingredients

- 2 cups raisins
- 1 cup water
- 1 cup lard or shortening
- 2 cups sugar
- 3 eggs, beaten
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 3-1/2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp baking soda
- ½ tsp salt

## Instructions

Cook raisins in water for five minutes. Cool. Cream lard or shortening and sugar. Add eggs, raisins and vanilla. Add sifted dry ingredients. Drop by spoonfuls about 1-1/2” apart on greased cookie sheet. Bake in 350 degree oven for 12 to 15 minutes.Oven grate on top – pan of water under.Remove cookies from pan immediately.

Makes four dozen large cookies.
