## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 2 tbsp coconut oil
- 1½ cups boiling water
- 1 pinch vanilla extract
- 1 tsppumpkin pie spiceor ground ginger

## Instructions

Blend all ingredients in a blender. Drink immediately.

### Tip!

If you’re craving hot chocolate, or just want a plain latte, replace the spices with 1 tablespoon of cocoa or instant coffee. Voila!
