## Ingredients

- 2 cups whole wheat flour
- 2 cups cornmeal
- l cup parmesan or cheddar cheese
- l/2 cup water or broth
- ¾ cup vegetable oil, drained tuna oil or bacon/chicken fat

## Instructions

Preheat oven to 300 degrees.  Combine flour, cornmeal and cheese in a large bowl.
Stir in oil until mixture is crumbly.  Stir in water/broth until well mixed.
Roll dough on lightly floured surface to a thickness of l/2”.  Biscuits can be cut out with a 2”or 4”dog bone cookie cutter.  Transfer to an ungreased baking sheet.
Bake for 45 minutes.  Transfer to rack to cool completely.
