## Ingredients

- ½ cup shortening
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1 grated rind of lemon or 2-3 tsp flavor
- 1-1/2 cup flour
- ½ cup milk
- ½ tsp salt
- 1 tsp baking powder

## Instructions

Cream sugar and shortening.  Add eggs and beat well.  Add rind.  Mix in dry ingredients with milk.  Pour into greased bread pan.  Bake at 350 degrees for 50 – 60 minutes until golden brown and toothpick comes out clean.  While loaf is still hot, poke holes and spread glaze on loaf.

Glaze:  Juice of ½ lemon (or 1 tbsp ReaLemon) and ¼ cup icing sugar.
