## Ingredients

- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 2 cups rolled oats
- 1 cup brown sugar
- ½ cup coconut
- 1-1/4 cup shortening
- ½ tsp soda
- ½ tsp salt
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1-1/2 lb dates

## Instructions

Rub first 8 ingredients together until crumbly.  Put 2/3 mixture in bottom of a 9 x 12 cake pan.  Cook dates with 1-1/2 cups water until soft.  Add ¼ to ½ cup white sugar and 2 tbsp butter.  Mix well.  Spread date mixture over crumbs in bottom of pan.  Top with remaining crumb mixture.  Bake in a 350 degree oven for 30 minutes.

NOTE:  This square can also be filled with mincemeat in place of the dates.
