## Ingredients

- 2 Cups of Flour
- 1 Cup of Brown Sugar
- 1 1/4 Cups of Shortening
- 1/2 Tsp Salt
- 1 1/2 Lbs of Dates
- 2 Cups of Rolled Oats
- 1/2 Cup of Coconut
- 1/2 Tsp Baking Soda
- 1 Tsp Baking Powder

## Instructions

1. Rub first 8 ingredients until crumbly.
2. Put 2/3 of  the mixture into the bottom of a 9x12 cake pan.
3. Cook dates with 1 1/2 cups of water until soft.
4. Add 1/4 - 1/2 cup of white sugar and 2 Tbsp of butter.  Mix well.
5. Spread date mixture over crumbs in bottom of pain.
6. Top with remaining crumb mixture.
7. Bake in a 350 degree oven for 30 minutes.
