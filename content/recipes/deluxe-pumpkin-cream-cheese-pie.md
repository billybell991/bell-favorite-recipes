Submitted by Charleen and Jim Martin

Cousins of the Bride

Requires 2 uncooked pie shells.

- 1 large pkg cream cheese
- 1 egg
- ½ cup white sugar
- 1 tsp vanilla

Beat the above ingredients together and spread in bottom of pie shells.

Pumpkin Layer:

- 2 cups pumpkin, canned
- 2 beaten eggs
- ½ cup sugar
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1 cup milk
- ½ tsp ginger

Mix ingredients and spread carefully over cream cheese layer in pie shells.

Bake at 425 degrees for 10 minutes at bottom of oven. Continue cooking for 35 minutes at 350 degrees (or until pumpkin is set).

Garnish with whipped cream or crushed peanut brittle.

**The edges of the pies can be wrapped with foil to prevent the pastry from getting too dark before the pumpkin is cooked.
