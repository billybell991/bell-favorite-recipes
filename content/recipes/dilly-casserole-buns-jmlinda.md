## Ingredients

Flour Ingredients (#1)

- 1 (450g) container of cottage cheese
- 4 Tbsp melted butter
- 2 Tsp salt
- 2 Eggs
- 1 Cup water
- 2 Tsp sugar

Wet Ingredients (#2)

- 4 Tsp active dry yeast
- 4 Tbsp dried onions
- 4 Tsp dill seed
- 5 Cups flour

## Instructions

1. Put cottage cheese in bowl.  Add eggs and hot water.  Whisk together.  Add salt and sugar.  Whisk warm mixture on stove stirring constantly until it is lukewarm.  Add the melted butter after it is done heating up.
2. Use your hands to mix in flour ingredients (#2) to wet ingredients (#1).
3. Dump out on floured board.  Knead dough using a little bit of flour as needed.  When you see it cracking on top, that's enough kneading!!
4. Grease a bowl, put the dough in it, and then turn if over (the bowl or the dough?  Not sure...lemme know if you make this and I'll update the recipe)
5. Cover with a tea towel and the put a black garbage bag on top of the tea towel.
6. Let rise for about 1 hour.
7. Roll into balls.  Cover a cookie sheet with parchment paper and put your balls on the sheet.  *giggle*  Cover with the tea towel and garbage bag and let them rise for about 1 hour.
8. Bake at 400 degrees for 20 minutes.
