## Ingredients

- 1 pkg (4-serving size) Jello Chocolate
- 1 cup cold milk
- instant pudding
- 1 1-L Cool Whip, thawed
- 20 chocolate sandwich cookies
- 1-1/2 cups miniature marshmallows
- chopped
- 1 graham crumb crust
- Gummy worms for decoration

## Instructions

Prepare pudding according to package directions reducing milk to 1 cup.  Fold in whipped topping.  Stir in 2/3 of the cookies and all the marshmallows.  Spoon into crust.  Sprinkle top with remaining cookies.  Decorate with gummy worms.  Freeze until firm – about 4 hours.  Remove from freezer 10 minutes before serving for easy slicing.  Makes 6 to 8 servings.  For best results, use only Jello instant pudding and Cool Whip topping.
