## Ingredients

- 1 pkg. marshmallows
- 1 small tin Carnation milk
- Graham wafers or Graham wafer crumbs

## Instructions

Soak marshmallows in the milk for 3 to 4 hours.  Stir often without breaking them up.  Roll out graham wafers to a powder.  Drop one marshmallow at a time on or into the graham wafer crumbs and roll.  Place on a plate to dry.
