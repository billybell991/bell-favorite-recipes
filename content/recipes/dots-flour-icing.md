## Ingredients

- 5 Tbsp flour
- 1 cup milk
- 1 egg
- ½ cup butter
- ½ cup shortening
- 1 cup white sugar
- 1 tsp lemon extract or vanilla

## Instructions

Mix flour, milk and egg and cook over low heat, stirring constantly until mixture thickens.

Cool. Combine sugar, butter and shortening, and then blend in cooled mixture. Beat until smooth and fluffy; mix in flavoring and spread on cake.
