## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- ¾ cup white sugar
- 1 cup milk
- 3 cups cake or pastry flour OR 2-5/8 cups All-Purpose flour
- 1 Tbsp baking powder
- 1-1/2 tsp salt (I use ½ tsp)
- ½ tsp nutmeg
- 1 Tbsp melted margarine or butter

## Instructions

BEAT eggs and sugar in beater bowl until pale – about 5 minutes. Sift together flour, baking powder, salt and nutmeg. Add them alternately with the milk to the bowl and beat until blended. Beat in melted butter or margarine.

HEAT fat to between 365 – 375 degrees F. – no hotter or it will burn the doughnuts before they are cooked through.. 1-1/4” of oil should do.

ROLL batter into balls about 1” in diameter, carefully place balls in fat and let cook for about 2 minutes. IF they do not turn over themselves give them a little nudge with your slotted spoon. When rich gold, skim out onto paper-towel-lined plates. When all are done, sprinkle generously with powdered sugar.

MAKES about 48 and they are superb.
