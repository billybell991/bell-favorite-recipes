Submitted by Kevin Roy

Cousin of the Bride

Base:

- 1 ½ cups vanilla or graham crumbs
- ½ cup chopped peanuts or walnuts
- ¼ cup melted butter
- 2 tbsp peanut butter

Mix the above ingredients and place in a 9x13 inch pan. Save some of the mixture for the topping.

Filling:

- 250 gr cream cheese
- ½ cup white sugar
- ½ cup peanut butter
- 2 tsp vanilla
- 4 eggs, add one at a time
- 1 large container Cool Whip

Mix the above ingredients and layer on top of the base.

Topping:

Reserved mixture from base of cake

5 tbsp chocolate sundae sauce

Sprinkle reserved topping on the cake. Heat the chocolate sauce in the microwave and swirl it on top of the cake with a knife.
