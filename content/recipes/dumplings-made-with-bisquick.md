## Instructions

Mix 2 cups Bisquick, ¾ cup milk well with fork.  Drop by tablespoonfuls gently onto boiling stew.  Cook over low heat 10 minutes uncovered and 10 minutes covered.  Let stew bubble gently.

Makes 10 – 12 dumplings.
