## Instructions

In a bowl, whisk together one 35 gr packet of Old El Paso Seasoning Mix, 1 cup of light sour cream and ½ cup of light mayonnaise until thoroughly combined.

Stir in 2/3 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese.

Service with vegetable sticks and chicken wings.

Makes 2-1/2 cups
