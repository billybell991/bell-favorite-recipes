## Ingredients

- ½ cup boiling water
- 3 tbsp margarine
- ¾ cup sugar
- 1-1/2 cup instant powdered milk

## Instructions

Put in blender and mix.  No cooking.  Can be used for all recipes that call for Eagle Brand milk.

Eagle Brand has a measure of 1 cup plus 2 tablespoons in a 15 oz can.
