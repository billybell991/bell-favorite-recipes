## Ingredients

- 1 cup light or dark brown sugar, packed
- 1 large egg, slightly beaten
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 cup chocolate chips

## Instructions

Mix all ingredients together till well mixed. Drop by teaspoonful on a greased cookie sheet 2” apart.

Bake in a 350 degree oven till puffed and golden brown – about 10 minutes.
