## Ingredients

- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- ¼ cup granulated sugar
- ½ cup butter
- 1/3 cup raspberry jam (or your choice)
- 2 eggs
- 1 pkg (212 g) Shirriff Lemon Pie Filling
- ½ cup ground almonds
- ¼ cup sliced almonds

## Instructions

Combine flour and sugar, then butter until the mixture clumps together. Press firmly in 8” square pan. Bake at 350 degrees 15 – 18 minutes or until slightly browned. Remove from oven and spread with jam.
Beat eggs until light and creamy. Beat in pie filling and ground almonds. Pour over jam layer. Sprinkle with sliced almonds. Bake 20 –25 minutes or until set and firm to touch. Cool. Cut into squares. Makes 25 squares.

NOTE: Grease pan and especially the sides as the squares really stick to the sides.

HINT:  For Lemon Coconut Squares Substitute 1-1/2 cups flaked coconut for the ground almonds and sliced almonds.
