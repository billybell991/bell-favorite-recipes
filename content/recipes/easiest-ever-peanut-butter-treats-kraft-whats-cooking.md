## Instructions

No –Bake Double Peanut Butter Bars   Prep: 20 Min.   Total: 20 Min.

1-1/2 cup Draft Smooth Peanut Butter	2/3 cup honey
2/3 cup sugar	3 cups Post Honey Bunches of Oats Cereal
1 cup peanuts	4 squares Baker’s Semi-Sweet Chocolate,
melted.

MICROWAVE peanut butter, honey and sugar in large microwaveable bowl on HIGH 2 or 3 min. or until sugar is melted and mixture is smooth, stirring every minute.

POUR over cereal and peanuts in large bowl; mix to coat well. Press into greased foil-lined 13” x 9” pan.

SPREAD melted chocolate evenly over cereal mixture. Let stand or refrigerate until chocolate is firm. Cut into 24 bars. Store in tightly covered container in refrigerate.

Makes 24 servings, 1 bar each.
