## Ingredients

- 1 cup white sugar
- ½ cup milk
- 1 cup brown sugar
- ¼ cup corn syrup
- ¼ cup melted butter
- 2 tbsp cocoa

## Instructions

Mix in small saucepan.  Bring to a boil (soft ball stage).  Five minutes after it begins to boil, remove from heat.  Add vanilla and nuts.  Less cocoa will give a caramel fudge.
