- 2 8-oz pkgs shredded coconut
- 1 15-oz (1-1/3 cup) Eagle Brand
- 2 tsp vanilla

Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Mix ingredients. Drop from teaspoon onto well-greased cookie sheet. Bake at 350 degrees for 10 – 12 minutes. Cool slightly. Remove to rack. Makes about four dozen.
