## Ingredients

- 2 lb (about 8 cups) yellow beans, cut in 1” pieces
- ½ cup flour
- 1-1/2 tsp salt
- ¼ cup dry mustard
- 2 cups brown sugar
- 2 tsp turmeric
- 1 Tbsp celery seed
- 3 cups Heinz white vinegar
- 1 cup water

## Instructions

Cook beans in lightly salted water until just tender-crisp; drain.  In large heavy saucepan, mix flour, mustard, turmeric, salt, sugar and celery seed.  Stir in vinegar and water gradually.  Bring to a boil over low heat and cook, stirring constantly until thickened, about 3 to 4 minutes.  Add beans to sauce and bring to boiling.  Pack into hot, sterilized jars and seal.

Makes 4 pints.
