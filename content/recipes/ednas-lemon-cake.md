All who attended Creation Unique’s Camp Wanna-sew enjoyed Edna’s Lemon Cake.

- 1 Lemon Cake Mix
- 1 Jello Instant Pudding Mix

Add:

- 4 eggs
- 1 cup of water
- ½ cup oil

Mix well. Pour into pan and bake for 1 hour at 325 degrees.

For the glaze, Edna stirred lemon juice with lemon juice.
