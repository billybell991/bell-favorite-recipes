## Ingredients

- 4 cups chicken broth
- 3 eggs
- 1/3 cup cold water
- 3 tbsp corn starch

## Instructions

1. Mix the corn starch slurry (the corn starch and the water) in a bowl
2. Bring the chicken broth to a simmer in a medium pot
3. Slowly stir the corn starch slurry into the chicken broth
4. Let it simmer for around 2-3 mins, stirring occasionally
5. Whisk the eggs in a separate bowl
6. Slowly stir the eggs into the soup, while stirring.  The more you stir, the finer the egg "strands" will be.  Think the same technique as making scrambled eggs.  Lots of stirring, little chunks.  Less stirring, big chunks.
7. Let that simmer for a few mins and enjoy.
