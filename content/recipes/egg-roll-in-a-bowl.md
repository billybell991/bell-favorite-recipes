## Ingredients

- 1 lb ground turkey or chicken
- 1 tbsp sesame oil (or olive oil if needed)
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp fresh ginger, minced (or 1 tsp ground ginger)
- 1 small onion, diced (or 3 green onions)
- 1 bag coleslaw mix (or ~4 cups shredded cabbage + carrots)
- 3–4 tbsp soy sauce or tamari (to taste)
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar
- ½–1 tsp sriracha or chili garlic sauce (optional)
- Salt & pepper, to taste

## Instructions

1. Brown the meat - Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add oil, then ground turkey or chicken. Cook until browned and fully cooked, breaking it up as it cooks. Season lightly with salt and pepper.
2. Add aromatics - Stir in garlic, ginger, and onion. Cook for 1–2 minutes until fragrant and softened.
3. Add veggies - Add the coleslaw mix. Stir and cook 3–5 minutes until cabbage is tender but still slightly crisp.
4. Season - Add soy sauce, rice vinegar, and sriracha (if using). Stir well and cook another 1–2 minutes. Taste and adjust seasoning.
5. Serve - Remove from heat and top with green onions and sesame seeds if desired.
