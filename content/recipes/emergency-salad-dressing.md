## Ingredients

- (Tastes lie Kraft Catalina)
- 1 tsp salt
- l tsp paprika
- l/3 cup sugar
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- l/4 cup vinegar
- 2/3 cup salad oil
- l/3 cup catsup (ketchup)

## Instructions

Measure ingredients into bottle or jar.  Cover tightly and shake until well mixed.  Makes l-l/4  cups.

NOTE:  Catsup is easily measured if you measure the oil then add the catsup to make l cup.  When measured this way, the catsup doesn’t stick to the measuring cup.
