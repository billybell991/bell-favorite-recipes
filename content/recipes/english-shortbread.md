## Ingredients

- 1 lb butter
- 1 cup fruit sugar
- 4 cups flour

## Instructions

Cream butter. Add sugar. Mix well. Add flour a little at a time, mixing well each time. Roll out ½ inch thick. Cut with cookie cutter. Bake on ungreased cookie sheet at 325 degree oven.

Bake 15 minutes. Turn cookie sheet. Continue baking for another 15 minutes.
