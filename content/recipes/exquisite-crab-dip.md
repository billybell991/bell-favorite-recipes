## Ingredients

- 1 8-oz pkg cream cheese
- 1 can (6 oz) crabmeat
- 1 tsp horseradish
- 1 tsp milk
- 1 tsp minced onion

## Instructions

Combine all ingredients. Serve cold or bake for 20 minutes at 350 degrees. Serve with cocktail rye bread.
