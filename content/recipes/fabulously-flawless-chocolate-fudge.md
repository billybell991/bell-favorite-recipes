## Ingredients

- 8 oz (8 squares) Baker’s semi-sweet chocolate
- 2/3 cup sweetened condensed milk
- 1 tsp vanilla
- ¼ tsp salt
- ½ cup chopped nuts (optional)

## Instructions

Melt chocolate with milk over hot water.  Remove from heat; blend in vanilla, salt and nuts.
Spread in buttered 8”x 4”loaf pan.  Flatten with buttered spoon.
Chill until firm, then cut into squares.  Makes 18 candies.

NOTE:  Recipe may be doubled and spread in 8-inch square pan.
