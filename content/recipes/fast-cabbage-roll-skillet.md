## Ingredients

- 1 lb medium ground beef or pork
- 2 cloves garlic, crushed
- 1 large onion, chopped
- 1 14-oz tin tomato sauce
- ½ cup water
- 1 cup 5-minute rice
- 1 tsp paprika½ tsp dried
- dill weed
- ¼ tsp each salt and pepper
- ½ head cabbage
- sour cream

## Instructions

In large, wide saucepan over medium heat, cook meat and garlic, stirring often with fork to keep meat crumbly.  Add 1 or 2 tsp of vegetable oil if meat sticks.

Stir in onion and cook until meat loses its redness, a total of about 5 minutes.  Stir in tomato sauce, water, rice, paprika, dill weed, salt and pepper.  Cover and bring to a boil.

Meanwhile slice cabbage into 1 x 3 inch strips.  Stir into meat mixture.  Cover tightly and reduce heat to medium-low.  Cook, stirring often, until rice and cabbage are tender, 10 to 15 minutes.

Serve with sour cream.  Serves 4 to 6.
