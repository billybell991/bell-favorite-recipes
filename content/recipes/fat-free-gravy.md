Call this a waist-watcher gravy. There are only 16 calories in one serving. Serve it over meatloaf, roast meat and mashed potatoes.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup beef broth and defatted roast drippings combined
- 2 tsp all-purpose flour
- 1 tsp cornstarch
- 1 tsp ketchup
- Pinch each of salt, freshly ground pepper and basil

## Instructions

Combine beef broth, flour and cornstarch in a screw-top jar, cover and shake until well combined. Pour into saucepan, stir in ketchip, salt, pepper and basil. Stir-cook over medium heat about 2 minutes until gravy thickens.

Makes 4 servings, 1 cup.

Each serving: ¼ cup – or 1 EXTRA 3 g carbohydrate, 1 g protein, 16 calories
