- 4 large potatoes
- 1 tsp thyme
- 2 medium onions
- ¼ tsp basil
- 1 to 1-1/2 lb fish fillets, deboned
- ½ tsp salt
- ¼ tsp pepper

SAUCE:

## Ingredients

- ¼ cup butter
- ½ tsp salt
- ¼ cup flour
- Dash of pepper
- 2 cup milk
- ½ tsp dry mustard
- 1 cup grated Cheddar cheese (or Cheez Whiz)

## Instructions

Boil 4 large potatoes until almost cooked. Slice and layer in a greased 9x13-inch dish. Slice a thin layer of onion over potato. Place a layer of thinly sliced fillet (uncooked &amp; deboned) over.

Sprinkle thyme and basil over fish as well as salt and pepper.

SAUCE: Mix thoroughly the butter and flour. Add milk and cheese. Add salt and pepper and ½ teaspoon dry mustard. Pour over top of fish. Bake, covered lightly with tin foil, for 1 hour at 350 degrees.
