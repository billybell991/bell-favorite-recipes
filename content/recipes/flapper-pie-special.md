Cousins of the Bride

- 18-20 graham wafers
- ¼ cup butter, melted
- ¼ cup shortening, melted
- ¼ tsp cinnamon

Crush and roll wafers fine. Blend in butter, shortening and cinnamon. Line bottom and sides of a 9 inch pie plate. Reserve 1/8 cup for topping.

Filling:

## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- ½ cup sugar
- 1 ½ tbsp cornstarch
- 2 cups milk
- 1 tsp vanilla

## Instructions

Separate eggs. Beat yolks until lemony in color. Add sugar and cornstarch and blend well.

Add milk and cook over direct heat, stirring constantly. When thickened, take off of heat and add vanilla.

Beat 4 egg whites until stiff. Add 8 tablespoons of sugar, 2 spoons at a time. Continue to beat.

Pour filling into pie shell. Spoon meringue over top and sprinkle with wafer mixture.

Bake at 250 degrees for 20 minutes. Cool well before cutting.
