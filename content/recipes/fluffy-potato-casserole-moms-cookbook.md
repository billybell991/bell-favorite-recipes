## Ingredients

- 2 cups hot or cold mashed potatoes
- 1 small onion, finely chopped
- 1 8-oz pkg cream cheese, softened
- 2 eggs
- 2 Tbsp all-purpose flour
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

Put the potatoes into the large bowl of your electric mixer.  Add the cream cheese, chopped onion, eggs, and flour.  Beat at medium speed until the ingredients are blended, then bet at high speed until light and fluffy.  Taste and add salt and pepper, if needed.  Spoon into a greased 9-inch square baking dish.  Bake, uncovered, in a 300 degree oven for about 35 minutes.

Makes 6 to 8 servings.

NOTE:  I usually make it in a regular casserole dish and bake at 350 for about 1 hour.
