- 2 Cups of mashed potatoes, hot or cold
- 1 250g package of cream cheese, softened
- 1 small onion, finely chopped
- 3 Tbsp of flour
- Salt & Pepper to taste

Add everything together.  Beat until light and fluffy.  Spoon into greased casserole dish.  Bake at 300 degrees for 35 mins (maybe more).
