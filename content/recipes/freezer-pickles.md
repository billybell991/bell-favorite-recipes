## Ingredients

- 7 cups sliced pickling cucumber, with peel
- 1 large onion, sliced
- 1 cup thinly sliced peppers, seeded, all green or half red
- 1 Tbsp coarse pickling salt
- ½ tsp celery seed
- 1 cup white vinegar
- 2 cups granulated sugar

## Instructions

Combine all ingredients in bowl. Stir well. Cover. Refrigerate for 3 days. Stir every day. Pack into freezer containers, covering with brine to within 1 inch of top. Freeze. Thaw before serving. Keeps in refrigerator at least 8 weeks. Keeps in freezer at least 1 year. Makes 3 pints.
