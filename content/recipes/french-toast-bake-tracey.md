## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup melted butter
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 loaf of Texas toast (no crusts)
- 4 eggs
- 1 1/2 cups of milk
- 1 tsp vanilla

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in microwave.  Add sugar, stir and pour into a 9 x 13 dish.  Spread all over the bottom.
2. Beat eggs, milk, and vanilla.
3. Place layer of toast, then remaining egg mixture on top.
4. Cover and chill overnight.
5. Sprinkle with cinnamon.
6. Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.
