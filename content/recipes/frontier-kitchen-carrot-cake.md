- 2 cups white sugar
- 4 eggs
- 1-1/2 cups oil
- 2 Tbsp cinnamon
- 2 cups flour
- 1 Tsp salt
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 3 cups grated carrots
- 2 tsp baking soda
- 1 cup chopped nuts

Blend sugar and oil. Add eggs one at a time, beating after each one. Beat well. Sift together dry ingredients. Add gradually to first mixture. Add carrots and nuts last.

Bake in 9” x 15” pan 45 minutes approx. at 350 F.

Icing For Carrot Cake:

- 1  8-oz pkg cream cheese
- ¼ lb butter
- 1 lb icing sugar
- 2 tsp vanilla

Mix Well.
