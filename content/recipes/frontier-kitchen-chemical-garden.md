FRONTIER KITCHEN CHEMICAL GARDEN

Mix:

## Ingredients

- 6 Tbsp liquid bluing
- 6 Tbsp salt
- 6 Tbsp water

## Instructions

Add 1 Tbsp ammonia

Mix well and pour slowly over 3 or 4 small rocks, clinkers or coal in an old pie plate (or a small fish bowl).  Crystals start to grow very soon and will continue as long as the liquid stays.

If you want a little colour; add a few drops cake colouring to the rocks. Set the pan where you can watch it daily.
