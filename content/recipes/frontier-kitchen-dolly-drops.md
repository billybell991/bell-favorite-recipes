## Ingredients

- 1 cup white sugar
- 1 Tbsp Crisco
- 2 Tbsp butter
- 3 Tbsp water

## Instructions

Cook 3 minutes. Dip pieces of bread in mixture (cut 2” or 3” pieces) and place on wax paper.

Sprinkle with coconut.
