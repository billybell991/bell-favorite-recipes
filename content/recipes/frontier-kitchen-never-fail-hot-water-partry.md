## Ingredients

- 6 cups flour
- ½ tsp salt
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1 lb Tenderflake lard
- 1 cup boiling water

## Instructions

Combine dry ingredients. Cut lard into it until grainy. Add water. Mix thoroughly. Wrap and set 24 hours. Take out 6 hours before use. Will last 3 months in fridge, 6 months in freezer.

Makes 4  2-crust pies.

NOTE: originated in England approx. 150 years ago. If you want to make it fussy for fruit pies, roll top crust in sugar.
