## Ingredients

- 2 eggs
- 2 cups brown sugar
- 2 Tbsp cream or milk
- 3 Tbsp melted butter
- 1 Tbsp cornstarch
- 1 tsp vanilla

## Instructions

Mix all together. Then place in uncooked tart shells and bake at 400 F. until pie is golden brown.
