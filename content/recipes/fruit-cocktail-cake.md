300 degrees   -   50 – 60 minutes

Ingredients for Cake:

- 2 eggs
- 1-3/4 cup W. Sugar
- 2 cups flour
- 2 tsp Baking Soda
- 1 – 14 oz tin fruit cocktail (drained but reserve juice) – Slightly crush fruit w/a fork

Ingredients for Topping:

## Ingredients

- 1-3/4 cup W. Sugar
- ½ cup pure Carnation Milk
- ½ cup butter or margarine

## Instructions

Directions for Cake: Beat eggs with Sugar. Put juice & Baking Soda in eggs & beat. Add Fruit Cocktail & beat. Add flour  & beat. Pour into a 13” x 15” well-greased pan. Bake for 50 -  60 minutes in a 300 degree oven. Test w/a toothpick for doneness.

Directions for Topping: Put topping in a saucepan & boil it for about 3 minutes. (It should get a little thicker.)  Do this just about 10 minutes before cake is finished baking.

After cake is done, loosen the edges and prick holes in the cake w/a fork. While cake is still warm, pour hot topping over cake.

This is called Nova Scotia Fruit Cocktail Cake and credit to Alda Sivret for this one!
