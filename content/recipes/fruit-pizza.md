## Ingredients

- Variety of Fruit
- Topping:
- Crust:
- 12 oz cream cheese, softened
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- ½ cup granulated sugar
- ½ cup brown sugar
- 1 tsp vanilla
- ¼ cup icing sugar
- Apricot Glaze:
- 1 cup butter, softened
- ¼ cup apricot jam or orange marmalade
- 1 Tbsp water

## Instructions

Crust:  Mix all ingredients together in a bowl until it forms a ball.  Press onto bottom of pizza pan.  Bake at 350 degree F about 10 to 15 minutes or until golden brown.  Cool

Topping:  Beat cheese, sugar and vanilla together.  Spread over crust.

Arrange fruit in pattern of your choice.  Make your own colour arrangement.  Glaze fruit.

Apricot Glaze:  Mix apricot jam or orange marmalade with water.
