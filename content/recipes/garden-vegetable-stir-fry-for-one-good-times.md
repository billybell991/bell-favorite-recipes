## Ingredients

- ½ cup vegetable, chicken or beef stock
- 1/3 cup couscous
- 2 tsp vegetable oil
- ¼ cup chopped onion
- 2 tsp minced gingerroot
- 2 cups packed fresh spinach, chopped
- ½ cup each diced sweet red and green peppers
- ½ cup sugar snap peas, trimmed
- 2 tsp soya sauce
- ¼ cup sliced radishes
- Pinch each salt and pepper

## Instructions

In saucepan, bring stock to boil. Add couscous; cover and remove from heat. Let stand for 5 minutes; fluff with fork.

Meanwhile, in non-stick skillet, heat oil over medium-high heat; stir-fry onion and ginger for about 3 minutes or until softened.

Add spinach, red and green peppers and peas; stir-fry for about 2 minutes or just until spinach is wilted. Drizzle with soy sauce, cover and cook for 1 minute. Fold in couscous and radishes. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Makes 1 serving.
