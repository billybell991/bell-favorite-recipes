### Ingredients

## Ingredients

- 5-6 Large Potatoes Peeled and cubed You can use more or less with the same cooking time
- 1 cup chicken broth
- 3 cloves garlic
- 1 sprig rosemary
- 2 tbs butter
- 1/4 cup milk

## Instructions

### Instructions

1. Place cubed potatoes in the Instant pot with chicken broth whole cloves of garlic, rosemary sprig, and butter.
2. Place IP on manual high pressure for 15 minutes.
3. Do a quick release.
4. Drain Potatoes if needed and add milk and butter
5. Mash until creamy and smooth. I use my kitchen aid mixer or a handheld masher.
