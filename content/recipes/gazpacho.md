## Ingredients

- 1 large tin tomato juice
- 1 can diced tomatoes or 3-4 ripe, fresh tomatoes, chopped
- ½ English cucumber, unpeeled and finely chopped
- ½ red pepper, finely chopped (whole one if small)
- 2 cloves garlic, minced or crushed
- ½ red onion, finely chopped
- 1 tsp sugar
- ¼ cup red wine vinegar
- approximately 2 tbsp good olive oil
- dashes of Tabasco to taste
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

Best refrigerated for a couple of hours or overnight. Serve cold, garnished with croutons.

Makes about 8 cups.
