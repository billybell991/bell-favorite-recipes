- 2 tbsp butter
- 1/3 cup white sugar
- 2 eggs
- 3 tbsp milk
- 1 cup flour
- 1 tsp baking powder
- Pinch of salt

Cream butter and add sugar little by little, then add the eggs and salt.  Sift flour with baking powder.  Add to egg and sugar mixture alternately with milk.  Beat well.  Pour into a well-greased and floured flan pan.  Bake 15 minutes in a 325 degree oven.  Knock firmly on bottom of pan when you take it out of the oven to loosen it from pan.  Cool.  Invert on a place and fill it with fruit or filling of your choice.  Top with whipped cream if desired.

I (Mom) have fond memories of a dear friend, Mrs. Ilsa Preuss, every time I look at this recipe.
