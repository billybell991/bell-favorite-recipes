## Ingredients

- 1 baked 9- or 10-inch pie shell
- 5 cups blackberries, strawberries, raspberries
- 3 tbsp cornstarch
- or blueberries
- 1 cup sugar
- ¾ cup water
- 1 tsp lemon juice
- 1 cup whipping cream

## Instructions

Crush and sieve 1 cup of the berries to remove seeds.  Combine cornstarch, sugar, sieved berries, and water.  Cook over medium-low heat until thickened and clear, about 10 minutes.  Cool slightly, then add lemon juice and remaining berries. Mix lightly and pile into baked pie shell.
Cool and chill about 2 hours. At serving time, whip the cream and pipe or spoon around the rim.
