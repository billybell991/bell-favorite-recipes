## Ingredients

Preparation Time: 10 minutes - Cook Time: 25 minutes

- 2 Tbsp cornstarch
- 2 Tbsp vegetable oil
- 1 tin condensed chicken broth
- 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
- ¼ cup apple juice
- 1 large carrot cut into thin strips
- 1 Tbsp honey
- 1 medium green pepper, cut in thin strips
- 2 tsp Dijonaise mustard

## Instructions

1. In small bow, stir together cornstarch, broth, apple juice, honey and mustard – set aside.

2. In large skillet, heat half of the oil. Brown chicken 5 minutes on each side. Remove chicken from skillet – set aside.

3. In same skillet, heat remaining oil. Stir-fry carrot and pepper until tender-crisp.

4. Stir in reserved cornstarch mixture. Cook until mixture boils and thickens, stirring constantly. Reduce heat to low. Cover, simmer 5 minutes or until chicken is no longer pink.

5. Serve with noodles, if desired. Makes 4 servings.
