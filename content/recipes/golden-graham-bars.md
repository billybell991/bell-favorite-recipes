## Ingredients

- 2/3 cup light corn syrup
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 2 tsp margarine or butter
- 1 10-oz pkg golden grahams
- 1 11-oz pkg chocolate chips
- 3 cup mini marshmallows

## Instructions

Butter 13”x 9”x 2” baking pan.  Heat syrup, butter and chocolate chips just to boiling in a 3-quart saucepan, stirring constantly.  Remove from heat.  Stir in vanilla.  Pour over cereal in large bowl; toss quickly until coated.  Fold in marshmallows, 1 cup at a time.  Press in pan.  Let stand until firm – about 1 hour.

Yield:  48 squares.
