## Ingredients

- 1 ½ kg meat (any kind, no fat)
- 1 bottle soya sauce
- ½ bottle Worcestershire sauce
- ½ - 1 cup of brown sugar
- a few cloves of garlic

## Instructions

Cut meat into 1/8-1/4 inch thick strips. Make sure there is no fat on the meat. Cut with the grain for chewier jerky.

Mix the rest of the ingredients and taste to your liking. Add meat strips, cover and refrigerate for at least 24 hours.

A dehydrator works great for drying meat, but an oven set on the lowest setting with a broom handle wedging the door open an inch will work too.

The meat will take 6-10 hours to dry, depending on your meat size and setup.

Note:For oven method, you will need a mesh or heavy screen to put the meat on.

This is a good start. Be creative!
