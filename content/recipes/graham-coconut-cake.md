## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter, room temperature
- 1 cup white sugar
- 2 cups graham crumbs
- 1 egg
- 1 cup medium coconut
- 1 cup milk
- 3 tbsp flour
- 1 tsp baking powder
- ½ tsp vanilla
- pinch of salt

## Instructions

Cream butter and sugar together. Add beaten egg. Add other ingredients and mix well.

Place in a 9x9 inch pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes. Serve with ice cream or whipped cream.
