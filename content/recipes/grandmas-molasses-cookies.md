## Instructions

Mix together
½ cup softened butter
½ cup white sugar
1 cup molasses
Add
2 tsp baking soda
2 tsp ginger
2-1/2 cups white flour

Mix well then shape i8nto small balls and press down with a fork. Add candy decorations if you wish. Bake in 350 F. Makes 3 dozen cookies.
