## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- ¼ tsp salt
- 2 tsp ginger
- ¼ cup molasses
- ¾ cup margarine
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 ½ tsp baking soda
- ½ tsp cinnamon

## Instructions

In a large bowl, cream sugar, margarine, egg and molasses. In a separate bowl, combine dry ingredients. Blend dry ingredients into cream mixture.

Roll batter into balls and place on a greased cookie sheet. Using a fork, gently press down on balls to flatten slightly. Sprinkle cookies with sugar. Bake at 375 degrees for 8-10 minutes.
