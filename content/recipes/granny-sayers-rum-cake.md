Submitted by Pat and Tom Sayer

Aunt and Uncle of the Bride

- 1 yellow Duncan Hines cake mix
- 1 small instant Jello vanilla pudding
- 4 eggs
- ½ cup Crisco oil
- ½ cup water
- ¼ cup White Rum

Mix above ingredients together and bake in a greased bundt pan to which ¼ cup of chopped pecans has been added. Bake at 350 degrees for 55 minutes.

Topping:

In a sauce pan, combine the following:

## Ingredients

- 1 cup of butter (use butter only)
- 1 cup of white sugar
- ½ cup of water

## Instructions

Boil above ingredients for five minutes then add ½ cup of white rum.

When cake is cooked, remove from pan and place topping in the bundt pan. Return cake to the same pan. Let set for approximately 20 minutes until all topping has been absorbed by the cake.

Remove from the pan and enjoy.
