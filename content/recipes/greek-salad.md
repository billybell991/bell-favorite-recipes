## Ingredients

- ½ head lettuce, torn bite size
- 1 Romaine lettuce
- 2 tomatoes, cut bite size
- 1 cucumber, cut bite size
- ¼ cup sliced green onions
- ¼ cup sliced ripe olives
- ¾ cup Feta cheese, cubed
- ½ cup salad oil
- ¼ cup red wine vinegar
- ¼ cup chopped parsley
- ½ tsp salt
- 1/8 tsp pepper
- 1/8 tsp oregano
- 1/8 tsp garlic powder

## Instructions

Layer lettuce in large salad bowl or on a large platter.  Spread with tomatoes, cucumber, onions, olives and cheese.
Beat next seven ingredients together well.  Shake before serving,  Serve with salad.

NOTE:  A few sardines or anchovies may be arranged over top of salad.  Feta cheese may be exchanged with 8 oz (250 gr) cream cheese.
