- 1/2 cup unsweetened Greek yogurt
- 3/4 cup unsweetened almond milk
- 1 tsp stevia (optional)
- 1 cup of fresh fruit (or drained canned fruit or frozen fruit).  Feel free to mix it up.
- 1 tbsp chia seeds
- 2 tbsp oatmeal

Chuck it all into a blender.  Drink it.

Weight Watchers Points: 2

Billy's Notes: The points are pretty much the chia seeds and oatmeal.  If you don't want the benefits those 2 things bring with them, this is a 0 point smoothie.
