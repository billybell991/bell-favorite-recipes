## Ingredients

- 2 ripe avocados, peeled and mashed
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- 2 tsp onion flakes
- 1 tsp salt (optional)
- ¼ tsp cayenne pepper
- ¼ tsp pepper
- ¼ tsp garlic powder
- 1 ripe tomato, diced

## Instructions

Mix all ingredients together. Serve with corn chips, tortilla chips or fresh, raw veggies. Makes about 1 cup.
