## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter or margarine
- 1 cup white sugar
- 2 eggs
- 2 cups pastry flour (1-3/4 cup all-purpose)
- 1 tsp baking powder
- ¼ tsp salt
- 1 cup milk
- 1 lb cooking gumdrops
- 1 lb seedless raisins

## Instructions

Cream sugar and butter; add eggs.  In separate bowl, dredge gumdrops and raisins with ½ cup of the flour.  To the creamed mixture, add 1 cup flour and ½ cup milk, beating well after each addition.  Add remaining flour with baking powder and salt.  Beat well.  Add remaining milk and the floured raisins and gumdrops.  Mix thoroughly.  Bake in a well-greased loaf pan in a 350 degree oven for 1 hour.
