## Ingredients

- 8 oz. Cream cheese, softened
- 16 oz powdered sugar
- 2 pkg (6 squares each white baking
- 1-1/2 tsp vanilla
- chocolate squares, melted
- 1 cup toasted chopped nuts
- 8 drops yellow food coloring
- 4 drops red food coloring
- multi-colored sprinkles

## Instructions

Beat cream cheese in large bowl with electric mixer on medium speed until smooth.
Gradually add sugar, beating until well blended.
Stir in white chocolate, vanilla, nuts and food coloring until well mixed.
Spread on foil-lined 8” square pan.
Top with sprinkles
Refrigerate 1 hour or until firm. Cut into squares.
