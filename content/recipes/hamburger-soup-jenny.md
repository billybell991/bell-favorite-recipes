- 1.5 lbs. of lean hamburg
- 3 small onions (chopped fine)

Brown meat and onions, then drain well.

Combine all other ingredients in a large pot with meat and mix well.

## Ingredients

- 1  28 oz. can of tomatoes
- 3 cans of beef consommé
- 4 carrots (chopped fine)
- 1 bay leaf (optional)
- 1/2 cup of pearl barley
- pepper to taste
- 2 cups of water
- 1 can of tomato soup
- 3 sticks of celery (chopped fine)
- 1/2 tsp. of thyme
- 1/2 tsp. of parsley

## Instructions

Simmer covered at least 2 hours or all day if possible.
