If you’re watching your salt, try this healthier seasoning blend that adds great flavor. Combine garlic, powder, onion powder, oregano, basil and salt-free lemon pepper seasoning.

Fat-free cream cheese is almost as good as the regular cream cheese. It, however, doesn’t get as firm in cheesecake recipes. To remedy this, use one envelope of unflavored gelatin for every 8 ounces of fat-free cream cheese. The gelatin adds no flavor but firms up the cheesecake nicely.

Here’s a simple way to frost an angel food cake – or any other cake. Simply thaw a large container of light frozen whipped topping and fold in one package of any flavor sugar-free gelatin powder. This flavorful frosting mixes up in seconds and tastes great!
