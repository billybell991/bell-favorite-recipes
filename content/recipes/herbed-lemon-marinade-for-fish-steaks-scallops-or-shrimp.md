## Ingredients

- ¼ cup vegetable oil
- ¼ cup Realemon lemon juice
- ¼ cup chopped green onions
- 1 Tbsp water
- ½ tsp garlic salt
- ¼ to ½ tsp dried dill weed

## Instructions

In small bow, combine oil, lemon juice, green onions, water, garlic salt and dill weed. Pour over fish, steaks, scallops or shrimp. Cover and marinate 1 hour. Remove fish from marinade. Grill or broil as desired, basting frequently with marinade. Makes about ¾ cup.

VERSATILE MARINE – For poultry, meat or vegetables

½ cup Realemon lemon juice	½ cup vegetable oil
2 cloves garlic, crushed, chopped	1 tsp salt
½ tsp dried rosemary, thyme or	¼ tsp pepper
tarragon leaves, crushed

In small bowl, combine lemon juice, oil, garlic, salt , rosemary and pepper. Pour over poultry, meat or vegetables. Cover and refrigerate for six hours or overnight, turning frequently. Remove from marinade, grill or broil as desired, basting frequently with marinade. Makes about 1-1/2 cups.
