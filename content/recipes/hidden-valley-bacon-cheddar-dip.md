## Ingredients

- 1 (1-oz) packet Original Ranch Party Dip
- 1 pint sour cream
- 1/4 cup bacon bits
- 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

## Instructions

Mix Ranch Party Dip with sour cream.

Add bacon bits and shredded cheddar cheese.

Mix and serve.
