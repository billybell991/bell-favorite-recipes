## Instructions

Place in large crock:
6 cups soft water

Into this put:
1 tin lye
½ lb Borax

When completely dissolved, put in 9 cups lukewarm fat, constantly stirring with a wooden stick until mixture begins to thicken. Set away in a warm place until next day, then cut into bars. Let ripen for 3 weeks before using.
