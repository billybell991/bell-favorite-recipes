- 10 to 12 chicken wings, cut into 2 pieces
- 3/4 cup brown sugar
- 2 tbsp corn starch
- 3/4 tbsp soya sauce
- 1 tbsp honey
- 2 cloves garlic, chopped very fine (or use a garlic press)

Brown chicken wings or spareribs in frying pan.  Drain fat and place in casserole dish.  In a separate pot, combine sauce ingredients.  Bring to a boil, stirring constantly.  Pour sauce over wings in casserole dish.  Cook in microwave for 10 to 12 minutes on power level 6 or bake in oven at 325 to 350 degrees Fahrenheit for 30 to 40 minutes.

NOTES:

## Ingredients

- Can also use 4 Lbs. of spareribs for this recipe.  We've also used it for just chicken strips and breasts as well.
- It's as good as restaurant honey garlic sauce.  I'd give this one a 5 out of 5.
