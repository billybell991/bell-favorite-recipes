## Ingredients

- 3/4 Cup brown sugar
- 2 Tbsp cornstarch
- 3/4 Cup water
- 3 Tbsp soya sauce
- 1 Tbsp honey
- 2 Cloves of garlic, chopped very fine

## Instructions

Combine all ingredients in a pot.  Bring to a boil, stirring constantly.  Real good slathered on chicken wings, ribs, or in a stir fry.
