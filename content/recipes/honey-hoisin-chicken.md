- 2 Tbsp soya sauce
- 2 Tbsp hoisin sauce
- 2 Tbsp liquid honey
- 2 Tbsp Ketchup
- 2 minced garlic cloves
- ½ tsp sesame oil
- 2 boneless skinless chicken breasts

In small saucepan, combine soy sauce, hoisin, honey, ketchup and garlic. Cook over medium heat for 2 minutes, stirring often. Remove from heat: stir in sesame oil. Remove ¼ of the sauce for basting; set remaining sauce aside.

Place chicken on greased grill over medium heat; close lid and cook for 2 minutes. Turn and brush with sauce, cook for 3 to 4 minutes on each side or until no longer pink inside. Serve with reserved sauce. Makes 2 servings.

Some of the extra sauce is delicious spooned over the stir-fried vegetable dish.
