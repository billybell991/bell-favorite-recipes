## Ingredients

- 1/3 cup liquid honey
- ¼ cup each of water or maple syrup
- 7 to 8 cups air-popped popcorn

## Instructions

Place honey, water and maple syrup in a medium-size saucepan set over medium-high heat. Boil, uncovered, without stirring, until thick, about 8 to 9 minutes. Meanwhile, place popcorn in a large bowl. Pour syrup over popcorn. Toss using 1 or 2 large spoons until popcorn is evenly coated. Using lightly buttered hands, form popcorn into 10 3” balls. Gobble up right away or wrap in waxed paper and store at room temperature.

Makes 10 balls.
