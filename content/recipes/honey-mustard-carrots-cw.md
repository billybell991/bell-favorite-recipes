## Ingredients

- 3-1/2 cups sliced or baby carrots
- 2 tbsp oil
- 1 tbsp honey
- 1 tbsp maple syrup
- 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
- 2 tsp dried parsley flakes

## Instructions

In a saucepan, bring 1” of water to a boil.  Place carrots in a steamer basket over water.  Cover and steam for 8 – 10 minutes or until crisp-tender.

In a saucepan, combine the oil, honey, syrup and mustard; bring to a boil.  Stir in carrots and parsley.

YIELD:  4 – 5 servings.
