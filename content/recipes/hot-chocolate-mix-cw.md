## Ingredients

- 1 box (8 qts) non-fat dry milk
- 1 6-oz non-dairy coffee creamer
- 1 16-oz container dry chocolate
- ½ cup confectioners’sugar
- milk mix

## Instructions

Place all ingredients in a very large bowl or kettle.  Stir until well blended.  Store in airtight containers or pack into small gift containers.  To serve, add ¼ cup chocolate mix to 2/3 cup hot water.
