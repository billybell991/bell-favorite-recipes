## Ingredients

- 1/2  cup milk
- 1/3 cup salsa
- 3-8 oz packages of cream cheese, cubed
- 2-8 oz packages of mock crab, flaked
- 1 cup thinly sliced green onions
- 1-4 oz can of chopped green chillies
- assorted crackers

## Instructions

Combine milk and salsa in a bowl. Transfer to a slow cooker coated with non-stick cooking spray. Stir in cream cheese, crab, onions and chillies. Cover and cook on low for 3-4 hours, stirring every 30 minutes. Serve with crackers. Yields 5 cups.
