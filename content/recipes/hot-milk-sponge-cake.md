## Ingredients

- 2 large eggs
- 1 cup white sugar
- ¼ tsp salt
- 1 tsp vanilla
- ½ cup milk
- 3 tbsp margarine or butter
- 1 cup all-purpose-flour
- 1 tsp baking powder

## Instructions

Into beater bowl break eggs and beat until fluffy then slowly add sugar until very light and doubled in bulk.  Beat in salt & vanilla.  In a pan heat together the milk and margarine OR butter to just below boiling point.  While very hot pour into egg mixture BEATING ALL THE TIME.  Remove beaters.

Sift the flour & baking powder over and FOLD in.  Pour into greased 8 x8 or 9 x 9 pan & bake on center rach in 350 degree oven for 30 min. or until when touched with fingertip dent does not remain.  Cool in pan on rack for a few minutes.  Then if desired, cover with:

TOPPING

1/2 cup soft margarine

½ cup light brown sugar

½ cup fine coconut

Cream those together.  Spread evenly & carefully over slightly cooled cake and heat under hot broiler – 4” from burner – until it bubbles but DO NOT LET IT BURN.  Cool.
