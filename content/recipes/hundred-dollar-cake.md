First Step:

- 1 cup white sugar
- 4 tbsp cocoa
- 1-3/4 cup all-purpose flour
- 2 tsp baking soda
- Pinch of salt

Second Step:

- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 1 cup cold water
- 1 tsp vanilla (Beat well)

Sift dry ingredients together.  Add to liquid mixture and beat for 2 minutes.  Bake in 9”x 9” cake pan for 40 minutes at 350 degrees. -   25 minutes for cupcakes.
