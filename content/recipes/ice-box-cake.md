## Ingredients

- 30 marshmallows, chopped fine
- 30 graham wafers, rolled fine
- 18 cherries, chopped
- ½ cup walnuts, chopped
- 1 can Eaglebrand Milk
- 1 cup coconut

## Instructions

Mix the first four ingredients with the can of Eaglebrand. Butter a shallow pan and spread thick with half the coconut.

Spread the Eaglebrand mixture over the coconut, then spread another layer of coconut on top. Pack well and put in the fridge for 24 hours.

Enjoy! It's easy and yummy!
