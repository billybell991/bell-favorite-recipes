## Ingredients

- 1 lb bacon
- 1 small onion (chopped)
- 1 can green chili (chopped)
- 1 cup grated cheese (Jack)
- 1 1/2 cups milk
- 1 cup Bisquick
- 6 oz sour cream
- 3 eggs
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Brown, drain and crumble bacon.
2. Put in bottom of ll”x 7”greased dish.
3. Top with onion and cheese.
4. Pour rest in blender for l minute.
5. Pour into pan.
6. Bake at 350 degrees until tests done with a knife. (35 – 40 minutes.)
