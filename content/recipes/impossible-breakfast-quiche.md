## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- ¼ tsp salt
- ½ cup Bisquick
- Dash pepper
- ½ cup butter – melted
- ½ cup sausage cooked and crumbled
- 1-1/2 cups milk

## Instructions

Blend all but cheese and meat for 15 seconds in blender.  Sprinkle with cheese and meat on top.  Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.  Cool for 5 minutes.
