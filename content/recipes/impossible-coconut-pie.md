## Ingredients

- 2 cups milk
- ¾ cup sugar
- ½ cup biscuit mix
- 4 eggs
- ¼ cup butter or margarine
- 1-1/2 tsp vanilla
- 1 cup flaked coconut

## Instructions

Combine milk, sugar, biscuit mix, eggs, butter and vanilla in electric blender container.  Cover and blend on low speed for 3 minutes.  Pour into greased pie plate.  Let stand about 5 minutes, then sprinkle with coconut.  Bake at 350 degrees for 40 minutes.  Serve warm or cold.
