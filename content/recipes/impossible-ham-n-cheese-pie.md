## Ingredients

- 2 cups cut-up fully cooked smoked ham
- 4 eggs
- 1 cup shredded natural Swiss cheese
- 2 cups milk
- 1/3 cup chopped green onion or onion
- 1 cup Bisquick
- ¼ tsp salt, if desired
- 1/8 tsp pepper

## Instructions

Heat oven to 400 degrees.  Grease pie plate, 10 x 1-1/2”.  Sprinkle ham, cheese and onion in plate.  Beat remaining ingredients until smooth, 15 seconds in blender on high or 1 minute with hand beater.  Pour into plate.  Bake until golden brown and knife inserted in center comes out clean, 35 to 40 minutes.  Cool 5 minutes.  6 servings.
