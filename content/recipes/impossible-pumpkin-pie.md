## Ingredients

- ¾ cup sugar
- 1 can (16 oz) pumpkin
- ½ cup tea biscuit mix
- 2-1/2 tsp pumpkin pie spice
- 2 tbsp margarine
- 2 tsp vanilla
- 1 can evaporated milk
- 3 eggs

## Instructions

Put everything in a blender.  Mix well.  Grease a 9”pie plate.  Fill.  Bake at 350 degrees F. for 45 – 50 minutes.
