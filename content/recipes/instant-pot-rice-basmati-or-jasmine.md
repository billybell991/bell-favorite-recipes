## Ingredients

- 1 Cup Basmati (or Jasmine) rice
- 1 Cup Water
- Salt to taste (just a few shakes will do)

## Instructions

1. Put all the ingredients in your Instant Pot.  I give them a little stir for good measure.
2. Set the IP for 6 mins and go chillax for a bit.
3. Once those 6 mins are up, let it natural release for 10 mins.
4. Likely good to get it out of the pot right when it's done, as it tends to stick to the pot if you let it sit for a bit.

Cleanup is easy.  Just let your pot soak with water for 10 mins and it rinses right out.  Really good for butter chicken, stir fry, etc...  Comes out perfect.
