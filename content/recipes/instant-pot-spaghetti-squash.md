[Instant Pot Spaghetti Squash | That Low Carb LifeINSTANT POT SPAGHETTI SQUASH is an easy low carb option for dinner. The spaghetti squash cooks in no time and can be used in lots of recipes!](https://thatlowcarblife.com/instant-pot-spaghetti-squash/)- 1 medium spaghetti squash (about 3 pounds)

1. Use a sharp knife to carefully cut the spaghetti squash in half.
2. Remove the seeds from the squash using a spoon or your hands.
3. Add the trivet to your Instant Pot and add 1 cup of water.
4. Place the spaghetti squash in the Instant Pot. If both halves do not fit at the same time, you'll have to cook them separately. The lid must fully seal with the squash in the pot.
5. Close the lid, set vent to sealed, and cook on high pressure for 7 minutes.
6. When the Instant Pot finishes cooking, manually release the pressure carefully.
7. Once pressure has fully released, remove the lid and use tongs to remove the squash.
8. To shred the squash into long spaghetti-like strands, use a fork to scrape the squash widthwise.
