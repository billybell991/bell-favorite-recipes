## Ingredients

- 1 cup long grain white rice (rinsed or unrinsed)
- 1 1/4 cup low-sodium chicken broth
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ tablespoon butter

## Instructions

1. Add rice, chicken broth, salt, and butter to the pressure cooker pot and stir.
2. Secure the lid and turn pressure release knob to a sealed position. Cook at high pressure for 3 minutes.
3. When cooking is complete, use a natural release for 10 minutes and then release any remaining pressure.
4. Fluff the rice with a fork.  Serve immediately.
