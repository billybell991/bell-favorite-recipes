- 2 cups flour
- 1 cup shortening
- 3 tsp baking powder
- 2 tbsp sugar

MIX LIKE PASTRY AND THEN ADD;

## Ingredients

- 1 egg
- 1 tsp vanilla
- ½ cup milk

## Instructions

Mix well.  Roll out on floured board.  Divide evenly and place in muffin tins.  Put about 1 tbsp jam in center of each and fold corners together.  Bake at 350 degrees for 15 minutes.
