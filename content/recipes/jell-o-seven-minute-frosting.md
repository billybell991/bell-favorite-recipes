## Ingredients

- 2 egg whites, unbeaten
- 1-1/4 cups sugar
- 1 pkg Jell-O (any flavour)
- Dash of salt
- 1/3 cup water
- 2 tsp light corn syrup

## Instructions

Combine all ingredients in top of double boiler.  Beat 1 minute to mix thoroughly.  Place over boiling water and beat constantly with rotary beater (or at high speed of electric mixer) 7 minutes, or until frosting will stand in stiff peaks.  (Stir frosting up from bottom and sides of pan occasionally.)  Remove from boiling water.  Beat 1 minute, or until thick enough to spread.

Makes 4-3/4 cups frosting, or enough to cover tops and sides of two 9-inch layers, 2 dozen cupcakes, or a 10-inch tube cake, or to fill and frost a 9-inch tube cake that has been split horizontally into 2 layers.
