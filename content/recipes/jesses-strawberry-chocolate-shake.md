Submitted by Jesse Beaudry

Nephew of the Bride

- ½ cup sliced strawberries
- 2 tbsp white sugar
- 1 tbsp cocoa
- 1 cup milk, divided
- ½ cup cold club soda
- ice cream or whipped cream
- fresh strawberries

In a blender, combine the sliced strawberries, sugar, cocoa and ½ cup milk. Cover and blend until smooth. Add remaining ½  cup milk and club soda. Cover and blend. Pour into 2 glasses. Garnish with scoop of ice cream or whipped cream and whole strawberries.
