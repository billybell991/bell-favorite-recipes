## Ingredients

- 1 cup flour
- 2 Tbsp sugar
- 4 tsp baking powder
- ½ tsp salt
- 1 cup cornmeal
- 1 egg
- 1 cup milk
- 1 Tbsp melted butter

## Instructions

Mix and sift flour, sugar, baking powder and salt. Add cornmeal. Beat egg and milk and add to the flour mixture. Mix thoroughly and add melted butter.

Bake in a greased cake pan 8-inch square at 350 F. for 30 minutes.
