## Ingredients

- 5 cups water
- 2 28-oz tins tomatoes
- 2 green peppers, diced
- 1 medium cabbage, shredded
- 1 large onion, finely chopped
- 1 stalk celery, diced
- 1 35-gram pkg onion soup mix

## Instructions

In large pot, combine water, tomatoes, peppers, cabbage, onion, celery and onion soup mix.  Bring to boil, reduce heat and simmer 45 minutes, adding more water if desired.  Makes about 24 cups.
