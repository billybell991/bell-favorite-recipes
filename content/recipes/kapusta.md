## Ingredients

- 3 or 4 onions, chopped
- ¼ cup butter
- 1 jar of sauerkraut
- 1 cup of Uncle Ben's rice
- 2 cups of water
- chicken cubes for broth

## Instructions

Cook onions in butter until soft. Mix chicken cubes in water. Add rice and cook for 20 minutes until water is absorbed.

In a casserole dish, mix rice, onions and sauerkraut and cook in a slow, 250 degree oven for about 1½ to 2 hours.

Very good with pork chops or sausages.
