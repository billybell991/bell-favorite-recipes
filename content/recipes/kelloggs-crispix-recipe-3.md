## Ingredients

- 6 cups Kellogg’s Crispix Cereal
- 2 Tbsp vegetable oil
- 5 tsp Club House SALADS ‘N DIPS
- Dressing & Dip Mix  - approx ½ pkg – any variety

## Instructions

In large mixing bowl, using two spatulas, gently toss cereal with oil until well coated. Spread on cookie sheet.

Bake at 250 degrees for 20 minutes, stirring after 10 minutes. Remove from oven.

Place dry mix in 8-cup or larger container. Add warm cereal, close container securely and gently shake and turn until cereal is thoroughly coated with mix. Serve warm, or cool on tray and store in container.      Yield: 6 cups.

NOTE:- You can change the flavour of this easy snack to suit your taste by varying the amount of dry mix. Use the complete package if you enjoy highly seasoned food.
