## Ingredients

- 1 butternut squash peeled, seeded, cubed
- 4 cupsbroth
- 12 oz frozen cauliflower thawed & cooked until soft
- ½ cup sour cream
- ¼ cup heavy cream
- 1 teaspoonsalt

Instant Pot:

## Instructions

1. Put the squash and broth in the instant pot.
2. Cook on manual high for 15 minutes. You can quick release the pressure at this point or just let it release naturally if you prefer.
3. Add the cauliflower. Puree with an immersion blender.
4. Stir in the sour cream and heavy. Season to taste. Serve immediately or let the instant pot stay on warm.

Stovetop:

1. Simmer the squash in the broth until tender, about 25-30 minutes. Then proceed with the other instructions.

Billy's Notes:  Keep your portions small if you're doing keto and eating this.  More of a side, since squash is a starchy veg.
