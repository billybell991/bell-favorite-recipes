## Ingredients

- 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
- 1/2 cup shredded parmesan cheese (use 1 cup if you really like cheese)
- 1 egg
- 1 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- Pinch of Italian seasoning
- Parchment paper
- Salt

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 F.
2. Mix all ingredients together in a bowl and pat it out like you would pizza dough into parchment paper (not wax!)
3. Sprinkle with some salt.
4. Bake for approx. 15 minutes.
5. Broil on high for 1-2 minutes to get a crisper top and brown edges.
6. Cut with a pizza cutter and dip in some Raos marinara sauce!
