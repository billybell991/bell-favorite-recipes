## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup almond flour
- 1/2 cup grated parmesan
- 2 tsp water

## Instructions

Add the almond flour and cheese to your food processor.

Pulse and combine.

Add the water one teaspoon at a time until small balls/clumps of dough start to form in the food processor.

Transfer the clumps of dough to a bowl and use your hands to form a ball of dough out of them.

Next, use a rolling pin to roll out 1/2 the dough at a time between 2 sheets of parchment paper.

Once the dough just starts to look translucent, that's when you know you've rolled it out enough.

Use a pizza cutter to cut the dough vertically and horizontally to form them into 1-inch squares.

One the crackers are shaped, use a toothpick to form a hole in the center of each cracker.

Use a pair of scissors to cut away the extra parchment paper from the dough, and then transfer the dough to a plate.

Microwave the crackers in 2 one minute intervals.  Off-set the plate on the left side for the first interval and then off-set it to the right side for the second one.

Let the crackers cool for at least 5-10 minutes after they're done cooking.

They will be soft when they come out of the microwave, but one they have time to cool they crisp up.

Once they do crisp up, break them apart into individual crackers.  Coat them with baking spray and then add the seasonings of your choice to them.

Billy's Notes:  I threw them in the air fryer for a minute to crisp them up even more.  I just put seasoned salt on them, but the linked recipe below has some other seasoning possibilities.
