## Ingredients

- 1 1/3 Cups shredded mozzarella cheese
- 1/2 Cup almond flour
- 2 Tbsp cream cheese
- 1 egg

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
2. Place thegrated mozzarella,almond mealandcream cheesein a microwave-safe bowl. Microwave on HIGH (100%) for 1 minute, stirring halfway, or until melted and combined.
3. Add theeggand working quickly, beat vigorously with a wooden spoon until combined.
4. Place the ‘dough’ between 2 pieces of parchment paper and roll to line a 32cm pizza tray.
5. Remove the top piece of baking paper and slide the dough with the bottom piece of baking paper onto the pizza tray.
6. Prick with a fork.
7. Bake for 10 mins or until puffed and golden.
