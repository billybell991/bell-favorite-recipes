## Ingredients

- 1 lb Hamburg
- 1 block Cream Cheese
- 1 can Hunt's diced tomatoes
- 1 box Beef Broth
- 1/2 Green Pepper
- 1/4 small onion

## Instructions

Brown the hamburg, green pepper, and onion.  Whip the cream cheese so it's nice and smooth.  Add the remaining ingredients until cream cheese is melted.  Serve.
