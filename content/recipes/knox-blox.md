## Ingredients

- 4 envelopes Knox Unflavoured Gelatine
- 3 pkgs. (3 oz each) flavoured gelatine
- 4 cups boiling water

## Instructions

In a large bow, combine Knox Unflavoured Gelatine and flavoured gelatine; add boiling water and stir until gelatine dissolves.  Pour into jelly roll pan (l5-1/2 “ x 10-1/2”) or large shallow baking pan.  Chill until firm.  Using cookie cutters, cut out shapes and remove carefully.  If desired, decorate with glossy decorating gel (available in 11/16 oz tubes) or with chocolate or multi-coloured long sprinkles as pictures.  (Note:- other varieties of sprinkles may melt – test before using.)

Chop gelatine trimmings and use in parfaits layered with ice cream or sherbet, or fold into whipped topping.
