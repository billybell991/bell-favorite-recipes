## Ingredients

- Potatoes, peeled
- Corn flakes
- l-l/2 tsp paprika
- 2 tsp salt
- Bacon fat

## Instructions

Crush corn flakes and mix with paprika and salt.  Cut peeled potatoes into serving size pieces, roll in melted bacon fat, then roll in corn flake mixture.  Place in a greased covered casserole and bake in a 350 degree oven for l hour.  This is delicious and different when you are tired of boiled, mashed or baked potatoes.  Be sure to prepare enough – the family will eat twice as many prepared this way.
