## Ingredients

- 12 slices white bread
- Butter (see below)
- ¼ cup dried red & green sweet pepper flakes
- ¼ cup parsley flakes
- 2 tsp cornmeal
- ½ tsp onion salt
- ½ tsp celery seed
- ½ tsp paprika
- ½ tsp salt
- 1/8 tsp garlic powder
- ¼ tsp pepper
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1 tbsp whole wheat flour (optional)
- 2 tsp flavor salt
- 1 tbsp grated Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

Butter bread on one side, arrange, buttered side up, on large cookie sheets and toast at 250 F. until golden and crisp (about 1 hour).
Buzz toasted bread slices, pepper flakes and parsley in blender until as fine as possible.
In bowl combine thoroughly the toasted buzzed crumbs with all remaining ingredients.  It is now ready to use.
NOTE: Leftover coating should be stored in plastic bag or tightly covered Tupperware container in refrigerator.
If you want to use this coating on chicken legs, thighs or breasts, do not dry them.  Shake on coating and place on buttered shallow pan and bake at 325 F. for 60 to 70 minutes. OR
Bake at 350 F. for 45 minutes turning chicken pieces over once.
