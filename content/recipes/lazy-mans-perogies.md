## Ingredients

- 15 medium-sized potatoes
- 400 gr cheddar cheese
- 1 small pkg Velveeta cheese
- 1 lb bacon, diced
- 3 large onions, diced
- 1 pkg lasagna noodles
- Sour cream
- Shred 2 pkg of cheeses together and set aside.

## Instructions

Dice bacon. Fry until almost cooked. Add diced onion and fry until golden brown. Set aside

to keep warm.

Peel and cook potatoes. When cooked, drain well. Add shredded cheeses while potatoes are

still hot. Cover and let stand for a few minutes, so cheeses melt a bit. Then mash until creamy.

Set aside to cool.

Boil lasagna noodles until cooked. In a square pan, put ½ of onion and bacon. Add layer of

noodles. Spread potatoes and cover with noodles. Then add rest of onion. Set in low oven 30

minutes. Serve with sour cream.
