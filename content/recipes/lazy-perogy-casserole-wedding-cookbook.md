## Ingredients

- 9 lasagna noodles
- 2 cups (1-500 gm container) of cottage cheese
- 1 egg
- 1/4 tsp onion salt
- 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese (old)
- 2 cups mashed potatoes
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1/8 tsp pepper
- 1/2 cup butter or margarine
- 1 cup chopped onions and bacon

## Instructions

Line a 9x13 inch pan with cooked noodles. Mix cottage cheese, egg and salt together. Spoon over noodles.

Mix cheddar cheese with potatoes, onion salt and pepper. Spread over noodles. Cover with another layer of noodles.

Melt butter, add onions and bacon and sauté. Pour over noodles. Cover and bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.

Let stand for 10 minutes. Serve with sour cream.
