## Ingredients

Mix together all of the following:

- 1 Lemon Cake Mix
- 1 Pkg. Lemon Instant Pudding
- 1 Cup of Water
- 1/2 Cup of Oil
- 4 Eggs

Topping:

- 2 Tbsp of cinnamon
- 1/2 Cup of Brown Sugar
- 1/2 Cup of Chopped Walnuts (Optional) (Billy's Note: They're gross - don't do it!)

Directions:

## Instructions

1. Grease 2 regular loaf pans.
2. Sprinkle 1/2 of the topping mixture on bottom of the pans.
3. Pour half of the cake mixture next.
4. Add the rest of the topping mixture.
5. Pour the rest of the batter on top.
6. Bake in a 350 degree oven for 50 minutes.
