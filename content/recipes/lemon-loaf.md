- 1/2 cup butter
- 1 cup white sugar
- 2 eggs, beaten
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 cup milk
- grated rind of 1 lemon

Cream butter and sugar.  Add rind.  Blend in dry ingredients alternately with milk.  Bake in greased loaf pan at 350 degrees Fahrenheit for 50 minutes.

Mix juice of 1 lemon and 1/4 cup icing sugar and drizzle over top while still hot.
