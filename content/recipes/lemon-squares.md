## Ingredients

- 1 cup shortening
- 2 cups flour
- ½ cup icing sugar

## Instructions

Mix until all crumbly.  Lightly grease a 9”x 13” pan.  Press in pan and bake for 20 minutes at 350 degrees.  (Looks watery).

Topping:

4 eggs	2 cups white sugar
½ tsp salt	1 tsp baking powder
4 tbsp lemon juice

Mix (not cook) – pour on crust and bake 18 minutes at 350 degrees.
