## Ingredients

- 3 medium potatoes, peeled and cubed
- 2 Tbsp butter
- 1 small onion, chopped
- 2 Tbsp flour
- 1 cup milk
- Celery salt and garlic to taste (optional)
- 2 slices processed cheese

## Instructions

Boil potatoes with seasonings in enough water to cover, until cooked. Mash potatoes with their cooking water. They should be slightly liquefied. In small saucepan, melt butter. Add flour to form a smooth paste. Stir butter mixture into mashed potatoes. Over low heat, gradually stir milk into potato mixture. Add cheese; stir until melted. Makes 3 to 4 servings.

If soup becomes too thick when cooled, a little more milk can be added when reheated.

Keeps in refrigerator for 3 days.

Recipe doubles easily.
