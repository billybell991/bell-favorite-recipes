## Ingredients

- 6 cups shredded cabbage
- 1 cup shredded carrot
- ¼ cup chopped green pepper
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 2 Tbsp sugar
- 2 Tbsp vinegar
- 1 tsp prepared mustard
- 1 tsp celery seed
- ½ tsp salt

## Instructions

Combine veggies and chill. Mix with slaw mix and toss well.

Serves 12.
