## Ingredients

- 2 Medium baking potatoes, unpeeled
- 1 can (10 fl oz.) chicken broth
- 1 cup milk
- 3 slices of cooked bacon (can be the ready-to-serve microwave stuff)
- 3/4 cup of shredded cheddar cheese, divided
- 2 Tbsp sliced green onions, divided
- 1/4 cup sour cream

## Instructions

1. Pierce potatoes; microwave on HIGH 5 mins., turning over after 2 1/2 mins.  Cut potatoes in chunks.  Combine potatoes, broth, and milk in large microwaveable bowl.  Microwave on HIGH 6 mins., stirring after 3 min.  Slightly crush potatoes with a potato masher.
2. Reserve 2 Tbsp. each of the bacon and cheese and 1 Tbsp. of the onions for topping; set aside.   Add remaining bacon, cheese, and onions to soup.
3. Serve topped with reserved bacon, cheese, onions, and sour cream.
