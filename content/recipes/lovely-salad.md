Cousins of the Groom

- 1 green pepper
- 1 red pepper
- 2 green onions
- 2 celery stalks
- ¼ lb mushrooms
- ¼ lb spinach (1 bag)
- 1 cup cooked rice
- 4 ozs cashew nuts, optional
- 1 tbsp fresh parsley

Dressing:

## Ingredients

- ½ cup cooking oil
- ¼ cup soya sauce
- 1 garlic clove, minced

## Instructions

Marinate dressing for one hour before using. Add to above salad 5-10 minutes before serving.
