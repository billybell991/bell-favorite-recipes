## Ingredients

- ½ cup raw long grain rice
- 1-3/4 cup peeled and chopped tomatoes (or use one 14-oz can)
- 1 small mild onion, chopped
- 1 green pepper, chopped
- 1-1/2 tsp salt – divided
- 3 thick loin pork chops
- 1 cup hot water (or 1 can consommé)
- 2 tbsp chicken bouillon cordial – see below

## Instructions

Measure raw rice into large shallow casserole. Stir tomatoes into it. (If time permits let stand 1

hour to soak rice.) Cover with layer of the onions then layer of the green pepper. Sprinkle with

1 tsp salt. Place chops on top.

Mix bouillon cordial with hot water and pour over all. Sprinkle remaining ½ tsp salt over all.

(If desired use one 10-oz can consommé instead of cordial and hot water) Cover, using foil if

you do not have a lid. Bake at 350 degrees 1 hour, removing cover during last ½ hour to

brown top. The rice is tender and the combination of vegetables delicious.

VARIATIONS: 1) Use ½ to ¾ pound sausages instead of chops. 2) Use ½ to ¾ pound ground

chuck made into three meat cakes instead of chops. 3) You could cover the vegetables with a

layer of ground chuck omitting the copes, to make this cheaper and quicker.

THIS SERVES THREE; DOUBLE THIS QUANTITY TO SERVE 6.
