Roll out one portion of the Sweet Yeast Bread (recipe foundHERE) to a large rectangle 14” x 10”. Spread with the following mixture to within about ½” of the edge.

## Ingredients

- ¼ cup Bee Hive Corn Syrup
- 2 tbsp St. Lawrence Oil
- ¼ cup brown sugar
- 1 tsp cinnamon

## Instructions

Roll up tightly starting at the long side of the rectangle. Into each of 12 muffins pour 1 tsp St. Lawrence Oil and 1 tsp

Bee Hive Corn Syrup. Place 3 to 5 half pecans or walnuts and half a maraschino cherry in the bottom of each tin.

Cut the roll into 1-1/2” slices and place cut side up in muffin tin. Let rise in a warm place until doubled in size and then bake in a moderate oven, 375 degrees F for 15 minutes. Remove from tins almost immediately after they are taken our of the oven.  Makes 12 Chelsea buns.
