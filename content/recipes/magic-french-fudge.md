## Ingredients

- 3 cups (one 500-gr pkg) semi-sweet chocolate chips
- 1 can Eagle Brand Milk
- Dash salt
- 1-1/2 tsp vanilla extract
- ½ cup chopped nuts, optional

## Instructions

In top of double boiler, melt chocolate over boiling water, stirring occasionally. Remove from heat, stir in sweetened condensed milk, salt, vanilla and nuts.  Spread mixture evenly onto waxed paper lined 8 x 8” baking pan. Chill 2 hours or until firm. Turn fudge onto cutting board. Peel off paper and cut into squares.  Tightly cover any leftovers.
