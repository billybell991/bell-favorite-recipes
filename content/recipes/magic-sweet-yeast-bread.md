Combine in a cup:

- 1/2 cup lukewarm Water
- 2 tsp sugar

Sprinkle over the top:

- 2 pkg dry yeast

Let soften for about 15 minutes.

Scald in a saucepan over boiling water:

- 2/3 cup Milk

Mix in & Set aside to cool:

- ½ cup St. Lawrence Oil
- ½ cup Bee Hive Corn Syrup

Measure and sift together:

## Ingredients

- 5-1/2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 tsp salt
- ¼ cup sugar

## Instructions

Combine with the milk, corn oil and syrup mixture …5 beaten eggs and 2 cups of the sifted flour, sugar, salt mixture. Mix to give a thick batter. Beat vigorously and stir in yeast.

Add remaining flour and stir until smooth. Place in a lightly oiled bowl, cover with wax paper and a cloth and let rise in a warm place until doubled in size. Punch down, knead lightly on a floured board and divide the dough into 2 parts.

This basic sweet rich dough may be used for making your own coffee cakes or sweet rolls. Try the following variation – on the following sheet.
