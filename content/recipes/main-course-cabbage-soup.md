## Ingredients

- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 – 28 oz can tomatoes
- 1 medium onion
- 2 – 5-1/2 oz cans tomato paste
- ½ cup diced celery
- 6 cups hot water
- ½ green pepper, chopped
- 2 beef or chicken bouillon cubes
- 2 tbsp oil
- 3 tbsp chopped parsley
- 2 tsp salt
- 2 cups raw potatoes, diced
- 2 tbsp sugar
- 1 cup raw carrot, diced
- 1/8 tsp pepper
- ¼ tsp baking soda
- 6 cups cabbage, coarsely chopped

## Instructions

In a very large soup pot, sauté ground beef, onion, celery and green pepper in the oil until redness is gone from meat.  Break meat up as it sautés.  Add remaining ingredients, except cabbage, and combine thoroughly.  Simmer uncovered for 1 hr, stirring occasionally.  Add cabbage and simmer covered, for 1 hr. longer.  If soup is too thick, more water may be added.

Serve generous amounts in large soup bowls with whole grain bread.  Serves 8 – 10.
