## Ingredients

- CAKE: 1 cup sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 tbsp apricot jam
- 1 cup flour
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp baking powder
- generous pinch of salt
- 1 tbsp butter
- 1 tsp vinegar
- 1 cup milk
- SAUCE 1 cup cream
- 6 oz butter
- 1 cup sugar
- ½ cup hot water

## Instructions

Beat the egg and sugar well in a mixer.  Add the jam, sift flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt.  Melt the butter and add the vinegar.  Add liquids to egg mixture alternately with the flour.  Beat well and bake in a covered dish at 350 degrees F. for 45 minute to 1 hour.  It is important to use a covered dish otherwise sauce won’t sink into the cake.

Melt together the ingredients for the sauce and pour over the pudding as it comes out of the oven.

This is a delicious dessert, but rich!!
