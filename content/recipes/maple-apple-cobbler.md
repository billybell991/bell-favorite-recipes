## Ingredients

- 2-1/2 Tbsp cornstarch
- 1 cup apple juice
- ¼ cup Ontario Maple Syrup
- 1 Tbsp lemon juice
- 1-1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 6 Ontario apples, unpeeled, cored, sliced
- 1/3 cup raisins
- ¼ cup milk
- 1 egg
- 1-1/4 cup biscuit mix
- 1 tsp sugar

## Instructions

Combine cornstarch, apple juice, maple syrup, lemon juice and 1 tsp of cinnamon. Place apples and raisins in a 2-1/2 quart shallow baking dish. Pour juice mixture over top. Bake at 400 F. (200 C.) for 20 minutes.

With fork, blend milk and egg. Stir into biscuit mix just until moistened. Drop from large spoon into 6 mounds on top of fruit. Combine sugar and remaining cinnamon, sprinkle over biscuit. Bake 20 to 25 minutes longer or until browned and bubbling.

Serve warm. Makes 6 servings.

NOTE: Good made with McIntosh apples.
