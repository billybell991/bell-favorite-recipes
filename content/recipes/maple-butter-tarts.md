## Ingredients

- 1 recipe pastry
- 1/3 cup butter or margarine
- ½ cup maple syrup
- ¼ tsp nutmeg
- ½ cup brown sugar
- ½ cup raisins or walnuts
- 2 eggs, slightly beaten

## Instructions

Prepare pastry.  Line medium tart tins.  Combine remaining ingredients.  Mix well.  Fill tart tins 2/3 full.  Bake at 350 degrees for about 25-30 minutes.  Makes about 16 medium tarts.
