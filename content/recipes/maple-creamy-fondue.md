## Ingredients

- ½ cup pure maple syrup
- 2 cups 15% cream
- 2 tsp cornstarch
- firm fruits and berries cut into pieces

## Instructions

Gently heat maple syrup in a pan for 5 minutes. Dilute the cornstarch in 2 tsp of cream. Bring the remaining cream to a boil and add to the maple syrup. Blend the cornstarch into the syrup mixture and stir continuously over gentle heat until thickened.

Serves four to six. Exquisite!
