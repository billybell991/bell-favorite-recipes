## Ingredients

- 2 Tbsp dry white wine
- 5 Tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp thyme
- 1 tsp tarragon
- 1 tsp chopped parsley
- Salt & pepper to taste

## Instructions

Mix all ingredients of the marinade together. Place steaks in a shallow dish and pour marinade over. Let stand for at least 2 hours, turning steaks once.

Remove steaks from the marinade and season with salt and pepper. Cook on a very hot grill to your liking.

Enough marinade for 6 steaks.
