## Ingredients

- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 tin mushroom soup
- ½ cup chopped onions
- 1 tbsp parsley flakes
- 1 tsp salt
- ¼ tsp pepper
- ½ tsp sage
- 3 tbsp ketchup
- 1 tbsp barbecue sauce
- ½ tsp garlic powder
- ½ cup rolled oats

## Instructions

Combine all ingredients in large bowl. Mix well. Pat down in 1-1/2 qt casserole. Bake

covered in 350 degree F oven for 50 minutes. Remove cover and bake about 15 minutes to

brown. Serves 4.
