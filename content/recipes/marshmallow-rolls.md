## Ingredients

- 30 lg or 120 mini colored marshmallows
- 1-1/2 cup Eagle Brand milk
- 2 cups graham cracker crumbs or vanilla wafers
- 2/3 cup maraschino cherries (drained)
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 2-1/2 cup coconut (fine cut)
- Few grains salt

## Instructions

Blend marshmallows, milk, crumbs or wafers, salt, vanilla and cherries.  Butter your hands and form ingredients into rolls.  Roll rolls in coconut.  Place in wax paper.  Refrigerate to cure for 24 hours.  Freeze and slice off in just the right size rounds.
