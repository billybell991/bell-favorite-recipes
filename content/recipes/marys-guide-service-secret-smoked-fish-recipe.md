## Ingredients

- 4 cups water
- 2 cups soya sauce
- 2 cups apple juice
- 1 cup packed brown sugar
- ½ cup pickling salt
- 2 tbsp whole black peppercorns
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp onion powder
- 3 lbs lake trout, or substitute fillets (8 oz each), 1 inch thick, skin on

Same instructions as recipe for Smoked Whitefish.
