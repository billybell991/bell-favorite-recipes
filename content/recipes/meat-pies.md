Submitted by Del Gosson

Aunt of the Bride

Filling:

- 3 lbs of beef, or cheaper cut of steak
- 2 lbs of pork, remove fat
- 2 double chicken breasts, cooked for about 20 minutes
- 3 large onions
- 1 tbsp salt
- 1 tsp pepper
- ½ cup flour

Pie Crust:

## Ingredients

- 1 lb of shortening

## Instructions

Follow pastry recipe on shortening box. Add 2 extra cups of flour and enough water to hold the pastry together.

Finely chop all of the meat. Put in a pot and cover with water. Bring to a boil . Add 2 chopped onions and pepper. Let simmer for about 2 hours. Add the last chopped onion and the salt, simmer for another hour.

Mix flour and water in a separate bowl. Add to meat mixture until thick.

Let cool overnight. Prepare pie crust and fill pies or turnovers.  Will make 20-25 turnovers. Bake at 375 degrees for about ½ hour.
