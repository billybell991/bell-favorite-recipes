## Ingredients

- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1/3 cup sugar
- 2 Tbsp lemon juice
- ¼ cup vinegar
- 1/3 cup ketchup
- ½ cup Canola oil

## Instructions

Measure all ingredients into a bottle or jar.

Cover tightly and shake well, chill thoroughly.

Shake well before serving.

Yield: 1-1/4 cups.
