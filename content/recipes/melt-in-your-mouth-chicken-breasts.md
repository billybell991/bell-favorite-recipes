## Ingredients

- 4 boneless chicken breasts
- 1 cup Greek yogurt (or mayo if you're going a little less healthy)
- 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
- 1/2 tsp seasoned salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1 tsp garlic powder

## Instructions

1. Mix mayonnaise or yogurt, cheese and seasonings.
2. Spread mixture over chicken breasts and place in baking dish.
3. Bake at 375°F for 30 minutes.
