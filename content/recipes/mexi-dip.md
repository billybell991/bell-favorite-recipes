## Ingredients

- 500 mL sour cream
- 1 pkg taco seasoning mix
- 1 tomato, diced
- shredded lettuce
- 1-2 green onions, chopped
- ½ lb grated cheddar cheese

## Instructions

Layer:

Mix sour cream with taco seasoning mix. On top of sour cream mixture, add cheese, lettuce, diced tomato, and onions.

Serve with taco chips. Easyandyummy!
