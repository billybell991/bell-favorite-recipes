## Ingredients

- 1 can of black beans, drained and rinsed
- 1 can of corn, drained
- 1/2 cup of salsa (no sugar added)
- 2 Tbsp non-fat Greek yogurt (or sour cream)
- salt and pepper to taste

Optional ingredients I've tried:

- Cilantro (chopped up) - added a good lemony and more of a sunny vacation-y flavour to it
- Chopped up jalapenos - little more zing is never a bad idea!

Pour the beans + corn into a bowl.

## Instructions

2. Then add salt and mix it well.

3. Add salsa + greek yogurt. Mix.

4. Serve immediately or store in an air-tight container for up to 4 days.

Billy's notes:  This was really good to eat just on its own or I even put it on eggs and a bed of salad for a homemade burrito bowl.  Might toss some cut up jalapenos in it next time.
