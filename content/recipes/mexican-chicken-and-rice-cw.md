## Ingredients

- 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch pieces
- 1 medium green pepper, chopped
- 1 small onion, chopped
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- 1 8-3/4 oz tin kernel corn, drained
- 1 cup chicken broth
- 1 cup salsa
- ½ to 1 tsp salt
- ½ to 1 tsp chili powder, optional
- ¼ tsp pepper
- 1-1/2 cups uncooked instant rice
- ½ to 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

## Instructions

In  large skillet, sauté the chicken, green pepper and onion in oil until chicken is no longer pink and vegetables are crisp-tender.  Add the corn, broth, salsa, salt, chili powder if desired and pepper.  Bring to a boil.

Stir in the rice.  Cover and remove from the heat.  Let stand for 5 minutes or until the cheese is melted.

YIELD:  6 servings.
