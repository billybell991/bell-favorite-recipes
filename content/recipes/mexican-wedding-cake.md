- 2 cups flour
- 2 cups white sugar
- 2 tsp baking soda
- 2 tsp vanilla
- 1 cup walnuts
- 2 eggs
- 19-oz tin crushed pineapple

Mix and bake in a 9”x 9” greased pan in a preheated 350 degree oven for 50 – 60 minutes.

Ice with cream cheese frosting:

## Ingredients

- 8 oz cream cheese (250 gr.)
- ¼ cup butter
- 1 tsp vanilla
