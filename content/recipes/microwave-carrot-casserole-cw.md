## Ingredients

- 1-1/2 lbs carrots, shredded
- ¼ cup water
- 1 10-3/4 oz tin condensed cream of celery soup (undiluted)
- 1 cup (4 oz) shredded cheddar cheese
- 1 medium onion, chopped
- ¼ cup milk
- ½ tsp ground mustard
- 2 tbsp dried bread crumbs
- 1 tbsp melted butter or margarine

## Instructions

In a 1-1/2 qt microwave-safe dish, combine carrots and water. Cover and microwave on high for 6 – 8 minutes or until crisp-tender, stirring once; drain and return to dish.

In a bowl, combine the soup, cheese, onion, milk and mustard.  Pout over carrots; stir to coat.  Cover and microwave on high for 2 minutes.  Toss the bread crumbs and butter.  Sprinkle over the top.

Cover and microwave on high 2 minutes longer.  Let stand for 2 minutes.

YIELD:  4 servings.

NOTE: This recipe was tested in an 850-watt microwave.
