WHITE SAUCE;

- 1 cup milk
- 4 tbsp flour

Cook as for white sauce, and let cool.

BEAT WELL;

## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter or margarine
- 1 cup white sugar
- 1 tsp vanilla.

## Instructions

When sauce is cooled, add the butter and sugar mixture, and BEAT WELL.

Makes enough for a layer cake.
