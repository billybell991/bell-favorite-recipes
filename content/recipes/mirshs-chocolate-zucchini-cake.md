## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- ¾ tsp nutmeg
- ¼ tsp salt
- ¼ cup cocoa
- 3 eggs
- 2 cups sugar
- ½ cup oil
- ¾ cup buttermilk  OR 2 Tbsp vinegar
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 2 cups grated zucchini

## Instructions

Mix all together. Bake in 350 degree F. oven for 40 – 60 minutes.
