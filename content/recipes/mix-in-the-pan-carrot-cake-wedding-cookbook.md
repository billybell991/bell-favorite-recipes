Submitted by Jeannine and Elmer Bell

Parents of the Groom

- 1 cup flour
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1 ½ cup shredded carrots
- ½ cup oil
- ½ tsp vanilla
- ¾ cup sugar
- ¼ tsp nutmeg
- 2 eggs, slightly beaten
- ¼ cup water
- icing sugar, optional
- ½ tsp each of salt, baking soda and baking powder

With fork, in an 8 or 9 inch square pan, mix flour, sugar, cinnamon, salt, baking soda, baking powder and nutmeg. Add carrots, eggs, oil, water and vanilla.

Mix briskly with fork until thoroughly blended. Bake in preheated, 350 degree oven for about 30 minutes or until pick inserted in centre comes out clean and cake pulls away from sides of pan.

Cool in pan on rack. Sprinkle with icing sugar or ice with cream cheese icing.

Cream Cheese Icing:

## Ingredients

- ¼ cup softened butter
- 1 tsp vanilla
- dash of salt
- 2 ½ cups icing sugar
- 4 oz (125 gr) softened cream cheese

## Instructions

Into cream cheese and butter, beat the vanilla and salt. Gradually add the icing sugar and blend well.

Note:This recipe makes a little more icing than “Mix-In-The-Pan Carrot Cake” needs. If you spread it on thin, you can probably ice two cakes.
