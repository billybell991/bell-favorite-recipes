## Ingredients

- 1-1/2 cup brown sugar
- ½ tsp vanilla
- ½ cup flour
- 1 pie shell, uncooked
- 1-1/4 cup cream

## Instructions

Mix all ingredients.  Pour in pie crust.  Bake at 425 degrees for 10 minutes.  Reduce heat to 350 degrees and bake another 25 – 30 minutes until filling is set.  Cool before serving

Tastes great with scoop of ice cream.
