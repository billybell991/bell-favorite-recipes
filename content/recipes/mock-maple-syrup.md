## Ingredients

- 2 cups light brown sugar
- 1 cup boiling water
- ½ tsp maple flavoring

## Instructions

Combine sugar and water, bring to boiling point and simmer 5 minutes.  Remove from heat, add flavouring and stir well.  Store in covered jar.

Makes about 2-1/2 cups.
