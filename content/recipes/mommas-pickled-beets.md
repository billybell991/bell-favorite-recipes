## Ingredients

- About 5 lbs beets
- 1-1/2 tbsp pickling spices
- 2 cups white vinegar
- 2 cups white sugar
- 2 cups water
- 1-1/2 tbsp coarse salt

## Instructions

Prepare beets by boiling till tender.  Mix and bring to a boil the vinegar, sugar, salt, water and spices (tied in a bag if used).  Boil for 5 minutes and keep hot.

Peel beets and, if they are large, cut them into bite-size chunks into hot sterilized jars.  Pour hot brine solution on top, filling to ½ inch from top.  Wipe rim of jars and put on prepared lids.  Tighten screw top just fingertip tight.

Makes enough brine for 4 quarts.
