## Ingredients

- 2 lbs. navy beans
- 1 onion, cut in two
- ¼ lb. salt pork
- ¾ cup brown sugar
- ¼ cup catsup
- 3 Tbsp. dry mustard
- 4 Tbsp. molasses
- Salt & Pepper to taste
- ¼ cup shortening, in chunks

## Instructions

This is sort of the recipe for baked beans that I (Mom) started with. They do not have to be boiled beforehand. This recipe can be altered to many ways – every cook puts his/her own touch to it.

I (Mom) have lost the knack of doing beans because my best “pal” (Dad) took over the job and did much better job of baking beans. Also, if you want good beans, look up Rob’s/Bob’s baked beans – they are especially delicious.
