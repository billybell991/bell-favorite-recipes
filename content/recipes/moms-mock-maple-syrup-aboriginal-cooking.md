## Ingredients

- 2 cups brown sugar
- 2 cups white sugar
- 2 cups water
- 1 tsp margarine or butter
- ½ tsp maple extract

## Instructions

Combine brown sugar, white sugar and water. Bring to a boil and simmer over medium heat – stirring constantly – for half an hour.
Stir in margarine or butter and maple extract. Ready to serve over your pancakes.
