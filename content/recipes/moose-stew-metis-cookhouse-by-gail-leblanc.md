## Ingredients

- 3 lbs moose, cut in stewing pieces
- 2 carrots
- ¼ lb butter or margarine
- 2 parsnips
- 6 cups water
- 1 small turnip
- Salt & Pepper to taste
- 10 medium potatoes

## Instructions

Brown moose meat in hot butter or margarine. Add water, salt & pepper. Simmer 30 minutes then add chopped onions and cook on low for one hour.

But up and add 2 carrots, 2 parsnips, 1 small turnip and 10 medium potatoes and cook for 30 minutes or until vegetables are tender.
