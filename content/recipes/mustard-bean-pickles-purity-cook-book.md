## Instructions

Wash and trim ends from 1 (6-quart) basket young yellow string beans.
Cut into 1” lengths. This amount will measure about 16 cups prepared beans. Cook in boiling salted water until just tender.
MUSTARD SAUCE:  Mix together in a large sauepan or kettle 3 cups sugar,
1 Tbsp turmeric
2 tsp  salt
½ cup dry mustard
½ cup all-purpose flour
Blend in ½ cup vinegar and 1 cup water.

Heat to boiling point 2-1/2 cups vinegar and 1 tsp. celery seeds.

Stir hot liquid slowly into flour mixture. Place over moderate heat, stirring until thickened and smooth (about 5 – 10 minutes. Add drained beans; bring mixture to boiling point. Ladle into hot sterilized sealers and seal.

Yield: About 6 pints.
