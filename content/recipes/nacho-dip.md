Cousin of the Bride

## Ingredients

- 1 package Philadelphia cream cheese
- 2 tbsp mayonnaise
- 1 small jar salsa
- 2 green onions, finely chopped
- 1 tomato, finely chopped
- approximately 2 cups finely grated cheddar cheese (marble
- works well)

## Instructions

Cream Philly and mayonnaise together, spread over bottom of dish, not quite to the edges.  Pour salsa over cream cheese mixture.  Sprinkle chopped green onions and tomato over salsa.  Cover with grated cheddar cheese.

Chill. Serve with tortilla chips.
