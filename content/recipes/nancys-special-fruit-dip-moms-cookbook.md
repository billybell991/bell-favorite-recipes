## Ingredients

- 250 gr. Cream cheese, softened
- ½ cup sour cream
- ¼ cup white sugar
- ¼ cup packed brown sugar
- 1 – 2 Tbsp real maple syrup

## Instructions

Mix all ingredients thoroughly. Garnish with maple sugar leaves, if desired.
