## Ingredients

- Prep. Time  -  15 minutes
- Microwave: -  2 minutes
- 2 Tbsp butter or margarine
- 3 cups miniature marshmallows
- 4 cups Post Fruity, Cocoa or Cinna-Crunch Pebbles Cereal
- Baker’s Angel Flake Coconut

## Instructions

Grease Muffin Pan.  Put butter or margarine in a 4-quart microwave-able bowl sprayed with no-stick spray on HIGH 3 seconds or until melted. Add marshmallows, Stir to coat. Microwave 1-1/2 minutes or until marshmallows are melted and smooth, stirring after 45 seconds. Add cereal. Stir with wooden spoon  to coat well.

Press cereal mixture firmly into prepared muffin pan to form 24 nests. Cool completely. Remove nests from pan.

Fill nests with coconut and candies.
