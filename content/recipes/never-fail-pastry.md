## Ingredients

- 5 cups flour
- 1 tsp baking powder
- ¾ tsp salt
- 3 tbsp brown sugar
- 1 lb shortening
- 1 egg, slightly beaten
- cold water
- 2 tbsp vinegar

## Instructions

Sift flour, baking powder and salt together. Add brown sugar. Cut in shortening until fine as meal. Slightly beat egg in measuring cup. Add cold water until egg and water mixture measures ¾ cup. Add vinegar. Drizzle egg mixture over flour mixture and stir with a fork until it is well mixed.

Divide into 8 equal portions This pastry can be used for dessert pies or meat pies.
