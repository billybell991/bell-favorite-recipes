## Ingredients

- 5 cups flour
- 2 tbsp white sugar
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 2 tbsp vinegar
- 1 egg
- 1 lb lard

## Instructions

Sift dry ingredients.  Add lard and cut into the flour until lard is in very small pieces.  In an 8 oz cup, beat egg.  Add vinegar and fill cup with cold water.  Combine wet and dry mixtures.  Use plenty of flour when rolling pie crust.  Store unused pie crust in  covered container in the refrigerator or separate into smaller batches and freeze.
