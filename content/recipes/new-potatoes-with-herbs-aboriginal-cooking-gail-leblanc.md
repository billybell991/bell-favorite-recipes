## Ingredients

- 1 lb tiny new potatoes (about 20)
- 1 Tbs chopped chives
- 1 Tbs chopped fresh tarragon/basil
- 1 Tbs lemon juice
- 2 Tbs olive oil or vegetable oil
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

In saucepan, boil unpeeled potatoes until tender, about 15 minutes. Drain. Add tarragon, chives, lemon juice and oil. Add salt and pepper to taste.

Makes 4 servings.
