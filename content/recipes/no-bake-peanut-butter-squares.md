## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter or margarine
- ½ cup lightly-packed brown sugar
- ½ cup corn syrup.

## Instructions

Mix and bring to a boil.

Add:  1 cup peanut butter chocolate chips.  Melt over medium heat til smooth.
Remove from heat and add 6 cups corn flakes.  Mix well.

Pour into a greased 9 x 13 pan.  Melt rest of chipits and add some chocolate chipits to make a cup.  Spread on squares.
