Cousin of the Bride

## Ingredients

- 1 ½  cups white rice
- 1-10 oz can condensed celery soup
- 1-10 oz can condensed mushroom soup
- 1 pkg dried onion soup
- 1 cup milk
- 4-5 skinless chicken breasts

## Instructions

Mix soup, milk and rice.  Place mixture in a casserole dish.  Lay chicken on top. Cover with foil and cook at 325 degrees for 2 hours.  Do not peek!
