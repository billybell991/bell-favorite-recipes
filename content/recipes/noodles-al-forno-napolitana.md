Get supper on the table in a flash using only six ingredients. Toss a salad, warm up an Italian bread stick and you have dinner fit for royalty in about 30 minutes.

## Ingredients

- 1 lb broad egg noodles
- 1 19-oz can tomato sauce
- 4 cups ricotta cheese
- ¼ cup sliced green onion
- 1 tsp dried basil
- 1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

Butter a 13 x 9-inch baking pan. Set aside. Cook pasta according to package directions. Drain well and return to pot. Stir tomato sauce, ricotta cheese, green onion and basil into pasta.

Spoon pasta mixture into prepared baking pan.

Sprinkle Parmesan cheese evenly over casserole. Bake in a 350-degree oven for 25 minutes or until hot and bubbly.

Makes 8 servings.
