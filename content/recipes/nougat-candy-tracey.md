## Ingredients

- 2 Tbsp butter
- 2 Bags of white chocolate chips (225g each)
- 1 Bag white mini marshmallows (400g)
- 1 1/2 cups chopped baking gumdrops (350g of jujubes)

## Instructions

1. Make large double boiler with pot and over proof bowl.
2. Over simmered water melt butter, chocolate chips, and marshmallows.
3. Stir in gumdrops.
4. Transfer to oval baking dish lined with parchment paper.
5. Pat down.  If too sticky use sprayed parchment.
6. Leave in fridge overnight.  Cut next day.
