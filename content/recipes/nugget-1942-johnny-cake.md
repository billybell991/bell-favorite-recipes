## Ingredients

- 2/3 cup yellow cornmeal
- 1 cup stirred but unsifted cake flour
- 5 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 cup milk
- 1 egg
- 2 Tbsp corn or other cooking oil
- 1/3 cup corn syrup
- 1 Tbsp butter or drippings

## Instructions

Turn the oven to 425 degrees. Next put the sifter over a bowl and into it measure the cornmeal, flour, baking powder and salt. Sift into bowl. At this stage measure the butter or drippings into a 9” x 9” pan and put it in the oven to heat up to sizzling point. To dry ingredients add egg, milk, oil and corn syrup and stir just enough to mix. Carefully remove hot pan from oven and pour batter into sizzling hot fat. Return to oven at 425 degrees and bake 20 minutes.

Cut into squares and serve as a hot bread with your main course OR serve as dessert with butter and maple or corn syrup. Your kids will drool over this.
