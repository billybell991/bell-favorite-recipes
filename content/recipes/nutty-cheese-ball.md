## Ingredients

- ½ lb grated med. Cheddar Cheese (about 2 cups)
- ½ cup milk
- 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
- 8 oz softened cream cheese
- ½ tsp garlic salt
- 3 oz crumbled blue cheese
- ¾ cup finely chopped walnuts

## Instructions

Mix in a 2-quart bowl, cheeses, milk, Worcestershire sauce and garlic salt.  Chill if necessary before shaping into a ball.  Roll in walnuts.  Chill until firm (about 3 – 5 hours).  Serve with crackers.
