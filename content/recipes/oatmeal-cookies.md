- 1 cup margarine or butter
- 1-1/2 cup brown sugar
- 1 egg

Mix above ingredients well, then add:

## Ingredients

- 1-1/2 cup flour
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1-1/4 cup oatmeal

## Instructions

Mix well and bake in 350 degree oven ‘til golden brown.

This recipe came from Florence Burton, who had gotten it from Roland Lejambe.
