## Ingredients

- 1-1/2 cups rolled oats
- 2 cups 2% milk
- ½ cup white flour
- ½ cup whole wheat flour
- 1-1/2 Tbsp brown sugar
- 1-1/2 Tbsp baking powder
- ½ tsp salt
- ½ tsp cinnamon
- 3 eggs (beaten)
- ¼ cup melted butter/margarine/Crisco

## Instructions

Mix oats and milk. Set aside until milk is absorbed.
Flour mixture: combine flour, sugar, baking powder, salt and cinnamon. Mix well.
Add eggs and melted butter to oats mixture. Beat well. Add oat mixture to flour mixture, mixing until smooth. Pour batter by ¼ cupfuls onto preheated lightly greased griddle. Bake until fluffy and bubbles break on surface.
Pour natural maple syrup over and enjoy. Makes 12 – 15 pancakes.
