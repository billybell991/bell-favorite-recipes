- 3/4 brown sugar
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 3/4 cup butter
- 2 eggs
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 2 cups quick cooking oats
- 1 cup raisins

Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit.

In a medium sized bowl blend together sugars and butter until light and fluffy.

Add eggs and vanilla; mix thoroughly.

In a separate bowl sift together flour, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon; stir into wet mixture.  Add oatmeal and raisins; stir until mixed.

Drop by heaping tablespoonfuls, two inches apart onto lined cookie sheet.  Bake for 10-12 minutes.
