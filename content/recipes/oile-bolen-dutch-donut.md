Submitted by Audrey and John Gosson

Cousins of the Bride

## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- ½ cup sugar
- ½ cup corn syrup
- 4 ½ cups flour
- 5 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp nutmeg
- 3 tbsp melted shortening or salad oil
- ½ cup milk
- apples chopped in small pieces, raisins and candied fruit-
- should equal 2 cups total

## Instructions

Beat egg yolks until thick. Add sugar gradually and then corn syrup. Mix shortening or oil with milk and stir into the egg and sugar mixture.

Add all but ¼ cup of dry ingredients. Stir just enough to blend. Do not beat. Fold in stiffly beaten egg whites, and remaining dry ingredients. Add fruit.

Drop by heaping tablespoonfuls into hot, deep frying fat (about 370 degrees) until golden brown. They will turn by themselves.

Drain on paper towel. Yields 4 dozen.
