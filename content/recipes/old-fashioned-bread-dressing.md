## Old-Fashioned Bread Dressing (Stuffing)

## Ingredients

- ½ cup chopped onion
- ½ cup butter or margarine
- 1 tsp poultry seasoning
- ½ tsp salt
- 1/8 tsp pepper
- 8 cups dry bread cubes1 cup chicken broth or water

NOTE:  (I (Mom) use 1 tsp chicken bouillon powder in 1 cup boiling water.)

Saute onions in margarine.  Add all other ingredients and mix well.  This amount stuffs a 10-lb turkey or can be heated in a greased foil-covered bread pan in a 325 degree oven for 40-45 minutes if it has been refrigerated, or at a lower temperature for a longer period.
