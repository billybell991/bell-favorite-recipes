## Ingredients

- 1 (8 oz/250g) pkg cream cheese, softened
- 2 cloves of garlic, minced
- 2 tbsp dried oregano leaves
- 1 tsp (or less) of black pepper
- 2 cups cooked, diced chicken breast (about 2 5oz breasts)
- 1 cup finely sliced strips of seeded zucchini (about 1 small)
- 1/2 cup sun-dried tomatoes, reconstituted, thinly sliced
- 1/4 cup mayonnaise
- 1 1/2 tsp grated lemon peel (1/2 a lemon)
- 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1 Parisan bread (baguette?)

## Instructions

1. Mix cream cheese, garlic, oregano, and black pepper together using an electric mixer until well blended.  Set aside.
2. In a separate bowl, stir remaining ingredients together, except the baguette, and set aside.
3. Slice the baguette lengthwise from the side but not all the way through.  Carefully scoop out bread from inside using a spoon.
4. Completely coat inside of baguette with cream cheese mixture ensuring the edges are well covered, reserving 2 tablespoons of the mixture.  Fill baguette evently with chicken mixture.
5. Close baguette, using the reserved cream cheese mixture to coat the cut edges.  Wrap very tightly with plastic wrap to keep baguette edges closed.  Chill in the refrigerator for 12 hours or overnight.
6. Before serving, unwrap baguette and cut 1" wide slices with a serrated knife.  Transfer slices to serving platter and add garnish.
