## Ingredients

- 1 square box of vanilla ice cream
- 1 container of Cool Whip

## Instructions

Cut ice cream up into ½ to 1 inch thick pieces to fill the bottom of a 9 inch square pan or a 9x13 inch pan.

Crush OREO cookies until crumbly. Spread on top of ice cream. Pour chocolate syrup over cookies.

Spread Cool Whip over top. Sprinkle more crushed cookies over Cool Whip to decorate.

Cover with Saran wrap and place in freezer for at least a day before serving.

OREO cookies and chocolate sauce can be substituted for Skor chocolate bars and caramel sauce.
