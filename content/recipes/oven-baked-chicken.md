- 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
- 1 tsp paprika
- 3/4 tsp seasoned salt
- 3/4 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp pepper
- 12 chicken drumsticks, skin removed

Combine first 5 ingredients in large resealable freezer bag. Dampen chicken with water.  Add a few pieces to bag.  Seal bag.  Shake until chicken is well coated.  Arrange chicken in single layer on greased baking sheet.  Repeat with remaining chicken and spice mixture.  Bake in 425 degree Fahrenheit oven for 30 minutes.  Turn chicken.  Bake for about 15 minutes until juices run clear.  Serves 4.
