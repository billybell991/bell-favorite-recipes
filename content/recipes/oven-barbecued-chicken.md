## Ingredients

Preparation: 5 minutes Cooking: 30 minutes

- 1 can condensed tomato soup
- 1 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
- ¼ cup vinegar
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- ¼ cup vegetable oil
- ¼ tsp hot pepper sauce (optional)
- 2 Tbsp packed brown sugar
- 4 chicken breast halves (about 2 lbs)

## Instructions

1. In a small bowl, combine soup, vinegar, oil, brown sugar, Worcestershire sauce, garlic powder and hot sauce.

2. Remove skin from chicken. On rack in broiler pan, arrange chicken bone side up. Brush chicken with sauce. Broil 6” from heat for 25 to 30 minutes or until no longer pink, turning once and brushing often with sauce during cooking. Season with salt and pepper. Serve with rice if desired.

Makes 4 servings.

Variation: On grill rack, arrange chicken over medium coals. Grill uncovered 20 minutes, turning often. Brush chicken with sauce, grill another 20 minutes or until chicken is no longer pink, turning chicken often and brushing with sauce.
