## Ingredients

- 2 lbs chicken wings
- 1/3 cup honey
- 2 tbsp Oxo chicken bouillon liquid
- 2 tbsp soya sauce
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- ½ tsp garlic powder
- ¼ tsp ground ginger

## Instructions

Cut wings in two sections and remove tips. In a large baking pan, place wings in a single layer.  Bake at 425 degrees for 10 minutes.  Remove from oven and raise temperature to 475 degrees.  Drain fat from pan.

Combine Oxo with remaining ingredients and pour over wings.  Bake 25 minutes until golden and glazed with sauce.  Reserve sauce for dipping.

Makes 6 appetizer servings.
