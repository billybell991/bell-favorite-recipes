## Ingredients

- 1 can crushed pineapple
- 1 small package of lime Jello
- 1 cup whipping cream
- 1 cup chopped celery
- 6 ozs cream cheese, room temperature
- 1 cup chopped nuts

## Instructions

Heat pineapple. Dissolve lime Jello in pineapple. Stir well. Set in refrigerator until partially set.

Whip cream. Beat in cream cheese. Add nuts and celery. Add cream mixture to pineapple mixture.

Set in refrigerator for about 3 hours before serving.
