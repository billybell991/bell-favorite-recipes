## Ingredients

Dressing:

- 1/4 cup oil
- 1/4 cup vinegar
- 2 1/2 tbsp sugar
- 1/4 concentrated orange juice
- 2 cloves of garlic
- 1 tsp crushed red pepper
- 1/2 tsp basil
- Salt and pepper to taste

Other stuff:

- Whatever pasta you want
- crushed feta cheese
- grapes (red & green)
- red and green peppers
- olives
- celery

## Instructions

1. Mix all that stuff up.  That's your dressing.
2. Cook choice of pasta, drain, and add the dressing.
3. Add the other stuff cut up however you want, however much you want, because I don't have these kinds of details.  You can blame Heather if it tastes bad.  She wrote down the initial recipe.
