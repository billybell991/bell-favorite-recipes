## Ingredients

- 2 85-gr pkg Ramen noodles
- 3 Tbsp vinegar
- (the ones w/the flavour packets)
- 1 Tbsp sugar
- 1 cup almonds
- 2 cups cooked chicken
- 2 Tbsp butter
- 2 green onions, chopped
- ½ cup oil
- 1 small cabbage, shredded

## Instructions

Drop noodles into boiling water for 1 to 1-1/2 minutes. Drain and cool. Saute almonds in butter until toasted. Toss all ingredients together.
