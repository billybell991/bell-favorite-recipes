## Ingredients

- 1 cup peanut butter
- 1 cup Rice Krispies
- 1 Tbsp butter or margarine
- 1 cup icing sugar
- ½ cup nuts (optional)
- 1 cup coconut so roll balls into

## Instructions

Cream peanut butter, icing sugar and butter.  Add Rice Krispies  and nuts. Shape into balls and roll in coconut.
