## Ingredients

- Mix  ½” slice off 1# butter
- 1 cup icing sugar
- ¾ cup peanut butter
- ¾ cup chopped walnuts.

## Instructions

Chill for some time; then make small balls. Return to freezer.

MELT: 4 squares semi-sweet chocolate and ½ block Para wax in double boiler.

DIP: chocolate balls in mixture and cool.
