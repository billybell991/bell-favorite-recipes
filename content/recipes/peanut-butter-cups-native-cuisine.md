## Ingredients

- 1 cup margarine, melted
- 4 cups icing sugar

## Instructions

1-1/2 cups peanut butter
1 cup graham wafer crumbs

Topping: 1 pkg semi-sweet chocolate chips

Mix the above ingredients together. Mixture will be rather dry. Spray cookie sheet with Pam, then press onto the cookie sheet. Melt 1 pkg of semi-sweet chocolate chips and spread on top. Chill for 10 minutes. Remove and cut into squares. Chill for about 2 hours before removing from cookie sheet.
