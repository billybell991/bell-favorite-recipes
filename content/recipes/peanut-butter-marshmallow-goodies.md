## Ingredients

- 1 cup peanut butter
- ½ cup butter of margarine
- 12-oz pkg butterscotch chips
- ½ bag coloured mini marshmallows

## Instructions

Melt first 3 ingredients.  Cool and add marshmallows.  Place in a lightly buttered 9”x 9”pan.  Chill in refrigerator.  Cut into 1 inch squares.
