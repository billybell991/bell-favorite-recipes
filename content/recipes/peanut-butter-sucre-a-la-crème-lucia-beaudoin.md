## Ingredients

- 1/3 cup margarine
- ½ cup corn syrup or honey
- ¾ cup peanut butter
- 1 tsp vanilla
- ½ tsp salat
- 1 lb or 3-3/4 cups icing sugar

## Instructions

Mix first 5 ingredients and gradually add icing sugar.  Spread in greased or buttered 8 x 8” pan, cut and refrigerate.

I haven’t tried this recipe yet but Lucia assures me it’s a prize-winner. And no cooking!
