## Ingredients

- ½ cup maple syrup
- ½ cup peanut butter
- ¼ cup sugar
- 3 cups Kellogg’s corn flakes
- ¼ cup brown sugar

## Instructions

Combine first 3 ingredients and bring to a boil over moderate heat.  Stir in peanut butter,  Remove from heat and stir in corn flakes.  Using a heaped tablespoon, drop onto greased cookie sheet.  Chill until firm.
