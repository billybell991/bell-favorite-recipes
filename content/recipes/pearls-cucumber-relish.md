## Ingredients

- Peel and remove seeds from cucumbers if desired and put through food chopper.  Should make about 12 – 13 cups of cukes and onions mixed.
- Add 3 tbsp salt and let stand for 4 hours.
- Press water out – Mix 3 cups white sugar, ½ to 2/3 cup flour, ½ tsp cayenne pepper, ½ tsp turmeric, 1 tsp dry mustard, 1-1/2 cups water, 2 cups vinegar and heat mixture to a boil.  Boil on low for 10 minutes.

## Instructions

NOTE:  ½ tsp cayenne makes it pretty peppy!  But Good!

Makes 6-1/2 pints.
