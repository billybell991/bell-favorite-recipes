## Ingredients

- 6 red bell peppers
- 14 ozs fresh mushrooms
- 8 ozs sausage, remove from casing
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp oregano
- 1 tbsp thyme
- 2 garlic cloves
- ½ cup fresh parsley
- grated parmesan cheese
- salt and pepper

## Instructions

Heat oven to 400 degrees. Cut peppers into ½ inch diagonal pieces. Place in a 9x13 inch baking dish. Drizzle with 1 tbsp of olive oil, salt and pepper to taste. Bake until charred (45 minutes), stir occasionally. Remove.

Finely chop parsley, oregano, thyme and garlic. Heat remaining oil, add sliced mushrooms and cook until golden brown. Add herb mixture. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and sauté.

Crumble sausage and cook until browned. Cook pasta. Set aside ¼ cup of pasta cooking liquid. Combine sausage, veggies and herbs.

Serve on pasta. Sprinkle with cheese and pasta cooking liquid.
