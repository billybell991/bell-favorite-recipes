## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cup all purpose flour
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 cups white sugar
- ½ cup cocoa
- 2 tsp vanilla
- 1 cup oil
- 4 eggs
- ¼ cup cold water
- ½ cup (or more) chopped walnuts

## Instructions

Place all ingredients in a beater bowl in the order given, ending with walnuts. Beat on low speed or by hand until smooth. Do not beat for too long.

Grease a 9x13 inch pan. Smooth batter into the pan and bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes, no longer. Ice if desired.
