Submitted by Kim and Raymond Roy

Cousins of the Bride

## Ingredients

- 10 lasagna noodles
- 6 cups mashed potatoes (about 12 medium potatoes)
- 3 cups grated, medium cheddar cheese
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 ¼ cup butter or margarine
- 2 cups chopped onions
- bacon bits if desired

## Instructions

Cook noodles according to directions on the package.

Line the bottom of a greased 9x13 inch pan with some of the noodles. In large bowl, mix mashed potatoes with cheese and salt. Place a layer of this mixture on top of the noodles. Put second layer of noodles over top and so on.

Melt butter in frying pan. Add onion and bacon bits. Sauté until onion is clear and soft. Spoon onions, butter and bacon bits over noodles.

Cover lightly with foil. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes. Let stand 10 minutes before cutting. Serve with sour cream.
