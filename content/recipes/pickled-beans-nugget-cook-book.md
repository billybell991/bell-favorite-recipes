## Ingredients

- 6-qt basket string beans (to make 16 cups) cut into 1” pieces
- 3 cups vinegar
- 3 cups brown sugar
- ½ cup dry mustard
- ½ cup flour (1/4 cup for thinner sauce)
- 1-1/2 tsp turmeric
- 1 tsp celery seed
- 1 tsp salt

## Instructions

Wash, cut and cook beans in boiling salted water for 15 minutes. Drain.

Make a sauce of the other ingredients and add cooked beans.  Cook for 5 minutes.  Bottle and seal.

Yield: 6 Pints.
