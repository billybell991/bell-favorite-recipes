## Ingredients

- 3 cups white vinegar
- 1 1/2 cups of water
- 1 1/2 tsp salt

Optional:

I like to jazz mine up a bit more so I put in:

- A couple of cloves of garlic in each jar
- A few sprigs of dill
- A shake of crushed red pepper

## Instructions

1. Put all ingredients in a bot and boil for 10 minutes.
2. Let this solution cool completely.
3. Hard cook a dozen eggs (this solution will easily do 2 dozen eggs).
4. Peel 'em and pack em into quart jars.
5. Pour the cold vinegar solution in the jars, then seal.
6. Let stand 2 days before crushing these delicious friggers.
