## Ingredients

- 3 cups white vinegar
- 1-1/2 cups water
- 1-1/2 tsp salt
- 1 tsp pepper berries
- ½ tsp celery seed

## Instructions

Boil vinegar solution for 10 minutes and cool.  Hard-boil 1 dozen eggs, dash into cold water to cool rapidly and remove shells.  Pack eggs in quart jars.  Pour cold vinegar solution over to cover, then seal.

Let stand at least 2 days before using.  These keep well at room temperature for a few days but should be refrigerated if kept more than 1 week.
