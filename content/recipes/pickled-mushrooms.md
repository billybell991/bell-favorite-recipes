## Ingredients

- 2 lb mushrooms
- 6 garlic cloves
- 1/2 cup white vinegar 5% acidity
- 2 cup water
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1 tbsp salt
- 6 bay leaves
- 8 whole peppercorns
- 6 tbsp vinegar

## Instructions

1. Clean and wash mushrooms, and put them in a pot.  Fill with enough water to cover the 'shrooms.
2. Add the 1/2 cup of vinegar to the water, bring it to a boil and cook for 10 mins.  (The recipe says 15, but I wanted my 'shrooms with a bit of body to them, so I'll try it with 10 mins next time).  At 15 mins, they came out more like the consistency of canned mushrooms.
3. When mushrooms are cooked, drain the liquid.
4. Now, the marinade: In a separate pot, place the 2 cups of water, salt, sugar, bay leaves, and peppercorns.  Bring them to a boil.
5. Once the sugar and salt have dissolved, add the 6 tbsp of vinegar and remove from the heat and set aside.
6. Clean and dice the garlic into small pieces.  Place the diced garlic on the bottom of the jars.
7. Fill the jars with the cooked mushrooms.
8. Pour marinade over the mushrooms.  Cover, using lids and store them in the refrigerator.  They can be stored in the fridge up to several weeks.
