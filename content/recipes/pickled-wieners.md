## Ingredients

- 1 lb 5” wieners (12 to a pound) OR equivalent in smoked or Polish sausage or pepperoni or knockwurst
- 1-1/4 cups white vinegar
- ½ cup water
- 2 tbsp sugar
- 2 tsp pickling spices
- 1 garlic clove, chopped

## Instructions

Cut wieners in thirds crosswise.  If other smoked type sausages are used, cut them into large bite-sized pieces.  Measure all remaining ingredients into shallow saucepan, bring to a boil, add cut wieners, reduce heat, cover and SIMMER gently 30 minutes.  Remove from heat and pour into preheated, broad-mouthed jar with lid.  Cool then refrigerate for 3 or 4 days before using.  During the marinating period, shake jar to make sure all wiener pieces are covered with marinade.

Serve as appetizers on toothpicks or as a snack with sliced rye bread.  These will keep in the refrigerator for a week.
