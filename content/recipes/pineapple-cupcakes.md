- ½ cup shortening
- ½ cup white sugar
- ½ cup light brown sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1-3/4 cup flour
- ½ tsp baking powder
- ¼ tsp salt
- ½ tsp vanilla
- ½ cup crushed pineapple including some syrup
- ½ cup water

Into beater bowl measure shortening and sugars and beat until creamy.  Beat in eggs.  Remove beaters.

Sift together flour, baking powder and salt.  Combine pineapple, water and vanilla.  Stir flour mixture into creamed mixture alternately with the pineapple-water mixture until blended.  Line the muffin pans with pleated paper cups and carefully spoon in batter to fill 7/8 full.  Bake at 350 degrees 20 – 25 minutes.  Cool in paper cups on cake rack.

When cool, frost with icing made by beating together 3 tbsp butter, 3 tbsp solid parts of crused pineapple, 1-1/2 cups icing sugar and ½ tsp lemon juice.
