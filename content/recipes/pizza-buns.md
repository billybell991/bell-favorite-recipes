## Ingredients

- ½ cup vegetable oil
- 1 minced onion
- 1 cup spaghetti sauce or 8-oz tomato sauce
- 1 lb grated cheddar cheese
- 1 tin mushroom pieces, drained
- 1 tsp oregano
- ½ tsp salt
- Dash pepper
- Dash garlic powder

## Instructions

MIX.      Spread on buns and broil till bubbly.

Sauce keeps for a month.
