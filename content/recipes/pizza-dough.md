Cousins of the Bride

In a large bowl, combine:

- 1 ½ cup warm water
- 1 tbsp yeast

Let sit for 10-15 minutes. Mix well, then add:

## Ingredients

- 1 tbsp oil
- 2 tbsp brown sugar
- 3 cups flour (+1 cup if needed)
- 1 tsp salt

## Instructions

Mix well and knead to form a ball. Cover and let rise in oven (heat off) for 1 hour.

Divide in two. Roll out. Place on a greased pizza pan. Garnish with your favorite toppings. Bake at 425 degrees for 12-15 minutes.

Makes 2 large pizzas.
