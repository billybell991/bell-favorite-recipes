## Ingredients

- 1-1/2 cup Bisquick
- 1/3 cup very hot water

## Instructions

Heat oven to 450 degrees.  Grease cookie sheet or 12-inch pizza pan.  Mix 1-1/2 cups Bisquick baking mix and 1/3 cup very hot water; beat vigorously 20 strokes.  Turn dough onto surface well dusted with baking mix.  Knead about 60 times or until smooth and no longer sticky.  With hands dusted with baking mix, press dough into 13-inch circle on cookie sheet or press in pizza pan; pinch edge, forming ½ inch rim.  Top pizza to taste.  Bake 12 to 15 minutes or until crust is brown and cheese is melted and bubbly.
