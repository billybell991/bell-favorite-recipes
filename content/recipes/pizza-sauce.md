## Ingredients

- 1 tin 213-ml tomato sauce
- ½ tsp dried basil
- ½ tsp dried oregano
- ½ tsp minced garlic
- ¼ tsp dried thyme
- Pinch of rosemary
- Pinch of dried dill

## Instructions

In a bowl or a jar, combine all ingredients.
