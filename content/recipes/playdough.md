PLAY  DOUGH w/Kool-Aid

- 1 cup flour
- 1 Tbsp vegetable oil
- 1 pkg unsweetened Kool-Aid
- ¼ cup salt
- 2 Tbsp cream of tartar
- 1 cup water

Mix flour, salt, cream of tartar and Kool-Aid in a medium saucepan.  Add water and oil. Stir over medium heat 3 – 5 minutes.  When mixture forms a ball in pan, remove from heat.  Knead until smooth. Put in a plastic bag and refrigerate.
