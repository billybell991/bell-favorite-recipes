## Ingredients

- 1-1/2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 cups white sugar
- ½ cup (8 tbsp) cocoa
- 2 tsp vanilla
- 1 cup oil
- 4 eggs
- ¼ cup cold water
- ½ cup (or more) chopped walnuts

## Instructions

Put all ingredients in beater bowl in order given ending with walnuts and beat on low speed or by hand until smooth.  Do not beat too long.
Grease a 9 x 13 pan.  Smooth batter in it and bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes – no longer.  Ice if desired.
