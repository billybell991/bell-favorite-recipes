Friends of the Bride and Groom

## Ingredients

- 1 big bucket
- 3 bags of ice
- 6 cans of concentrated fruit juice
- 60-100oz of Vodka
- 24 bottles of beer

## Instructions

Pour beer and vodka into bucket. Add ice and concentrated juice. Mix well.

Dunk your cup in and enjoy!
