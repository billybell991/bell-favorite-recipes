## Ingredients

- 4 pork chops
- 4 medium potatoes, thinly sliced
- 2 tomatoes, chopped
- 4 carrots, thinly sliced
- 1 cup onion, chopped
- ½ cup green pepper, diced
- 1 tsp salt
- ¼ tsp pepper
- 2 chicken bouillon cubes
- 1 ½ cups boiling water
- 2 tbsp butter

## Instructions

Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Trim fat from chops. Brown chops on both sides, season with salt and pepper to taste.

Butter a casserole dish and arrange the potatoes on the bottom. In a bowl, combine tomatoes, carrots, onions and green pepper.

Spread over potatoes and sprinkle with the salt and pepper.

Dissolve bouillon cubes in the boiling water. Pour over the vegetables. Dot with butter. Arrange chops on top. Cover and bake for 45 minutes, then uncover and bake for 30 minutes longer.
