Submitted by Tina Sasyniuk and Kevin Long

Friends of the Bride and Groom

- ¾ lb lean, boneless pork
- 1 tbsp cornstarch
- 1 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce
- 1 tbsp cooking sherry
- 1 bunch broccoli
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- 2 tbsp minced garlic cloves
- 2 tbsp minced gingerroot
- ¼ cup water
- 2 tbsp hoisin sauce (optional) 25ml

Slice pork thinly across the grain.

In a bowl, stir together cornstarch, soy sauce, and sherry. Add pork and stir to coat well. Separate broccoli into florets, peel stalks, then cut in 1 ½ inch pieces.

In wok or large nonstick skillet, heat oil over high heat. Add pork mixture, stir-fry for 2 minutes or until meat is lightly browned.

Stir in garlic, ginger, and broccoli. Stir-fry for 2 minutes. Add water. Cover and steam for 2 minutes or until broccoli is tender and crisp.

Stir in hoisin sauce if desired.
