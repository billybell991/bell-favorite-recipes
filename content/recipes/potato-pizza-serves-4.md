## Instructions

This recipe is for microwave ovens but could be prepared in the same manner and used in a conventional oven.

1-1/2 cups mashed potatoes	½ cup chopped green pepper
¼ cup dry bread crumbs	¼ lb bacon slices, chopped
1 tsp dried mixed Italian herbs	1 14-oz tin tomatoes, drained
Salt and pepper	1-1/2 cups grated cheese
1 small tin anchovy fillets(optional)	Few ripe olives (optional)

Mix together the mashed potato, bread crumbs and herbs.  Season lightly and press into a round shallow glass pan.
Mix together the onion, green peppers, bacon and cook in the microwave for 5 minutes (until vegetables are softened and bacon is lightly cooked).  Arrange this mixture on the potato base.  Top with drained tomatoes and grated cheese.  Arrange the anchovy fillets in a lattice pattern on top and cook in microwave for 4 minutes or until heated thoroughly and the cheese has melted.  Place an olive in each square of the pizza before serving.
