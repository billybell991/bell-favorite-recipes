## Ingredients

- 1/3 cup butter
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 egg, beaten
- 2 tbsp milk

## Instructions

Melt butter, mix all ingredients and put in tart shells.  Bake at 350 degrees until pastry is browned – approx. 30 minutes.
