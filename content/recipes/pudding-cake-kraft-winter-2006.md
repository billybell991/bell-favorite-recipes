## Instructions

Prep. Time: 15 min.    Total: 1 hour 35 Min.

1 pkg (2-layer size) yellow cake mix	2 cups cold milk
1-1/4 cup water	2 pkg (4-serving size each) Jell-o Lemon
1/3 cup sugar (optional)		    Instant Pudding
2 Tbsp icing sugar (optional)

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Prepare cake mix as directed on package. Pour batter into greased 13 x 9-inch baking dish.
Pour milk and water into large bowl. Add dry pudding mixes and granulated sugar, if using. Beat with wire whisk 2 minutes or until well blended. Pour over cake batter in dish. Place baking dish on baking sheet to catch dripping, as mixture does bubble.
Bake 55  min. to 1 hour or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool in pan 20 minutes. (Mixture will thicken slightly as it cool.). Sprinkle with icing sugar, if using. Spoon onto serving dishes. Serve warm; garnish with raspberries, if desired.
Makes 16 servings.
Serving suggestions:  There’s no need to reheat leftovers – this cake is also delicious as a cold treat. The lemon filling transforms from saucy and gooey to thick and custard-like. Yummy!
