## Ingredients

- 1 Pumpernickel
- 1 300 gr pkg of Chopped Spinach
- 1 40-gr Knorr Vegetable Soup Mix
- 1 500 mL tub of Sour Cream

## Instructions

Defrost chopped spinach & squeeze out excess water.

Hollow out center of pumpernickel.

Cut center of pumpernickel into cubes or slices. In a small bowl combine sour cream soup mix  and spinach.

Stir until thoroughly mixed. Place in center of pumpernickel

Refrigerate for 1 hour

Include bagels as an option for dipping.
