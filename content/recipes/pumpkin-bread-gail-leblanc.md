## Ingredients

- 3-1/2 cups flour
- 2 tsp cinnamon
- 1 tsp cloves
- 1 tsp nutmeg
- 2-2/3 cups sugar
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 tsp baking powder
- ½ tsp baking soda
- 2 cups canned pumpkin
- 2.3 cup cooking oil
- 4 eggs – beaten
- 2/3 cups water
- 1 cup walnuts, optional

## Instructions

Mix all dry ingredients. Add all wet ingredients and mix well together. Pour into 2 greased 2-pound load pans. Bake approx. 1 hour at 350 degrees F.

Makes 2 – 2 pound loaves.
