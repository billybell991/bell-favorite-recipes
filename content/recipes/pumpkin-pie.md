## Ingredients

- ½ cup brown sugar
- l tbsp pumpkin pie spice
- l tbsp all-purpose flour
- l/2 tsp salt
- 2 eggs
- l-l/2 cups pumpkin
- l-l/2 cups milk (can be part cream)

## Instructions

Mix sugar, spice, flour and salt.  Beat eggs until light and fluffy.  Stir in pumpkin.  Add dry ingredients, mixing well.  Gradually stir in milk.  Blend thoroughly.  Bake in pie pastry in a 375 degree oven for 45 – 55 minutes.

NOTE:  I mix this all together in the blender.  Much easier and faster.

NOTE:  If I don’t have pumpkin pie spice on hand, I use l-l/2 tsp cinnamon with l/2 tsp ginger and l/4 tsp ground cloves.
