A Tea Biscuit dough should be soft but not sticky.  Little kneading improves the quality of the biscuits, but should be done with a gentle touch as too much handling tends to make them tough.

Preheat oven to 450 F.  Blend or sift together:

## Ingredients

- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 4 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp salt

## Instructions

Cut finely 1/3 cup shortening (or ¼ cup oil).

Add ¾ cup plus 2 Tbsp milk (200 ml).

Stir with a fork to make a soft dough. Turn dough onto  a lightly floured surface and knead gently 8 to 10 times. Roll or pat to desired thickness, (biscuits all be doubled in height when baked). Cut out with a floured cutter.

Place biscuits on an ungreased baking sheet, close together for soft-sided biscuits or about 1” apart for crusty-sided biscuits. Bake in a preheated oven for 12 to 15 minutes.
