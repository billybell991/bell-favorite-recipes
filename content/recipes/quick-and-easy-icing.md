## Ingredients

- 1 envelope whipped topping mix (2 to 2-1/2 cup yield)
- 1 pkg instant pudding mix (4 serving size)
- 1-1/2 cup cold milk
- 1 tsp vanilla, if desired

## Instructions

In a chilled bowl, blend and whip topping mix, instant pudding, cold milk and vanilla until stiff (3-8 min.).  Immediately frost cake and store in refrigerator.  Serve chilled.  Frosted cake may be frozen for storage
