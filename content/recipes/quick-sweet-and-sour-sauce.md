## Instructions

For hamburger, pork, chicken, etc.

2 oz catsup	6 oz sugar
8 oz water	2 tsp corn starch
2 oz vinegar

Boil first four ingredients until clear.  Mix cornstarch in 2 oz water.  Add to sauce to thicken.
