## Ingredients

- 1 quart chicken broth or stock
- 1 16-oz can tomatoes
- ½ cup chopped onion
- ½ cup chopped cabbage
- 1 10-oz pkg frozen mixed veggies,
- ½ tsp dried basil
- thawed
- 1/8 tsp pepper
- Dash sugar
- ¼ cup uncooked elbow macaroni
- 1 cup leftover cubed cooked chicken
- or roast beef

## Instructions

In a large saucepan or Dutch oven, bring broth, tomatoes, onion, cabbage, vegetables and seasonings to a boil.  Add macaroni and simmer 10 minutes or until tender.  Add meat and heat through.

Yield:  6 servings.
