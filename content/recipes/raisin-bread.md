## Ingredients

- ½ cup lukewarm water
- 1 pkg or 1 Tbsp yeast
- 1 tsp sugar
- 3 cups tepid water (2 boiling, 1 cold)
- ¼ cup shortening
- ½ cup sugar
- 2 tsp cinnamon
- ½ tsp salt
- 2 beaten eggs
- 1 cup lightly floured raisins
- Flour

## Instructions

Preheat oven to 350 degrees.         Dissolve sugar and yeast in 1stquantity of water

Melt ½ cup sugar, shortening and salt in 2 cups boiling water, then cool mixture with the one cup of cold water.

Beat the eggs and cinnamon. Add yeast mixture, egg mixture and the raisins to the water mixture.

Stir and add flour gradually until you have a soft dough that leaves the sides of the bowl.

Turn dough onto a lightly floured board and knead for about 10 minutes, adding flour as needed, until dough is smooth and springy. Shape into a ball and put into a large bowl, cover with a towel and put in a warm place until dough has doubled in volume. (About 1-1/2  to 2 hrs.) Punch dough down, shape into 4 small loaves (8-1/2” x 4-1/2” well-greased pans),  cover again and let double in volume again.

Bake 30 – 45 Minutes at 350 degrees. Because of higher content of sugar and raisins, bread seems to brown fast so you should keep an eye on it for the last 5 or 10 minutes.
