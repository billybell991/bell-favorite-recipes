## Ingredients

- 2 pkgs Philadelphia cream cheese
- 1 pkg ranch style dry mix
- 8 large tortilla shells
- 1 jar of drained and finely chopped, jalapeno peppers
- red pimentos, drained and finely chopped
- onion to your taste, finely chopped
- black olives, finely chopped, optional

## Instructions

Mix cream cheese with dry ranch mix. Spread evenly over tortilla shells. Sparingly top tortillas with the rest of the ingredients.

Roll shells up and wrap in Saran. Refrigerate for 24 hours. Slice into 1 inch slices and serve.

Very colorful and may be very hot depending on the amount of jalapenos you’ve added. Very nice for showers, parties and Christmas.
