## Instructions

These decadent treats have soft coconutty centers, a touch of raspberry and a luscious layer of chocolate on top.

1-1/4 cups all-purpose flour	1/3 cup granulated sugar
½ cup butter	1 egg yolk
1/3 cup raspberry jam	1 can (300 ml) sweetened condensed milk	2 cups sweetened flaked coconut	5 oz semisweet chocolate
1 tbsp butter

In bowl, mix together flour and sugar, using pastry blender or 2 knives, cut in butter until mixture is roughly the size of peas. Stir in egg yolk, mix thoroughly.

Press into bottom of 9-inch square baking pan. Bake in 350 F oven for 18 – 20 minutes or until starting to brown. Spread jam over warm base. Combine condensed milk and coconut; gently spread evenly over jam. Return to oven, bake for 25 minutes or until golden brown and set. Let cool completely on rack.

In small saucepan, stir together chocolate with butter until melted. Spread over coconut layer, smoothing top with knife. Cover and refrigerate until chocolate is set. Let stand at room temperature for 15 minutes. Cut into squares. Makes 36 squares.
