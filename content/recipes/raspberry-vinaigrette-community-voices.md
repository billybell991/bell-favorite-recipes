## Instructions

INGREDIENTS
1 cup virgin olive oil
½ cup raspberry vinegar
2 tsp dry mustard
3 Tbsp dried , finely crushed onion flakes
4 Tbsp liquid honey (more or less to suit taste)
Fresh herbs to taste (basil, dill, mint or your choice)
Salt & pepper to taste

DIRECTIONS
Put ingredients in a bottle and shake well before using. Store dressing in fridge.

This vinaigrette is wonderful sprinkled on green salads or dribbled over steamed vegetables.
