## Instructions

If you like your summer salads light and refreshing, all you need to do s splash on a dash of raspberry vinegar. Sprinkle it straight from the bottle or use it as a base for Raspberry Vinaigrette.

INGREDIENTS: 5 cups apple cider vinegar (or red wine vinegar)
5 handfuls of raspberries, washed

DIRECTIONS:  Put berries into bottle or jar. Heat vinegar until boiling point. Pour over raspberries, leaving about one inch head space. Cork or cap and set in a cool dark place for several days before using.

This allows vinegar to draw out the berry flavour. After setting time is up, you can strain the vinegar and discard well-squeezed berries, if you wish.

Keeps up to  six months in refrigerator.
