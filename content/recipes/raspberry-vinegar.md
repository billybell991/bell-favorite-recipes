## Ingredients

- 1 pint raspberries
- 2 cups champagne vinegar
- ¼ cup sugar

## Instructions

Set aside ¼ cup berries.  Place remaining in medium bowl.  With fork, crush berries slightly.  In medium saucepan, bring to a boil vinegar and sugar.  Pour over crushed berries, cover and steep 48 hours.  Place whole berries in sterilized  bottles.  Strain vinegar through cheesecloth, pour into bottles.
