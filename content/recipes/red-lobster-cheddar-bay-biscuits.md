- 2-1/2 cups Bisquick baking mix
- 4 Tbs cold butter or margarine
- 1 heaping cup grated cheddar cheese
- ¾ cup cold whole milk
- ¼ tsp garlic powder

TOPPING:

- 2 Tbs butter
- ½ tsp garlic powder
- ¼ tsp dried parsley flakes
- Pinch of salt

Yield: 1 dozen biscuits

Combine Bisquick with cold  butter in medium bowl, mixing with pastry cutter or fork until remaining chunks of butter are size of peas. Add cheddar cheese, milk and ¼ tsp garlic powder. Mix by hand until combined but don’t overmix.

Drop ¼-cup portions of dough onto an ungreased cookie sheet using an ice cream scoop. Bake for 15 to 17 minutes, or until tops begin to turn light brown.

While you remove biscuits from oven, melt 2 Tbs butter in small bowl in microwave. Stir in ½ tsp garlic powder and dried parsley flakes. Use brush to spread garlic butter over tops of all biscuits.
