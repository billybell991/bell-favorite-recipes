## Ingredients

- 1-1/2 cups sugar
- 4 tbsp flour
- ¼ tsp nutmeg
- 1 tbsp butter
- 2 beaten eggs
- 3 cups cut-up rhubarb
- 1 unbaked pie shell

## Instructions

Blend sugar, flour, nutmeg and butter.  Add beaten eggs.  Beat until smooth.  Pour over rhubarb in a 9-inch pastry lined pan.  Top with pastry if desired.  Bake 10 minutes at 450 degrees, then reduce heat to 350 degrees and bake for 30 minutes.

NOTE:  This is the original recipe which I copied.  However, I bake this pie for about one hour at 375 degrees.

NOTE: Be sure to use a large deep pie plate. It has a tendency to run over.
