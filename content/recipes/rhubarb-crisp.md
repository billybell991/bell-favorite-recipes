## Ingredients

- 3 cups rhubarb
- ½ cup white sugar
- 1 tsp cinnamon

## Instructions

Mix and place in bottom of buttered dish.

1 cup rolled oats	½ cup margarine
½ cup flour	1 cup brown sugar

Mix till crumbly and place on top of rhubarb.

Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.

Yum, Yum delicious!
