## Ingredients

- 1 egg, beaten
- 1 cup flour
- 1-1/2 cup white sugar
- 3-1/2 tsp baking powder
- 2 tbsp shortening
- ½ tsp salt
- ¼ cup milk

## Instructions

Mix the above ingredients and put in 13 x  9 inch pan.  Cover with 4 or 5 cups cut rhubarb.  Sprinkle with 1 cup sugar and pour 2 cups boiling water over top.  Bake at 350 to 400 degrees F. for 30 minutes (or until top is golden brown).   Serve with whipped cream.

Note:  I baked this for 45 minutes.
