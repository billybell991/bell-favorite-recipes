Cousins of the Bride

- 3 cups rhubarb, diced
- 1 1/3 cups whole wheat flour
- 1 1/3 cups all purpose flour
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1 cup yogurt or sour cream
- 1/2 cup vegetable oil
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup chopped nuts

Topping:

- 1/2 cup brown sugar
- 1/2 cup chopped nuts
- 2 tbsp butter
- 1 tsp cinnamon

In a large bowl, combine rhubarb, flours, brown sugar, baking soda and salt.

In a separate bowl, blend together yogurt, oil and eggs. Stir into rhubarb mixture just until moistened. Fold in nuts.

Spoon batter into 24 greased muffin cups

Combine topping ingredients. Sprinkle a bit of topping mixture on each muffin.

Bake at 350 degrees for 25-30 minutes.
