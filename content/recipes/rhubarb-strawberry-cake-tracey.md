## Ingredients

- 1 cup white sugar
- 3 tbsp flour
- 3 cups sliced strawberries
- 3 cups diced rhubarb
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 cup margarine
- 1 cup rolled oats

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
2. In a large bowl, mix white sugar, 3 tbsp flour, strawberries, and rhubarb.
3. Place mixture in a 9x13 pan.
4. Mix 1 1/2 cups of flour, brown sugar, margarine, and oats till crumbly.
5. Put crumble on top of rhubarb and strawberry mixture.
6. Bake 45 mins in oven or until crisp and brown.
