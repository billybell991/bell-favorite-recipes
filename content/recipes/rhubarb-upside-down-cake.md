- 3 cups sliced fresh rhubarb, ½” pieces
- 1 Tbsp unsalted butter, room temperature
- ¼ cup strawberry or seedless raspberry jam
- 1 cup sugar
- 1-1/2 cup all-purpose flour
- 2-1/2 tsp baking powder
- ½ tsp cinnamon
- 2/3 cup butter, room temperature
- 2 eggs
- ¼ tsp each vanilla & almond extract
- 1/3 cup milk

Put rhubarb in a medium bowl.  Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Generously coat a deep dish pie plate with margarine.

- Put jam in a small bowl. Microwave on medium until melted – about 30 seconds.
- Pour jam over rhubarb and stir to coat evenly.

## Ingredients

- Evenly sprinkle ¼ cup sugar in pie plate. Spoon rhubarb and jam in pie plate.
- In a medium bowl stir flour, cinnamon and baking powder.
- In a large bowl, with an electric mixer whip ¾ cup butter until creamy. Beat in remaining ¾ cup sugar until well mixed.

## Instructions

- Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Beat in almond and vanilla extracts & mix well. Gradually beat in 1/3 of the flour mixture and about ½ of 1/3 cup milk. Repeat until flour & milk are used up. Batter will be thick so spoon carefully to coat all the fruit.
- Bake in a 350 degree oven 40 – 45 minutes. Let stand 5 minutes – scrape edges with a knife and invert on a serving plate.
