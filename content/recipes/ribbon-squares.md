## Ingredients

- ½ cup melted butter or margarine
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1 cup brown sugar, packed
- 2 cups quick cooking oatmeal
- ½ tsp vanilla

## Instructions

Mix last four ingredients, add butter and mix.  Place on cookie sheet – spread out so sheet is evenly.  Bake for approximately 5 minutes at 350 degrees – or until mixture is bubbling.  Remove and let sit for 5 minutes.  Cut in squares.
