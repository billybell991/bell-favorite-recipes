## Instructions

Excellent with beef, turkey or ham.  Can be reheated many times.

2 cups cooked rice	6 medium zucchini, sliced
2 large sliced onions	½ lb sharp cheese, grated
2 cans mushroom soup

Cook onion and zucchini over low heat until soft with butter of margarine in fry pan (cover and stir occasionally).  Put a layer of rice, layer of vegetable mixture, some of the cheese and part of mushroom soup.  Repeat in layers.  Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes, or until bubbly and top is browned.  Serves 12
