Cousin of the Bride

- 2 cups cooked and cooled, Uncle Ben’s Converted Brand rice
- 3/4 cup English cucumber, cut into slices
- 3/4 cup tomatoes, diced
- 3/4 cup green and red peppers, diced
- 1/3 cup red onion, diced
- 1 cup Feta cheese, cut into large dice
- 1/3 cup Kalamata olives, pitted

Dressing:

## Ingredients

- 1/3 cup olive oil
- 1/4  cup red wine vinegar
- 1 garlic, minced (about 1 tsp)
- 1 tsp rubbed oregano
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp pepper

## Instructions

In a large bowl, combine cooked rice with all diced vegetables, diced cheese and olives.

In another bowl, combine dressing ingredients. Pour dressing over rice/vegetable mixture. Toss well and refrigerate.

Salad may be served over a bed of mixed greens or as a side dish. Yields 6-1 cup servings.
