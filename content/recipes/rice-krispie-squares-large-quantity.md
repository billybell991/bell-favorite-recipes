## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter or margarine
- 10 cups miniature marshmallows
- 3 tsp vanilla extract (optional)
- or 80 regular (2 250g pkgs)
- 11 cups Rice Krispies

## Instructions

In large saucepan over low heat, melt margarine.  Add marshmallows.  Stir until melted and well blended.  Remove from heat.
Stir in vanilla.  Add cereal, stirring until coated.
Using lightly-buttered spatula, press into buttered 13 x 9 pan.  Cool.
