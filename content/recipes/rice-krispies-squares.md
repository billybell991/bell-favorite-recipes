## Ingredients

- ¼ cup margarine or butter
- 5 cups miniature marshmallows
- (or 40 regular marshmallows)
- (250 gram pkg)
- l/2 tsp vanilla
- 6 cups rice crispies

## Instructions

In large saucepan over low heat, melt margarine.  Add marshmallows, stir until melted and well blended.  Remove from heat
Stir in vanilla.  Add cereal, stirring until well coated.
Using lightly buttered spatula, press into buttered 13 x 9 “pan.  Cool..  Cut into squares.  YIELD; 24 – 2”squares.

Microwave Directions:- In large microwaveable bowl microwave margarine at HIGH 30-60 seconds or until melted.  Add marshmallows, tossing to coat.  Microwave at HIGH 1 to 1-1/2 minutes or until smooth when stirred, stirring after 45 seconds.  Follow Steps 2 and 3 above.
