## Ingredients

- 1-1/2 cup cooked rice
- 1 Tbsp butter
- 1 cup milk
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 2 eggs
- ½ cup raisins
- 1/3 cup sugar

## Instructions

Combine rice, milk, eggs and sugar in double boiler. Heat until mixture coats metal spoon. Add remaining ingredients and let sit for 30 minutes.
