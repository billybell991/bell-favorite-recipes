## Ingredients

- ¼ cup butter
- 2-1/2 or 2-3/4 cup sifted icing sugar
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1 egg

## Instructions

Cream butter well; stir in egg.  Add sugar, a little at a time, beating until light and fluffy.  Stir in vanilla.

Yield:  enough for 2 pans.
