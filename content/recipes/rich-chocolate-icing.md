## Ingredients

- 2 squares unsweetened chocolate
- 1 cup icing sugar
- 1 egg, beaten
- 2 Tbsp butter
- 1 tsp vanilla

## Instructions

Melt chocolate in top of double boiler. Stir in icing sugar, beaten egg, butter and vanilla. Remove from heat at once and stir. Cool a little before spreading on brownies.
