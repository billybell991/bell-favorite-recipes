## Ingredients

- 1 250-gr whipping cream
- ¼ - ½  cup of Carnation milk
- 1 full cup brown sugar
- ¼ cup flour
- Vanilla and nutmeg

## Instructions

Mix all together at minimum speed and then at full speed until well blended.

Pour into uncooked pie shell and bake at 350 until “you decide it’s cooked”.
