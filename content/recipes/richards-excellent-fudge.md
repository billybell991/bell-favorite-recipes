## Ingredients

- 1 lb butter
- 1 lg tin Carnation (whole milk)
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1 kg brown sugar
- 1 kg icing sugar

## Instructions

Mix well,  Bring to a boil over medium heat, stirring a lot.  Boil for no more than 12 minutes.  Take off heat.  Add 1 kg icing sugar.  Mix well and quickly pour into a well-buttered 9 x 13 pan
INSTRUCTIONS AS WRITTEN BY RICHARD BELANGER:-  In a big pot on stove, let melt 1 lb of butter.  When melted, add 1 big can of Carnation and a little bit of vanilla.

Add 1 kg of brown sugar. Mix well and when it starts to boil good, lower your stove as long as it boils for 12  minutes only, not more.

Take off the stove and mix in with a wooden spoon 1 kg of icing sugar. Mix well, but finish with mixer at high speed. When you see that it is very smooth, put fudge in a big enough pan or a big one and small one.
