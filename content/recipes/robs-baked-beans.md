Empty 5-lb. bag of beans into pot.

Cover with water.

Boil for 1 hr. after water begins to bubble.

Drain beans and save the water.

Put 1/2 the beans in a dish and ½ in a pot.

To the beans in the pot, add:

- ½ lb. shortening ( break in chunks).
- ½ cup syrup.
- a good handful of brown sugar.
- 1 lb. of bacon, cut in small pieces.

Put the other ½ of beans on top and mix with a large spoon.

Next day (or whenever, add the reserved water until it covers the beans 1-1/2 to 2”(add

more water if necessary.

Bake in 350 degree oven for 3-1/2 to 4 hours.
