## Ingredients

- 2 cups (12-oz pkg) chocolate chips
- 2 tsp vanilla
- 1 can Eagle Brand milk
- 2 cups peanuts
- 2 tbsp margarine
- 1 10-oz pkg mini marshmallows

## Instructions

MELT:
Chips with Eagle Brand and margarine over low heat, or in top of a double boiler.  Remove from heat and stir in vanilla and nuts.  Cool to lukewarm.

Place marshmallows in a large bowl; fold in chocolate mixture.  Spread in a waxed paper-lined 9”x 13”pan.  Chill until firm, about 2 hours.  Remove from pan and peel off waxed paper.  Cut into squares.
