## Ingredients

- 3 cups flour
- 1 cup butter or shortening
- 1 cup white sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1-1/2 tsp baking powder
- ¼ tsp salt
- 1 tsp vanilla or lemon extract
- 1 tbsp milk

## Instructions

Cream butter, sugar, eggs and milk. Add vanilla. Stir in sifted dry ingredients and blend well.

Roll out dough ¼” thick and cut in desired shapes. Bake at 375 degrees until done, about 8 – 10 minutes.
