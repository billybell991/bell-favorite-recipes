## Ingredients

- 2 lbs soup bones
- 8 cups water (to start with)
- 1 cup barley
- ¾ tsp savory
- 3 large carrots, diced
- Pinch garlic salt
- 3 medium onions, diced
- ½ tsp oregano
- 4 stalks celery with leaves, diced
- 28-oz tin stewed tomatoes
- 2 tsp parsley
- Salt & Pepper to taste

## Instructions

Boil water and bones for about ½ hour. Add barley and spices; let boil for about 1 hour. Add more water, if needed. Add vegetables and tomatoes; let cook until vegetables are ready. Sit and enjoy.
