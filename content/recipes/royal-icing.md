- 3 level Tbsp meringue powder
- 500 gr (or 1.10 lb) sifted icing sugar
- 3-1/2 oz warm water
- ½ tsp cream of tartar

Combine ingredients, mixing slowly.  Then beat at medium speed for 7 – 10 minutes.  Utensils have to be grease-free.  Icing dries rapidly so keep it covered with a damp cloth.  To restore texture, re-beat slowly.  Makes 3-1/2 cups.

OR:

## Ingredients

- 3 egg whites
- 500 gr. (1.10 lb) sifted icing sugar
- 1 tsp almond flavoring

## Instructions

Whip at low speed till desired working consistency is reached.  Keep covered with damp cloth while working flowers.  Store tightly covered in fridge.  Whip again before using.
