Prep: 10 min. Total Time: 55 min.

## Ingredients

- 2 tsp vegetable oil
- 6 small boneless skinless chicken breast halves (about 1-1/2 lbs.)
- ½ cup Kraft Russian Dressing
- ½ cup Kraft Pure Apricot jam
- ½ pkg dry onion soup mix (about 3 Tbsp.)

## Instructions

PREHEAT oven to 350 F. Heat oil in large skillet on medium-high heat. Add Chicken; cook 4 min. on each side or until browned. Remove chicken from skillet; place in 3-L baking dish.

MIX dressing, jam and soup mix in small bowl. Pour over chicken.

BAKE 45 min. or until chicken is cooked through.

Makes 6 servings.
