## Ingredients

- 1 lb hamburg
- 1 cup rice
- ½ tsp salt
- ¼ tsp pepper
- 1 tin tomato soup
- 1 tin water
- 3 onions

## Instructions

Mix together uncooked hamburg, rice, salt, pepper, soup and water. Slice onions on top. Bake in a covered casserole in a 350 degree oven for 2 hours.
