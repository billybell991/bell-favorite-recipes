## Ingredients

- ¼ cup vinegar
- 1 or 2 Tbsp sugar
- ½ tsp salt
- ¼ tsp celery seed
- 1 large cucumber, thinly slice (about 3 cups)
- ½ cup thinly sliced onions

## Instructions

Combine marinade ingredients. Add cucumber and onions and toss to coat. Cover and chill in fridge for four hours or up to five days. Stir occasionally.  Enjoy!
