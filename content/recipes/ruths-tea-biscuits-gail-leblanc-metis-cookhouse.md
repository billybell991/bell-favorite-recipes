## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- ¼ cup sugar
- 1 Tbsp baking powder
- 1 tsp salt
- ¼ cup oil or shortening
- ¾ cup milk

## Instructions

Mix flour, sugar, baking powder and salt. Cut in the oil; add milk and stir with a fork to make a soft dough.

Gather into a ball and knead several times until smooth.  Roll out to ½” thick and cut with a small round cookie cutter ( about 2” diameter).

Bake at 420 F. for about 13 minutes (depends on your oven.)
