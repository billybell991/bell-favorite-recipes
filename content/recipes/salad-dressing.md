## Ingredients

- 1 can Eagle Brand milk
- 2 eggs
- 1 can vinegar
- salt and pepper
- 1 tsp dry mustard

## Instructions

Mix 2 eggs, add Eagle Brand milk and dry mustard; mix thoroughly.  Then add salt and pepper to taste.  Mix thoroughly and add 1 can vinegar.  This is an excellent dressing for potato salad and will keep in the fridge for quite a while.
