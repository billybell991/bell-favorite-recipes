## Ingredients

- 3 Tbsp butter
- 3-1/2 cups milk
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- 7-1/2 oz tin salmon of 1 qt canned lake trout
- 1 stalk celery, chopped
- salt and pepper
- 3 Tbsp flour

## Instructions

Melt butter in a saucepan and sauté onion and celery. Add flour and stir well. Add milk slowly, stirring constantly. Add salmon and season with salt and pepper. Cook over low heat until creamy and smooth.

Reheat gently if served a t later date. Serves 4. Makes a hearty first course or a full meal when served with fresh biscuits and salad.
