## Ingredients

- 1 pkg (8 oz) cream cheese, softened
- 1 tin (7-1/2 oz) salmon, drained & flaked
- 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
- ¼ tsp salt
- 1/8 tsp pepper
- Snipped parsley
- Snack crackers

## Instructions

Mix cream cheese, salmon, Worcestershire sauce, salt and pepper until well blended, about 1 minute. Shape into a ball. Cover and refrigerate at least 1 hour.

Sprinkle with parsley. Garnish with sliced olives if desired. Serve with crackers.

Option:- Substitute 1 tin (6-1/2 oz) tuna, drained & flaked, for the salmon.

About 6 servings.
