## Ingredients

- 1 (16 oz ) tin red salmon
- ¼ or ½ lb butter
- ¼ tsp dill weed (or 1 tsp fresh)
- 2 Tbsp Miracle Whip
- Salt & pepper to taste

## Instructions

Blend for a few minutes, then chill.
