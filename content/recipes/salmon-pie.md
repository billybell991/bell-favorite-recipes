## Ingredients

- 15-1/2-oz tin canned salmon
- 1 cup liquid (liquid from salmon plus milk)
- ½ cup soft bread crumbs
- 1 tsp grated onions
- ¼ tsp salt
- 1 tsp lemon juice
- 1 tbsp chopped parsley
- 2 eggs, slightly beaten
- ½ tsp garlic salt

## Instructions

Flake salmon and add salmon liquid and milk. Stir in remaining ingredients and mix well.

Pour into a greased 8-inch pie pan and bake in a 350 degree oven for 45 – 50 minutes or until center is solid when tested with a knife.

Makes 6 servings.
