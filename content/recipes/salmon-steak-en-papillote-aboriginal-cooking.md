## Ingredients

- For oven or BBQ
- MARINADE:
- 1 Tbsp brown sugar
- ½ tsp chopped dill
- 1 Tbsp Dijon mustard
- Juice of half a lemon
- Salt & pepper to taste
- Half a lemon, thinly sliced
- 2 Tbsp soft margarine
- Aluminum foil
- 1/3 cup dry white or red wine

## Instructions

Stir together brown sugar, dill, mustard, lemon juice, salt and pepper
Brush over salmon and top each steak with lemon slices
Brush margarine on  half of two sheets of foil.
On each sheet place 2 salmon steaks side by side and baste with wine.
Wrap up and seal like a turnover.
Cook either in your oven or on the BBQ at 375 F. for approx 15 minutes.
