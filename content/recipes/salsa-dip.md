- 500 ml sour cream
- 440 gram jar salsa
- shredded medium cheese
- chopped fresh tomatoes
- chopped mild onions
- chopped lettuce

Layer cheese/sour cream/salsa mix, chopped tomatoes, chopped onions and chopped lettuce.  Top with more grated cheese.

Makes 2  12”round dishes.  (My low green Pyrex dish)  (BB - Left this in because it made me laugh.  I'm guessing we don't have that "low green Pyrex dish" anymore?)
