## Ingredients

- 2 Tbsp olive oil
- 1 lb hot Italian sausage
- 1 medium onion, thinly sliced
- 2 lg green peppers, cored, seeded and cut into thin strips
- 1 680-ml Hunt’s Italian tomato sauce
- 1 cup water
- 2/3 cup uncooked small pasta
- 5 sprigs each: mint &amp; basil tied together
- Salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

In a large skillet, heat oil; add sausage and onion. Cook until sausage is brown on all sides and onion is tender.

Add peppers; cook 2 minutes. Add remaining ingredients; stirring to submerge pasta. Bring to a boil; reduce heat to medium-low. Cover, simmering about 25 minutes, stirring occasionally, until sausages are cooked thoroughly and pasta is tender. Sausages may be cut into slices before serving.

15 minutes preparation time: 25 minutes cooking time: 4 servings.
