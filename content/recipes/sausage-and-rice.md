## Ingredients

- 1 can of tomatoes, or stewed tomatoes
- one onion, cut in long slivers
- 1 pkg beef bouillon
- ¾ cup water
- basil, garlic, salt and pepper

## Instructions

Fry onion. Add other ingredients. Thicken sauce with corn starch. Fry breakfast sausage. Slice sausage and add to sauce.

Pour sauce with sausage over white rice.
