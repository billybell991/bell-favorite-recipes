## Ingredients

- 2 cans cream of celery soup
- 2 medium cooking onions, chopped
- ½ cup milk
- potatoes to fill a good-sized casserole dish
- Approx. l lb medium cheddar cheese
- Salt – approximately 1 tsp.
- Pepper to taste

## Instructions

Peel and wash potatoes. Place alternate slices of potatoes and finely chopped onions in casserole.
In a separate bowl, combine soup, milk, salt and pepper. Pour over potatoes. Grate cheese and sprinkle approximately ½ between layers. Top with remaining cheese and small bite-size pieces of butter, a dash paprika.
Bake in a moderate oven at 350 degrees, covered, in casserole dish, for approximately 1 hour. Then remove cover and bake an additional ½ hour.
