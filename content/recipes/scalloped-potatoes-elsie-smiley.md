## Ingredients

- 4 raw potatoes
- 3 Tbsp flour
- 1 can cream of mushroom soup or
- 2 to 4 Tbsp butter
- cream of chicken soup
- 2 cans of milk
- 1 onion
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

Scrub, peel and thinly slice the potatoes. Place half of them in a thickly buttered baking dish, salt and pepper, and sprinkle with the flour. Add soup and onions, butter and the rest of the potatoes, salt and pepper and butter.

Pour the milk over all and bake in a 350 degree oven for 1-1.2 hours. Take the cover off 20 minutes before the time is up or until a fork goes into potatoes.
