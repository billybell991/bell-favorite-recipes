## Ingredients

- 1 pack of beef
- 1 pack of pork
- 1/2 turkey breast or 1 chicken breast
- 1 can of beef consommé
- 1 can of water
- 1/2 medium onion
- 2 tbsp lard
- Bannock (double batch)  Recipe here:Bannock (Yan's)

## Instructions

1. Lard up the sides of the cast iron dutch oven.
2. Cut meat into bite-sized cubes and toss that shit into the pot.
3. Chop up the stupid onions into small enough pieces so you won't know it's there.  Mix that devil's veggie witht he meat chunks.
4. Salt and pepper that bitch, but keep in mind the consommé is salty, so don't overdo it.
5. Throw in lard tbsps over top the meaty mix.
6. Add in consommé and water.  Should be covering meaty mix but just barely.
7. Mix up the bannock dough and form a flat circle the size of the pot.  It won't shrink or grow, so make it pretty close.
8. Slap that doughy deliciousness over to the meaty mix, then cut some air vent slits into it.  Just a few - no need to go full Dahmer on it.
9. Splash a bit of water over the bannock so that it doesn't burn or get drier than a bucket of sand.
10. Put it in the oven at 350 degrees for about 4 hours or so.
