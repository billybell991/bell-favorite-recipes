## Ingredients

- 1 can 7-Up
- ½ bag marshmallows
- 1 large pkg lime Jello
- 1 19-oz can crushed pineapple
- 8 oz pkg cream cheese
- 1 pkg Dream Whip

## Instructions

Dissolve over low heat marshmallows in one can of 7-Up.   Add lime Jello and stir until dissolved.  Add cream cheese and beat until smooth.  Add pineapple.  When partially set, beat Cream Whip and fold in.  Mold and chill.
