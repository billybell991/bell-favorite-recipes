## Ingredients

- 1 cup flour
- 1 cup cornmeal
- 3 Tbsp white sugar
- 4 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 cup milk
- 1 beaten egg
- ¼ cup oil

## Instructions

Mix. Put in hot greased frying pan. Lower heat and bake 20 – 25 minutes.
