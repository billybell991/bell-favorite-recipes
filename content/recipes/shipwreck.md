## Ingredients

- 2 large onions
- 2 medium potatoes
- 1 lb ground beef
- ½ cup long grain rice
- 1 cup chopped celery
- salt and pepper
- 1 tin tomato soup
- 1 soup can boiling water

## Instructions

Peel onions and slice over bottom of 2-quart casserole. Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Slice peeled potatoes over onions. Salt and pepper potatoes. Pat ground beef over. Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Sprinkle rice on next, followed by celery. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.

Mix soup and water together. Pour over top. Bake covered in 350 f oven for 2 hours until vegetables are tender. Serves 4.
