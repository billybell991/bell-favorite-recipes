- 1 cup soft butter
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 1/2 cup cornstarch
- 1/2 cup icing sugar
- 1 tsp vanilla or almond flavouring

Roll out or cookie press.  Decorate with small pieces of red and green cherries.  Bake in 325 degree Fahrenheit oven for around 15-20 mins.
