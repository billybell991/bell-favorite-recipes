- 1 1/4 cups all purpose flour
- 1/2 cup cornstarch
- 1/2 cup icing sugar
- 1 cup butter, softened
- pinch of salt

Preheat oven to 275 degrees Fahrenheit.

In a medium bowl sift together cornstarch, icing sugar, and flour.  Add butter; stir until smooth dough is formed.

Line a cookie sheet with parchment paper.  Roll dough into 1-inch balls.  Place two inches apart on cookie sheet.  Flatten with your fingers or fork.  Decorate with seasonal sprinkles.

Bake for 20-22 minutes, until edges are lightly brown.

TIP: I like to put 1/3 cup of mini semi-sweet chocolate chips in half of the dough.  To mix it up a little.
