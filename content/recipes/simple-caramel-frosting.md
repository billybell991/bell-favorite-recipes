## Ingredients

- ¼ cup lightly salted butter
- 1-1/2 cups dark brown sugar
- 1/3 cup milk
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 3 cups icing sugar

## Instructions

1.      Melt butter in small pan and stir in brown sugar.

2.      Stir in milk and bring mixture just to a boil.

3.      Cool to lukewarm. Stir in vanilla and icing sugar and beat until creamy.
