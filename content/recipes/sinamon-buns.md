Cousin of the Bride

## Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 2/3 cup butter or margarine
- 2 tbsp sugar
- 2 cups brown sugar
- 4 tsp baking powder
- 6-8 tsp cinnamon
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 cup raisins
- 1/4  cup cold butter
- 1 cup cold milk

## Instructions

Put flour, sugar, baking powder and salt in a large bowl. Cut in 1/4 cup of butter or margarine. Make a well in the centre. Pour milk into well. Stir to form soft dough (add a bit more milk if necessary).

Turn out on floured surface and knead 8-10 times. Roll into rectangle about 1/3 inch thick and 12 inches long.

Cream 2/3 cup of butter, brown sugar and cinnamon together. Drop 1 teaspoon or so into each of 12 greased muffin tins. Spread the remaining mixture over dough rectangle and sprinkle raisins over top.

Roll up like a jelly roll. Mark first, and then cut into 12 slices. Place cut side down into muffin pan.

Bake in 375-400 degree oven for 20-25 minutes. Makes 12. Recipe can be doubled and frozen.

Glaze:

To ½ cup of icing sugar, add enough milk or water to make a thin glaze and drizzle over rolls.
