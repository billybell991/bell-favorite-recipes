Cousins of the Bride

- 1 box of Ritz crackers, finely crushed
- 4 Skor chocolate bars, finely crushed
- 1 can of Eaglebrand
- 1 tub of chocolate icing

Put ½ cup of Skor pieces aside for topping.

Combine Ritz crackers and Eaglebrand, mix well. Mix in remaining Skor pieces.

Pour into a greased 8x8 inch pan. Bake at 300 degrees for 20 minutes. Let cool for 20 minutes. Frost with chocolate icing and sprinkle remaining Skor pieces on top.
