## Ingredients

- 1 tin Honeydew Melon or Strawberry Daiquiri
- 1 tin frozen orange juice
- 2 cups cold tea
- 12 oz Lemon Gin

## Instructions

Mix.  Freeze.  Scoop out and mix with Gingerale, 7-up or Sprite.
