## Ingredients

- 1 1/3 cups warm water
- 1 tbsp active dry yeast
- 2 3/4 to 3 cups all purpose flour
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1 tsp salt, for pretzel dough
- 1 egg + 1 tbsp, for egg wash
- Coarse salt, to taste, for finishing pretzels
- Mustard, for serving (optional)

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
2. Combine warm water with yeast in a small bowl.  Let stand 5 minutes.
3. Combine 2 3/4 cups flour, sugar, and salt in a large bowl.
4. Add yeast mixture to flour mixture.  Stir or mix with your hands to bring dough together, adding 1/4 more flour, as needed.
5. Sprinkle work surface with flour and knead dough until smooth and elastic.
6. Divide dough into 12 equal pieces.  Roll into 15-inch long ropes.  Shape into a pretzel knot.  (You could also just make pretzel sticks, if you like.).
7. Brush each pretzel with egg wash and sprinkle with coarse salt.
8. Bake until golden brown, about 15 to 20 minutes.  Serve with mustard, if desired.
