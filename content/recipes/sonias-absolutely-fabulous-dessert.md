## Ingredients

- (1 cup chopped walnuts or pecans
- 1 Tbsp sugar   )
- ( 1 cup flour
- ½ cup butter    )  1st Part
- 1 large tub Cool Whip
- 1 cup icing sugar
- 8 oz (250 gr) cream cheese
- 1 small pkg instant chocolate pudding
- 1 small pkg instant vanilla pudding
- 4 cups milk (Separated)
- 1 Jersey milk chocolate bar

## Instructions

Cream together first four ingredients: press in a 9” x 13” pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 15 to 20 minutes. Cool well.

Cream icing sugar, cream cheese, and then add ½ of the Cool Whip. Spread over the bottom crust. Mix puddings in separate bowls with 2 cups of milk each.  Place chocolate and then vanilla puddings in layers. Add second half of Cool Whip.

Then garnish with shredded chocolate bar on top.
