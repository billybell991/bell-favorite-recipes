## Ingredients

- 1 cup dairy sour cream
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 3 tbsp all-purpose flour
- ¼ tsp salt
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1 egg
- 4 cups peeled, cored & diced tart apples
- 1 9”unbaked pastry shell
- 1/3 cup packed brown sugar
- ½ cup all-purpose flour
- ¼ cup butter

## Instructions

Beat together cream, sugar, flour, salt, vanilla and egg.  Add apples.  Pour into unbaked pastry shell.  Bake in 375 degree oven for 25 minutes.

Mix brown sugar, ½ cup flour and butter  -  sprinkle on top.  Bake 10 to 15 minutes more.
