Cousins of the Bride

A rich sour cream coffee cake which may be layered or simply topped with tart cranberries and spicy sweet streusel. Wonderful served warm with ice cream or whipped cream.

Streusel:

- ¾ cup lightly packed brown sugar
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- ½ cup all purpose flour
- ¼ cup butter

Mix together sugar, flour, and cinnamon. Blend in butter until crumbly.

Cake:

## Ingredients

- ½  cup soft butter
- 2 eggs
- 2 cups all purpose flour
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 cup dairy sour cream
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1 tsp baking powder
- ½ tsp salt
- 2 cups cranberries, fresh or frozen

## Instructions

Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in eggs one at a time, then vanilla. Sift together flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt. Add to creamed mixture. Add sour cream.

Spread ½  batter in 10 inch spring form pan (or 9x13 inch pan). Sprinkle ½ streusel, then ½ cranberries. Spread remaining batter, cranberries and streusel. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
