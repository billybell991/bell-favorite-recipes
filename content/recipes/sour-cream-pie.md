## Ingredients

- 1 cup thick sour cream
- ½ cup sugar
- 1 cup raisin
- 1-1/2 tbsp flour
- 2 eggs, separated
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- Few grains nutmeg
- Few grains salt

## Instructions

Mix together raisins, sugar, flour, salt and spices.  Add to sour cream.  Mix and add well beaten egg yolks.  Whip egg whites until stiff; fold into mixture.  Pour mixture into an unbaked pie shell.  Bake in a moderate oven – 350 degrees – until filling is firm enough not to stick to a knife inserted into center of pie- about 50 minutes.
