## Ingredients

- 1 cup rice
- 4 tbsp butter
- 3 – 4 onions, chopped
- 1 28-oz can tomatoes
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 red or green pepper, chopped
- 1 tsp paprika
- 2 tsp salt

## Instructions

Saute onions, garlic and pepper in butter. Add remaining ingredients and cook slowly on top of stove for 1 hour.

YIELD: Four servings.
