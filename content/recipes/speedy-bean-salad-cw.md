## Ingredients

- ¾ cup sugar
- 2/3 cup vinegar
- 1/3 cup vegetable oil
- 1 tsp salt
- Dash pepper
- 1 15-1/2 oz kidney beans, rinsed &
- 1 15-oz garbanzo beans, rinsed &
- drained
- drained
- ½ medium onion, chopped
- 1 15-oz great northern beans, rinsed
- ½ cup chopped green pepper
- & drained

## Instructions

In a bowl, combine sugar, vinegar, oil, salt and pepper.  Stir in all remaining ingredients.  Let marinate until serving time.

Yield:  6 servings
