## Ingredients

- ½ (12 oz pkg) semi-sweet chocolate chips
- ½ tsp salt
- 1 – 15 oz tin sweetened condensed milk
- ½ cup chopped nuts
- 1 tsp vanilla

## Instructions

Melt chocolate over low heat stirring.  Remove from heat and stir in sweetened condensed milk, salt, vanilla and nuts.  Pour into a waxed paper lined 8”square pan.  Spread evenly smoothing surface.  Refrigerate until firm, about 2 hours.  Turn candy out on board and peel off paper.  With a sharp knife, cut fudge into desired size pieces.
