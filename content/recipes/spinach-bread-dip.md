- 1 10-oz pkg spinach
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 1 cup sour cream
- 1 pkg dry Knorr vegetable soup mix
- 2 green onions
- 1 small can water chestnuts

Cook spinach just lightly; drain and chop.  Chop onions and water chestnuts.  Mix all ingredients together and serve with small pieces of bread.

NOTE:  You can use a large round load of pumpernickel bread.  Cut bread from the center and use this to serve your dip.  The bread taken from the center is used as small pieces to dip.
