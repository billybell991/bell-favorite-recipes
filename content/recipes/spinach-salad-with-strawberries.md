Cousins of the Bride

- Spinach, washed and torn
- 1 pint of fresh strawberries, washed and sliced
- ½ cup slivered almonds

Dressing:

## Ingredients

- ¼ to ½ cup of sugar
- 2 tbsp sesame seeds
- 1 tbsp poppy seeds
- ¼ tsp Worcestershire sauce
- 1 ½ tsp chopped onion
- ¼ tsp paprika
- ½ cup vegetable oil
- ¼ cup cider vinegar

## Instructions

Make the dressing the day before. Pour over salad, toss and serve.
