## Instructions

To prepare dressing, whisk together
1/3 cup mayonnaise
2 Tbsp milk
2 Tbsp sugar
1 Tbsp white wine vinegar
1 Tbsp poppy seed
1/8 tsp salt

Combine in a bowl
8 cups baby spinach or torn romaine lettuce
2 cups sliced strawberries
¼ cup slivered almonds, toasted

Toss with dressing to coat. Serve immediately. Serves 8.
