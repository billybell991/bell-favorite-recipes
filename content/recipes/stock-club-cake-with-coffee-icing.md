Cousin of the Bride

Cake:

- 1 1/2  cup flour
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 1/2  tsp soda
- 1/4  cup cocoa
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 7/8 cup water

Bake for 1 hour or less at 350 degrees.

Coffee Icing:

## Ingredients

- 1/2  tsp coffee
- 1 tbsp margarine
- 2 tsp boiling water
- 1 tsp cocoa
- 1 cup icing sugar

## Instructions

Mix coffee, margarine, water and cocoa until smooth.  Slowly add icing sugar until creamy.
