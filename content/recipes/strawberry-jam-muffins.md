Yield: 24 muffins                                                  Baking Time: 20-25 minutes

Preparation Time: 15 minutes                           Oven Temperature: 350 degrees

## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- ¾ cup margarine
- 3 eggs
- 1 tsp soda
- 2 cups sifted flour
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1 tsp nutmeg
- 1/3 cup sour milk
- ¾ cup jam

## Instructions

Cream sugar and margarine. Add eggs one at a time, beating after each addition.

Sift dry ingredients together. Add dry ingredients alternately with sour milk. Fold in ¾ cup jam. Fill muffin tins ¾ full (grease if not using liners).

Bake 20-25 minutes. At 350 degrees.

NOTE: Any flavour jam, or jellied or whole cooked cranberries can be used.
