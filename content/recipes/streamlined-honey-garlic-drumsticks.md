## Ingredients

- 2/3 cup honey
- 3 Tbsp soy sauce
- 2 cloves garlic
- 1 tsp ground ginger or 1” fresh ginger root
- 1 tsp (any kind) vinegar
- ¼ cup water
- 2 lb drumsticks (about 12)

## Instructions

Put the honey, soy sauce, garlic, ginger, vinegar and water into the container or a blender and whirl it around till smooth. Arrange drumsticks in 9” x 13” pan and pour sauce on top.

Bake at 350 F. for about an hour, basting from time to time with the sauce and turning the legs over about halfway through.

Serve with rice or something to soak up the delicious sauce.

Makes about 4 servings.
