## Ingredients

- 2 tbsp butter
- ½ pkg mushrooms, diced
- 1 red pepper
- 1 envelope of Lipton onion soup mix
- 1 cup bread crumbs
- ½ tsp pepper
- 4 thick pork chops

## Instructions

Combine vegetables and butter in sauce pan and cook until mushrooms are done. Add the soup mix, bread crumbs and pepper. Allow the mixture to cool.

Cut the pork chops on the side and make a pocket on the inside of the pork chop. Do not remove excess fat from the pork chops. Stuff the pockets with the above mixture.

Arrange in low sided cookie sheet and cook for 45-60 minutes at 350 degrees. Serve with noodles or rice.
