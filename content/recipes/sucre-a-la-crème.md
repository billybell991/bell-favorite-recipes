## Ingredients

- 1-1/2 cups brown sugar
- 1-1/2 cups white sugar
- 1 cup Carnation Evaporated Milk – undiluted

## Instructions

Bring the Carnation milk and the sugars to a boil.  Boil to 240 F., stirring continually till a soft ball stage is reached.  (Dropping a few drops in cold water.) Let mixture cool slightly, then stir till mixture thickens – this happens quickly. Pour mixture in a buttered 8” square pan.

Cut into squares before it is completely cooled. ½ to ¾ cup of chopped nuts may be added before cooling.
