## Ingredients

- 1 cup evaporated milk or cream
- 2 eggs
- 4 Tbsp flour
- 1 tbsp butter
- 2 cups brown sugar
- 1 tsp vanilla

## Instructions

Beat eggs with milk.  Add remaining ingredients and blend well.  Pour into an unbaked 9-inch pie shell.  Bake at 350 degrees for approximately 40 minutes
