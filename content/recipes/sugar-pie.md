## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cup brown sugar
- 1 ½ tbsp flour

## Instructions

Place the above ingredients in a saucepan. Add 1 cup of canned milk. Cook over low heat, stirring until dissolved. Remove from heat. Slowly add 1 beaten egg, stirring constantly. Pour into an 8 inch, unbaked pie shell. Bake at 400 degrees for 10 minutes. Reduce heat to 300 degrees and continue baking for 30 minutes, or until thick.
