## Ingredients

- 1 lb each cashews, whole almonds and pecan halves
- ½ cup unsalted butter or margarine, melted
- 2 egg whites, lightly beaten
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 tbsp ground cinnamon

## Instructions

Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  In large bowl, combine nuts.  In medium bowl, whisk together remaining ingredients, pour over nuts.  Mix, spread on baking sheet.  Bake 25 minutes, stirring occasionally, until crisp.  Cool before packing.

Makes 3 pounds.
