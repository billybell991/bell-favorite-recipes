Niece of the Bride

2 pkgs Pillsbury Dough Croissant Rolls

Flatten on a cookie sheet and bake at 350 degrees for 10-12 minutes, or until golden brown.  Let cool.

Topping:

- ½ cup Miracle Whip
- ½ cup Hellman's Mayonnaise
- 1 pkg Hidden Valley Ranch Dressing (powder)
- 8 oz cream cheese

Mix together and spread on cooled Croissant crust.

Sprinkle with chopped veggies:

## Ingredients

- 1 cup broccoli
- 1 cup green pepper
- ½ cup green onions
- ½ cup olives
- 1 cup tomatoes

## Instructions

Cover with saran wrap and pat lightly. Best if made the day before.
