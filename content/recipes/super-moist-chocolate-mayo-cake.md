## Ingredients

- 1 box chocolate cake mix
- 1 cup Hellmann’s Mayonnaise
- 1 cup water
- 3 eggs
- 1 tsp ground cinnamon (optional)

## Instructions

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.

Blend all ingredients.

Bake as directed on the box.
