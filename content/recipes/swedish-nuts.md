## Ingredients

- ½ lbs (1-1/2 cups) blanches almonds
- ½ lb (2 cups) walnut halves
- 1 cup sugar
- dash salt
- 2 stiffly beaten egg whites
- ½ cup butter

## Instructions

Toast nuts (325 degrees) until light brown.  Fold sugar and dash of salt in egg whites.  Beat until stiff, fold in nuts.

Melt butter on cookie sheet.  Spread nut mixture in pan, baking at 300 degrees for about 30 minutes, stirring every 10 minutes until no butter remains.  Cool.  Makes about 4 cups.
