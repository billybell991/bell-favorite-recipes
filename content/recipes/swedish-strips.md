## Ingredients

- 1 cup shortening
- l cup brown sugar
- l egg
- l tsp vanilla
- l/4 tsp salt
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- l/2 cup jam
- 2 tbsp icing sugar
- l/2 cup nuts, finely chopped

## Instructions

Cream shortening and sugar until light and fluffy.  Add egg, vanilla and salt and mix well.  Add flour and combine. Place cookie batter on slightly greased cookie sheet.  Spread and flatten until l/4”thick.

Baking time:  l5 minutes at 350 degrees.

While giant cookie is still hot, spread thinly with jam and dust with icing sugar.  Sprinkle with nuts.  Cut immediately into small bars.
