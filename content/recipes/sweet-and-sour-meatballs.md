Groom

- 2 lbs hamburg
- 1 cup milk
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 cup bread crumbs
- 2 eggs

Knead all ingredients together. Form meatballs and place on a cookie sheet. Cook at 350 degrees until brown.

Sauce:

## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cup brown sugar
- 1 tsp dry mustard
- ¾ cup ketchup
- ¾ cup water
- ¾ cup vinegar

## Instructions

Mix ingredients together in a sauce pan. Bring to a boil. Mix

1 ½ teaspoon of corn starch with water and add to boiling mixture.

Place meatballs in large casserole dish or roast pan and pour sauce over top. Cook at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
