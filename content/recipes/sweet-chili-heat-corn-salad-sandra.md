## Ingredients

- 1/2 an orange pepper
- 1/2 a red pepper
- 1/3 a yellow pepper
- 1 can of corn
- 1/4 cup of shredded cheese (optional)
- 2 cups crushed Sweet Chili Heat Doritos
- 2 Tbsp of mayonnaise

## Instructions

Mix everything but the Doritos ahead of time.  When you're ready to serve it, mix in the Doritos.
