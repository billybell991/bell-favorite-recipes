## Ingredients

- Melt over heat:
- ½ cup corn syrup
- ½ cup peanut butter
- ½ cup brown sugar
- Add:
- 2 cups rice krispies
- 1 tbsp melted butter
- ½ cup peanuts

## Instructions

Spread in 8”x 8”pan (slightly buttered).  Ice with chocolate icing or melt semi-sweet chocolate chips and pour over bars.
