## Ingredients

- 1 small onion, minced
- ¼ cup crushed crackers (or bread crumbs)
- 1-8 oz tin tomato sauce
- ¼ cup brown sugar, packed
- ¼ cup vinegar
- 1 tsp prepared mustard
- 1 egg
- 2 lbs ground chuck
- 1 ½ tsp salt
- ¼ tsp pepper

## Instructions

Mix tomato sauce with sugar, vinegar and mustard until sugar is dissolved. Put ½ cup of sauce mixture aside.

With fork, slightly beat egg. Add onion, crackers, chuck, salt, pepper and remaining tomato sauce mixture. Combine lightly, but thoroughly.

Place in loaf pan and cover with the ½ cup of sauce mixture that was put aside.

Bake at 400 degrees for 45 minutes.
