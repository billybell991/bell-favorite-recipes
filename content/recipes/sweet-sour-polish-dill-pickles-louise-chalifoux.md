## Ingredients

- Brine: 4 cups vinegar
- Onions
- 3 cups water
- Garlic
- 3 cups sugar
- Dill
- ½ cup pickling salt
- Green Cucumbers

## Instructions

Slice onions (about 2 medium ones to 1 quart) and put 1 piece of garlic and dill in bottom of quart sealers. Then slice green cucumbers (about ¼” thick) into sealers. Bring brine (vinegar, water, sugar, salt) to a boil. Pour over cucumbers in jar and seal.

Enough for 5 quarts.
