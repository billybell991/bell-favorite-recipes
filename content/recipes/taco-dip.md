## Ingredients

- 1 pkg of cream cheese
- 1 envelope of taco seasoning
- 1 jar of salsa
- 1 medium tomato, diced
- 1 can black olives, optional
- 1 small pkg of shredded cheddar cheese
- 1 small container of sour cream

## Instructions

Blend cream cheese and taco seasoning. Spread on baking dish. Layer on sour cream and salsa. Top with diced tomato and black olives. Sprinkle cheddar cheese on top.

Serve with nacho chips.
