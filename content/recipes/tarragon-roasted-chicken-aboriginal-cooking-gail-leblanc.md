## Ingredients

- 2 cloves of garlic
- 1 3-lb chicken
- 2 tsp dried tarragon
- ¼ cup white wine or apple juice
- 1 Tbs vegetable oil

## Instructions

Cut 1 garlic clove in half. Rub cut sides on outside of chicken. Place garlic in chicken cavity. Sprinkle half of the tarragon in chicken cavity. Tie chicken with string and place in roasting pan.

Mince remaining garlic and combine with remaining tarragon, wine (or apple juice) and oil. Drizzle over chicken and roast in 350 F/180 C oven, basting frequently, for 1-1/4 hours or until juices run clear when chicken is pierced with a fork.

Makes 6 servings.
