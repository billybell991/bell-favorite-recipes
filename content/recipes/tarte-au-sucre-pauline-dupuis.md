## Ingredients

- 3 cups brown sugar
- 2 eggs, beaten
- 2 tbsp margarine
- 3 tbsp flour
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1-1/2 cups milk

## Instructions

Mix together all ingredients and pour into a large uncooked pie shell or two small ones.  Bake at 375 degrees for approx. 50 minutes.
