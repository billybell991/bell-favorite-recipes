Cousins of the Bride

- 1/2 cup margarine
- 2 cups flour
- 2 tsp baking soda
- 1/2  tsp salt
- 1 1/2 cups white sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1-19 oz can of crushed pineapple

Combine all ingredients and place in a 9x13 inch glass pan. Cook at 325 degrees for 45-55 minutes. Insert a toothpick in the centre of the cake to check if it’s ready.

Sauce:

## Ingredients

- 1/2  cup margarine
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 2/3 cup Carnation milk
- 1 cup coconut
- 1 tsp vanilla

## Instructions

Combine margarine, brown sugar and milk in a saucepan and bring to a boil. Continue boiling sauce for 5 minutes and then add coconut and vanilla.

Let cake cool a little bit and then pour sauce over top.
