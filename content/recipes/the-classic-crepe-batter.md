## Ingredients

- 2 large eggs
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 1 ¼ cups whole milk (or 1 cup milk + ¼ cup water for crispier edges)
- 2 tbsp butter, melted (plus extra for the pan)
- 1 tbsp sugar
- Pinch of salt
- Optional: ½ tsp vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Blend: Combine all ingredients in a blender and pulse for 20 seconds. (If mixing by hand, whisk eggs and milk first, then slowly sift in flour to avoid lumps).
2. Rest (Crucial): Let the batter sit in the fridge for at least 30 minutes. This allows the flour to fully hydrate and the air bubbles to escape, ensuring your crepes don't tear.
3. Heat the Pan: Set your crepe pan over medium heat. Lightly brush with a tiny bit of butter.
4. The Pour: Pour about ¼ cup of batter into the center. Immediately tilt and swirl the pan in a circular motion to coat the bottom thinly.
5. The Flip: Cook for about 60 seconds until the edges look dry and lacy. Use a spatula to lift an edge, then flip with your fingers or a turner. Cook the other side for 30 seconds.
