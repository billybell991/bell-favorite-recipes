## Ingredients

- 1 Loaf French or ciabatta type bread - sliced in half, horizontally
- 1/2 Cup butter - softened
- 1/2 Cup mayonnaise
- 1/4 Cup cheddar cheese - shredded
- 1/4 Cup mozzarella cheese - shredded
- 1 1/2 Tbsp garlic - minced
- 1/4 Tsp oregano to finish

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees.
2. Mix all of the ingredients together, except for the oregano.
3. Slice a loaf of French bread horizontally and spread 1/2 the mix on the bread.
4. Bake for 10 mins, or until the cheese is toasted and browned.
5. Spread the other 1/2 of the mix on the bread and let it melt in there (don't put it back into the oven!)
6. Sprinkle with oregano to finish.
