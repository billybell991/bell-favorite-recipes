## Ingredients

- ½ cup butter
- 1 cup sifted flour
- ¼ cup brown sugar
- Nuts, chopped finely or corn flakes crushed
- 1 egg, separated
- Jelly
- 1 tsp flavouring

## Instructions

Cream butter. Add sugar gradually and mix well. Add well Beaten egg yolk, flavouring and flour. Shape into balls about the size of a walnut. Dip in unbeaten egg white, then in finely chopped nuts, almonds, walnuts or soya bean flakes or corn flakes. Dent in center with a thimble and bake in a moderate oven at 350 degrees for 5 minutes and dent again. Bake for 12 to 15 minutes more. Fill with jelly or jam while still hot if planning not to freeze them.

Otherwise, fill them only when you’re ready to serve them.
