## Ingredients

- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- ½ tsp baking powder
- ¼ tsp salt
- 1 cup Skor chips
- ¾ cup chocolate chips
- 1/3 cup sliced natural almonds
- 1/3 cup unsalted butter, melted
- ½ cup firmly packed light brown sugar
- 1 tsp almond extract
- ½ tsp vanilla extract
- 1 large egg

## Instructions

Preheat over to 350 F. and coat an 8” pan with non-stick spray, or line with non-stick aluminum foil. Whisk flour, baking powder and salt together in a small bowl. Toss together toffee, chocolate chips and almonds in a small bowl. Put one cup of mixture into another small bowl and ½ cup into a third bowl.
Whisk together melted butter, brown sugar and extracts in a medium bowl (or pot you melted butter in) until blended. Whisk in egg until well combined. Stir in flour mixture until a few streaks of it remain, then stir in first bowlful of toffee mixture until evenly combined. Spread in an even layer in baking pan and sprinkle top evenly with reserved 1 cup of toffee mixture.
Bake until light golden brown around edges but soft in center, 30 minutes;  a toothpick inserted in center should come out with wet batter attached.
Sprinkle remaining ½ cup of toffee mixture evenly over top. Bake until toothpick inserted in center comes out with a few moist crumbs attached, about 5 more minutes.
Place pan on rack to cool completely. Cut into 16 bars.
NOTE: Grease pan really well as this recipe tends to really stick, especially around the edges.
