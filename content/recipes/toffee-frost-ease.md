## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 2 cups flour
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1 cup walnuts
- 1 cup chocolate chips

## Instructions

Cream butter, sugar and vanilla. Stir in flour & nuts and mix well.

Pat into an ungreased 9 x 13 pan. Bake at 350 degrees F. for 15 – 20 minutes, until lightly browned.

Remove from oven and sprinkle chocolate chips evenly over top. After about 5 minutes they’ll be soft – then spread evenly.

Cut in squares while still warm.
