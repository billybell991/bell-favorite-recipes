## Ingredients

- 6 slices buttered bread
- 1 28-oz tin tomatoes
- ½ tsp salt
- 1 to 3 tbsp brown sugar
- ½ tsp dried basil
- 2 tbsp butter
- 2 tbsp or more chopped onion (or green onions)

## Instructions

Cut the buttered slices of bread into 1”squares and dry them out in the oven at 250 degrees for about ¾ hr.  Put half in bottom of large casserole (about 2-quart size).
Melt butter in a saucepan and lightly sauté onions.  Pour over bread cubes.
Mix tomatoes, sugar, salt and basil and pour over bread.
Top with rest of bread cubes, pushing them down a little.
Bake uncovered at 350 degrees for 45 – 60 minutes.
