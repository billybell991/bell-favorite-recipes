## Ingredients

- 1 lb hamburg
- 1 medium onion, chopped
- 5 or 6 medium potatoes, sliced
- 1 can Heinz tomato soup

## Instructions

Brown hamburg and onions. Place potatoes in a greased casserole dish. Add hamburg and onions. Mix in tomato soup.

Cook at 350 degrees for 1 hour, or until potatoes are tender.
