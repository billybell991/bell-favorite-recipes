## Instructions

The tuxedo of dessert bars, this dressed up sweet will become a holiday tradition at your house.

½ cup butter, melted	1-1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
1 200-gr pkg flaked sweetened coconut	1 tin (300 ml) sweetened condensed milk
1 350-gr pkg semisweet chocolate chips	½ cup smooth peanut butter

Combine butter with cracker crumbs in bowl; press evenly over bottom of 13 x 9 cake pan or glass baking dish lined with parchment paper. Top evenly with coconut, then condensed milk.

Bake in 350 F oven (or 325 F if using glass baking dish) for 25 minutes or until top is lightly browned. In small saucepan over low heat, stir together chocolate chips with peanut butter until melted. Spread evenly over hot coconut layer. Let cool in pan on rack for 30 minutes; refrigerate until chilled.

Cut into bars and remove parchment paper. (To make ahead, store bars in air-tight container at room temperature.)
Makes 36 bars.
