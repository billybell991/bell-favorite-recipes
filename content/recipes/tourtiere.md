## Instructions

If you don’t have your own favorite meat pie, here is a recipe you might like to consider

Pastry for a 2-crust 9”pie.

Filling:
1 Tbsp shortening	2 – 3 Tbsp boiling water
1 small onion, chopped fine	Salt and pepper
½ lb lean veal, minced	Celery leaves or parsley snipped
½ lb minced pork	Enough crumbs to thicken the meat

Method:
Saute the onion in melted shortening until limp.  Add pork, veal and water.  Simmer 35 minutes or just until meat becomes very tender.  Add salt, pepper, celery or parsley and crumbs.  Bake at 375 degrees for about 45 – 50 minutes or until crust is nicely browned.
