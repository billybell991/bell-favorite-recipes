## Ingredients

- 2 Tbsp butter
- 1 small onion, diced
- 2-3 clove garlic, minced
- 3-4 carrots, diced
- 2 stalks celery, chopped
- 2 Cups cooked turkey, shredded or diced
- 7 Cups chicken broth
- 6 oz noodles of your choice
- 1 tsp dried basil
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

### Instructions

1. In your Instant Pot, combine the onion, garlic, carrot and celery along with the butter.
2. Push saute and let the veggies cook for 4-5 minutes.
3. Turn off the saute function, and add the chicken, broth and noodles - place the lid on the Instant Pot and lock, ensure the valve is closed.
4. Push manual (high) and set the timer for 4 minutes.
5. The pot will take 8-10 minutes to come to pressure, then will count down the 4 minutes.
6. Once it beeps, slowly release the pressure and open the lid.
7. Add salt/pepper and basil to taste.
8. Serve and enjoy!
