## Ingredients

- 1 beaten egg
- 1/2 cup Stove Top stuffing mix
- 1 Tbsp dried onion
- 1 Tsp chicken bouillion powder
- 1/4 cup water
- a squirt ofWorcestershire sauce (optional)
- 1 lb ground turkey
- 1 cup Stove Top stuff mix (crushed fine)

## Instructions

1. In bowl combine egg 1/4 cup water; stir in 1/2 cup stuffing mix, dried onion, a shot of Worcestershire sauce, and bouillon. Let stand for five minutes.
2. Add turkey and mix well; shape into 1/2 inch patties.  (This made 5 patties for me)
3. Coat with crushed stuffing mix.  (Make sure you crush it well enough or it won't stick!)
4. Cook patties in skillet coated with oil about 10 minutes per side until thoroughly cooked.
