## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 1-2/3 cups granulated sugar
- 1 cup cooking oil
- 16 oz. Can pumpkin
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 2 tsp baking powder, ground cinnamon
- 1 tsp salt, baking soda

## Instructions

In mixing bowl, beat together eggs, sugar, oil and pumpkin till light and fluffy. Stir together baking powder, cinnamon, salt and soda. Add to pumpkin mixture and mix thoroughly. Spread batter in 2 lightly greased  8” x8” pans.

Bake in 350 F oven for 25 minutes. Cool.  Frost bars with your favourite cream cheese frosting.
