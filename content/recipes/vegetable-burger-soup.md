## Ingredients

- 1 lb ground beef
- ½ cup dry onion soup mix
- 1 can stewed tomatoes
- 1 tsp white sugar
- 8-oz can tomato sauce
- 1 cup diced potatoes
- 2 cups water
- 1 tbsp celery salt
- 10-oz pkg frozen mixed vegetables

## Instructions

In Dutch oven or large pan, brown ground beef.  Drain off fat. Stir in tomatoes, tomato sauce, water, onion soup mix, vegetables, sugar and celery salt.  Bring to boil; reduce heat, cover and simmer ½ hour.  Add diced potatoes; simmer 20 minutes longer.
