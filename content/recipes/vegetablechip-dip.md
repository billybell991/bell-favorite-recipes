Submitted by Brenda and Denis Arsenault

Cousins of the Bride

- 1 cup sour cream
- 1 cup Hellman’s mayonnaise
- 1 tsp beau monde spice
- 1 tsp minced onion
- 1 tsp accent
- 1 tsp dill weed
- 1 tsp parsley
- ½ tsp garlic salt
- 2 to 3 drops of Tabasco

Mix all ingredients together and serve with vegetables or chips.
