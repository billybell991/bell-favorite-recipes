## Ingredients

- 4 cups flour
- 1 tsp curry powder
- 3 tbsp salt
- ½ tsp poultry  seasoning
- 1 tbsp pepper
- 1/16 tsp cloves
- 1 tbsp garlic powder
- 1/16 tsp turmeric
- 1 tbsp onion salt
- ½ tsp dry mustard
- 1 tsp mixed spice

## Instructions

Mix well and store in a jar.  For potatoes, beat 2 eggs with 1 tbsp of milk.  Dip potatoes in egg mixture, roll in the prepared flour, then fry in oil until golden.

P.S. – I use this dry mixture to coat liver that I fry in bacon fat.
