## Ingredients

- 6 cups finely shredded cabbage
- 1 cup finely shredded carrot
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- ½ cup milk
- ½ cup minced onion
- ¼ cup sugar
- ½ tsp salt
- 2 drops hot pepper sauce (optional)
- 1/8 tsp pepper
- ½ tsp celery salt

## Instructions

Toss cabbage and carrot.  Mix all other ingredients in a separate bowl.  Toss with large fork until well coated.  Refrigerate for at least 1-1/2 hr. before serving.

Makes one quart of coleslaw.
