## Ingredients

- ½ lb butter
- ½ lb margarine
- ½ cup cornstarch
- 1 cup sifted icing sugar
- 3 cups sifted flour

## Instructions

Cream butter and margarine gradually with cornstarch and sugar. Add flour, a little bit at a time, and whip mixture until it looks like cream. The more they are beat – the better they are.

Drop by spoonfuls on cookie sheet and bake in a 325 degree oven for 22 minutes.
