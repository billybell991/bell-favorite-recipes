## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- 5 eggs
- 1-1/4 cup sugar
- 2 cups flour
- 1 cup red glazed cherries
- 1 cup green glazed cherries
- 1 cup chopped walnuts

## Instructions

Cream butter and sugar.  Add eggs, one at a time.  Add flour.  Mix well.  Add cherries and nuts.  Mix well.  Bake in greased angelfood tin or a pan lined with well-greased brown paper.

Bake for 1-1/2 – 2 hours at 300 degrees.
