## Ingredients

- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 1 cup whole wheat flour
- 1 Tbsp granulated sugar
- 1 Tbsp baking powder
- ½ tsp baking soda
- ½ tsp salt
- ¼ cup soft margarine
- 1 cup blueberries
- 1 cup buttermilk or sour milk
- In bowl, combine all-purpose and whole wheat flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda and salt. Using fingers, rub in margarine until mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
- Stir in blueberries; add buttermilk and mix lightly. Drop by spoonfuls into 10 mounds. Bake in 425 degree (220 C) oven for 12 to 15 minutes or until golden.
- Makes 10 biscuits (5 g fat per biscuit)
