## Instructions

INGREDIENTS
2 handfuls of wild blueberries
2 single serving containers of about 6 oz  of yogurt (vanilla or plain)
1 Tbsp of honey if plain yogurt is used
3 – 4 ice cubes

DIRECTIONS
Blend well at high speed. Top with loose Wild Blueberries. Serve immediately.
Serves 2.
