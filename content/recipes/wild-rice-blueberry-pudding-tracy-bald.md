## Ingredients

- 2 cups cooked wild rice
- 2 cups cream or 2% evaporated milk
- 6 eggs
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 cup blueberries
- ¾ cup maple syrup

## Instructions

Whisk together the eggs, milk, vanilla and maple syrup. Over medium heat, stir together the wild rice and blueberries in a large sauce pan. Gradually add in milk mixture and stir well. Simmer over medium low heat for 30 minutes, stirring frequently until pudding thickens. Pour into bowls and cool slightly before serving.  Serves 8

Tracy Bald is the Community Wellness Coordinator for the  Georgian Bay Metis Council and she had this to say about this recipe. “This year I have been able to freeze a great deal of wild blueberries. I love to have them on hand for pancakes, oatmeal, and one of my favourites, wild rice pudding.  I made this recipe for an event we held to honour our Elders back in June. The pudding was enjoyed by all and many asked for the recipe. I thought I would share it since I am not the only one that thinks it is delicious. Enjoy!
