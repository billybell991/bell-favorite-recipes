## Ingredients

- 1 cup wild rice
- 3 cups water
- ½ cup chopped onion
- 1 cup grated cheese
- ¼ tsp salt
- 3 cups sliced mushroom
- 3 Tbsp butter or margarine
- 1 – 19 oz tin stewed tomatoes

## Instructions

Wash rice under cold water. Put the rice into 3 cups of water and boil for 5 minutes. Let soak in the same water for 1 hour. Cook rice in boiling water for an additional 20 minutes.

Drain rice if necessary. Saute onions and mushroom for about 5 minutes. Toss all ingredients in a 2-quart casserole and bake at 350 F. degrees for 1 hour.
