## Ingredients

- 2/3 cup wild rice
- 2 chopped onions
- ¼ cup margarine or butter, melted
- ½ cup blanched almonds
- 1 cup sultana raisins
- 2 tsp poultry seasoing
- Salt & pepper to taste

## Instructions

Wash rice; soak overnight; in the morning drain; cook in boiling salted water until tender. Drain.

Saute onions in hot margarine or butter until golden brown. Add almonds, raisins, poultry seasoning, salt and pepper.

Lightly stuff fowl or game. Cover bird with oiled, heavy brown paper or foil paper, with no water, no cover and no basting, roast in 325 F. oven until done.
