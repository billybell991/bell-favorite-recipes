## Ingredients

- 1 cup plain yogurt
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 1 cup brown sugar
- ½ cup oil
- 1 egg
- 1 cup bran
- 2 cups flour
- ½ tsp vanilla
- 1 cup blueberries

## Instructions

In a small bowl, mix yogurt andoneteaspoon of baking powder. Set aside.

In a large bowl, mix all other ingredients, including the second teaspoon of baking powder. Mix the yogurt mixture into the large bowl.

Grease muffin tins. Bake at 350 degrees for 25 to 30 minutes. Makes 12 muffins.
