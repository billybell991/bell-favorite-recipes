Submitted by Kathy and Sean Demarce

Friends of the Bride and Groom

Take one clean industrial size olive or pickle jar (4 litre size would be perfect) with a lid.

- 3 lemons
- 3 limes
- 3 oranges

Cut the fruit into quarters. Squeeze the juice into the jar and place the rinds into the jar as well. Add some different fruit like whole strawberries, quartered kiwi (2-3) is good. We recommend NOT using grapefruit, or bananas because they can get mushy. Add 1 bottle of flavoured Vodka (750ml) (Absolut Mandarin, Blueberry, Raspberry, whatever flavour sounds good). Fruit flavoured vodka is the best, although, Vanilla Vodka can be good.

Add ½ cup of sugar and 1 jar of marashino cherries (juice and all).

Pack jar with ice, close and cover jar with tea towel. It gets pretty frosty. Shake for 20 minutes (NO LESS). Pass around the group for everyone to have a turn shaking. If the ice gets low, just add more.

Once 20 minutes has passed, open and take a sip (or 2 or 3) right from the jar! Pass around to the other guests to enjoy.

There are some rules: You may NOT put Yuck-a-flux in a glass to drink, it must be from the jar and there will be NO use of straws!! Don't tempt the Yuck-a-flux Gods.
