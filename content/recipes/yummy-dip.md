Cousin of the Groom

- 1-8 oz pkg of cream cheese
- 1 cup sour cream
- ½ cup mayonnaise

- 1 cup seafood sauce
- 2 cups shrimp (optional)
- ½ cup diced green onions
- ½ cup diced tomatoes
- ½ cup green peppers
- 2 cups grated mozzarella and/or cheddar cheese

Mix the first 3 ingredients together and spread it on a pizza pan or large plate. Spread seafood sauce on top and add shrimp (optional). Cover with cheese. Sprinkle with green onions, tomatoes and green peppers.
