## Ingredients

- 12 oz uncooked ziti or other medium pasta
- 2 pkg (16 oz) frozen broccoli, carrots, and water chestnuts
- 2 Tbsp olive or canola oil
- 2 Tbsp non-hydrogenated margarine
- 2 tsp dried basil
- 2 tsp dried marjoram
- 1 tsp dried thyme
- 3/4 tsp salt
- 4 tsp corn starch
- 1 cup chicken broth
- 1/2 cup dry white wine or more broth
- 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

Cook pasta according to package directions. Meanwhile, in a saucepan, bring 1 inch of water to a boil; place vegetables in a steamer basket over water. Cover and steam for 5-7 minutes or until tender.

In a Dutch oven or large saucepan, combine the oil, margarine, basil, marjoram, thyme and salt. Cook and stir over medium heat for 1 minute. Combine cornstarch, broth and wine or additional broth until smooth; stir into the pan. Bring to a boil; cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened.

Drain pasta. Add pasta and vegetables to the sauce; toss to combine. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.

Yield: 8 servings.

Nutritional Analysis: One serving (1 cup) equals 181 calories, 9 g fat (3 g saturated fat), 13 mg cholesterol, 500 mg sodium, 18 g carbohydrate, 3 g fiber, 6 g protein. Diabetic Exchanges: 1 starch, 1 lean meat, 1 fat.
