Cousins of the Bride

## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup oil
- 2 cups grated zucchini
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 2 tsp vanilla
- 3 cups flour

## Instructions

Mix all ingredients together. Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes. Makes 2 loaves.
