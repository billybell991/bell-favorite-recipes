## Ingredients

- 2 cups thinly sliced zucchini
- 1 tomato, sliced thin
- 1 medium onion, thinly sliced
- 3 tbsp grated Parmesan cheese
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

In greased 9 x 5 x 3 baking dish, layer half the zucchini, tomato and onions.
Sprinkle with half the cheese; salt and pepper to taste.
Repeat.

Bake uncovered in a 350 degree oven for 40 minutes.  Cover and bake 20 minutes longer.

Makes 4 servings.

P.S.  I always use much more vegetables than this as they really decrease in amount as they cook.  Then I also alter the amount of cheese I use.  This recipe is fool-proof.  If you blow it it’s because you’re trying to.
