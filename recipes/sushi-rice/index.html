Sushi Rice

Fluffy, fragrant satisfying rice with sushi rice and hot water — every grain perfectly cooked and packed with flavor. The side dish that steals the spotlight.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 2.5 cups hot water (the hot water will help the Instant Pot come to pressure faster)
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp kosher salt

NOTE: I rinsed my rice ahead of time. This particular recipe didn’t say to do so, but the rice comes out less “gummy” if you do (from what I understand, anyway!). Just put the rice in a big bowl and cover it with cool water. Swish it around with your hand a bit, then pour the now-murky water out. Do this as many times as it takes until the water drains clear.

Add 2 cups of sushi rice and 2.5 cups of hot water to the Instant Pot cooking pot.

Place the lid and ensure the sealing valve is set to Sealed. Select Pressure Cook, high pressure for 4 minutes.

Allow pressure to release naturally (around 10 mins), then remove lid.

In a small microwaveable bowl, combine rice vinegar, sugar and kosher salt. Microwave for 30 seconds, stir.

Add vinegar mixture to hot, cooked rice and combine. Let it come to room temperature before adding to your favorite sushi dishes.

Leftover rice can be covered and stored in the fridge.
