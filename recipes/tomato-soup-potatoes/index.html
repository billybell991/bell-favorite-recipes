Tomato Soup Potatoes

Steaming bowls of bold soup packed with hamburg, medium onion, and or 6 medium potatoes. The recipe you reach for when the weather turns and comfort is non-negotiable.

Submitted by Hazel Pigeau

Grandmother of the Bride

Ingredients

  • 1 lb hamburg
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 5 or 6 medium potatoes, sliced
  • 1 can Heinz tomato soup

Instructions

Brown hamburg and onions. Place potatoes in a greased casserole dish. Add hamburg and onions. Mix in tomato soup.

Cook at 350 degrees for 1 hour, or until potatoes are tender.
